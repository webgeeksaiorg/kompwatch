# CompeteWatch — Product Story

Use this as voice context when writing marketing content.

## Who built this
Built by Stive — a developer who got tired of manually checking competitor
websites every Monday morning. "I had 6 browser tabs pinned for competitor
pricing pages. Half the time I'd forget to check. The other half I'd miss
a change that happened on Wednesday."

## The honest origin
Started as a cron job that diffed HTML pages and emailed alerts. Then added
AI to summarize the diffs into plain English. Turns out lots of product
teams have the same problem.

## What makes it different
- Not trying to be Crayon ($500/mo enterprise intelligence platform)
- Just does ONE thing well: tracks websites for changes, tells you in English
- $49/mo Pro vs $500/mo competitors
- No 30-slide pitch deck about "competitive intelligence strategy"
- You paste URLs, we tell you when stuff changes

## Voice
- Direct, slightly irreverent
- Technical enough for PMs but not jargon-heavy
- Honest about limitations: "We don't do social listening or patent tracking.
  We watch websites. That's it."
- Building in public: share real metrics, real decisions, real mistakes

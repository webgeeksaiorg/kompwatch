Rule of thumb: if you don't have a full-time analyst to process the output, don't buy a platform that requires one.

Crayon and Klue generate a lot. Someone has to curate it into battlecards, keep them current, train sales. If that's not someone's actual job, the subscription becomes expensive shelfware fast.

The question isn't "which CI tool" — it's "what do I actually need CI to do for me today."

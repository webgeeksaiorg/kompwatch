Crayon/Klue buyers: median time to meaningful ROI is 14 months.

That's a $25K-$40K/year commitment. No free trial. ~4 weeks of onboarding. Then you need 5-10 hrs/week from someone to make it useful.

By month 3 most teams are using it as an expensive RSS feed. By month 8 they're questioning renewal. By month 14 — maybe — they've got a workflow that delivers.

That's not a bad product. It's a wrong-fit product for teams without a dedicated CI analyst.

The moves that hurt you most never come with a press release.

Competitor quietly adds an annual discount. Reframes a tier. Drops their entry price $20.

You find out on a sales call when the prospect says "well, [competitor] just offers it at X now."

72% of B2B SaaS companies made that kind of change in 2025. Silently.

The DIY competitor monitoring stack everyone ends up with:

→ Visualping for visual diffs (noisy)
→ Google Alerts for news (misses website changes)
→ Owler for company updates (useful, separate context)
→ A Google Sheet nobody updates after week 6
→ A #competitive-intel Slack channel that's been quiet since March

Works for a while. Breaks when the person holding it together gets busy.

Then you're back to checking manually, but now you have 3 tool subscriptions you keep meaning to cancel.

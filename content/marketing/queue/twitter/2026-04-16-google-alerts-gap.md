Google Alerts will tell you when a competitor gets mentioned online.

It will not tell you:
- What changed on their pricing page
- That they quietly removed their free tier
- That their feature comparison page now lists a competitor (you) in the "alternatives" column
- That the wording on their enterprise tier shifted from "contact sales" to "self-serve"

Those are different kinds of changes. Website monitoring catches them. Google Alerts doesn't.

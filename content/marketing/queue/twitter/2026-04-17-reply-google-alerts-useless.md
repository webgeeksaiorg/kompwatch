Google Alerts catches new pages that get indexed. Not changes to existing pages.

Competitor updated their pricing? You won't hear about it until Google re-crawls that URL — which could be days or weeks.

For website change detection specifically, it's the wrong tool. It was built for brand monitoring, not competitive intel.

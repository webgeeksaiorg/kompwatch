Something I've been thinking about since the Klue security incident in June:

A competitor intelligence platform is, by definition, a database of your competitive strategy. Everything you track, every battlecard, every analysis of why you beat or lose to a specific competitor.

That data sitting in a third-party SaaS is a real risk vector. Most companies never think about it until something happens.

At minimum, know what data your CI tool has. Know where it's stored.

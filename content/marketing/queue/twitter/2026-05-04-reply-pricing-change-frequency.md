The easy miss: most teams think competitor pricing changes happen once or twice a year. It's more like once a month — they adjust annual discounts, swap plan names, quietly remove the free tier.

Google Alerts catches a press release about it 2 weeks later, if you're lucky. You need something rendering the actual page.

Depends what you need from CI.

If you want strategic narrative, synthesis, and sales enablement — hire a human. No tool replaces that.

If you want to know what your competitors actually changed on their websites this week — that's a solved automation problem. A person checking 10 URLs every Monday is just a slow cron job.

The mistake is using a $150K analyst to do what a $49/mo tool should handle.

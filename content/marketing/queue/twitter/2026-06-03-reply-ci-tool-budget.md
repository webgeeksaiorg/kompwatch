The issue isn't that Crayon is bad — it does the sales enablement thing well.

The issue is that "competitive intelligence" became a synonym for that product category. So when a 15-person team Googles CI tools, they get quotes for $20-40K platforms built for dedicated CI analysts.

Most teams don't have a CI analyst. They have a PM who checks competitors on Mondays.

The spreadsheet approach works fine until you have 5+ competitors and the "person who updates it" goes on vacation.

What breaks first: frequency. You meant to check weekly. Then it becomes monthly. Then it becomes "whenever someone asks."

Automation doesn't need to be fancy. Even a simple "tell me when this page changes" beats the best spreadsheet that nobody maintains.

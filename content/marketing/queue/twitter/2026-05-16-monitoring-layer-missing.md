12–18 direct competitors, each shipping features weekly. Read that stat this morning.

Most PMMs I've talked to actively track 3-5. The rest are "we should probably watch those guys" names that get checked once a quarter, if that.

Not lazy. Just impossible. Reading 15 competitor blogs a week isn't a job, it's a punishment.

So the industry's answer is better battlecards. More AI summaries. Smarter sales enablement.

But you can't summarize what you didn't capture. The monitoring layer still doesn't exist for most teams.

That's the actual problem.

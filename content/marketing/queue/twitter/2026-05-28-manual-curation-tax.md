Crayon charges ~$30K/year.

And then you still need someone to read the alerts, categorize the updates, decide what matters, and turn it into a battlecard.

That's the part nobody talks about. The tool is the cheap part. The human curation on top of it — that's where the actual cost lives.

I built KompWatch because I couldn't afford the tool OR the analyst. So the AI does the curation. Not perfectly. But enough for a team of three.

Klue and Crayon both assume you have a person whose actual job is running competitive intel. If that's you: they're worth it.

If you're a PM with 18 other priorities: the tool sits unused, you cancel in 6 months, and the money feels wasted. Not a tool failure — a use-case mismatch. The product was built for a CI analyst, not someone who needs one answer per week.

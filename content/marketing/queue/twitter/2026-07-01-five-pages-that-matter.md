The 5 competitor pages that actually matter for PMs:

1. /pricing (or /plans, /buy)
2. /features comparison table
3. /enterprise — "contact sales" vs listed price tells you a lot
4. Careers page — what they're hiring = what they're building
5. Homepage headline — positioning shifts show up here first

Google Alerts monitors the internet for *mentions* of a keyword. It does not monitor competitor *websites* for changes.

These are different problems.

Google Alerts: "Someone wrote an article about CompetitorX."
Website monitoring: "CompetitorX silently changed their pricing page at 11pm."

That second one never shows up in an alert. It's not a press release. No journalist covered it. Just a 3-line pricing table edit.

Both tools. Different jobs. Not interchangeable.

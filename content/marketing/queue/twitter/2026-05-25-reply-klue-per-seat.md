Per-seat is what kills it. You start with 3 seats, get value, want to share with sales, suddenly you're negotiating a $40K/year renewal.

We went flat rate — $149/mo covers 50 competitors, unlimited seats. Makes more sense if you want CI to spread across product + marketing + sales without HR approving every new login.

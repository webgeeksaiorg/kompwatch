Depends what you're trying to catch.

If it's news/press: Google Alerts is fine, genuinely.

If it's pricing changes, feature adds, homepage reframes — you need something that actually loads and renders the page. Google Alerts doesn't see any of that.

Been down this path. $49/mo beats 2 hours of manual tabs every week.

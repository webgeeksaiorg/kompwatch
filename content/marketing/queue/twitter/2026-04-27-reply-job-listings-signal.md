Job listings are one of the best competitive signals nobody monitors.

Competitor posts 3 ML engineer roles in one week → they're building something new. Posts a "Head of Enterprise Sales" → moving upmarket. Suddenly goes quiet on hiring → budget freeze or strategic pivot.

You don't need Crunchbase or a research team. Just watch their /careers page.

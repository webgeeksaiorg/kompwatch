Pricing pages change on Wednesdays more than any other day. Visualping published this from 12,000 tracked pages.

Theory: PM teams ship pricing updates tied to sprint cycles, and Wednesday is mid-sprint for most teams running 2-week sprints.

If you're only checking weekly — especially on Fridays — you're statistically most likely to miss changes that happened midweek.

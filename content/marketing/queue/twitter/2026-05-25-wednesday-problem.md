The changes that hurt most aren't the big announcements.

Competitor drops their starter plan price on a Wednesday. Adds a feature your prospects keep asking about on Thursday. Removes a usage limit that was your main differentiator on Friday.

None of it is press released. None of it shows up in Google Alerts. By Monday morning, when you do your manual tab check — three deals are already in conversation with updated pricing info you didn't have.

Wednesday is the worst day to miss.

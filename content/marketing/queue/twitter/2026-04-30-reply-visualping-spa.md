Heads up if you're using Visualping for competitor sites: it fetches raw HTTP. React/Next.js/Vue sites render content in the browser, not in the HTML. So it screenshots the shell, sees "no change," and you never know your competitor added a feature or killed a pricing tier.

Fails silently. That's the worst kind of failure.

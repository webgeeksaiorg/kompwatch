Google Alerts catches news mentions. Misses everything that happens on the actual website.

Pricing updates, feature additions, copy changes, new case studies added — all invisible to Alerts. The signal that matters most (what changed on their site this week) lives in HTML, not press coverage.

The gap between "I set up Google Alerts" and "I know what my competitors actually changed" is wider than most teams realize until they miss something.

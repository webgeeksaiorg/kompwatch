Honest answer for a team your size: skip the "CI program" framing entirely.

Just set up automated monitoring on competitor pricing + features pages. When something changes, you get an email. Read it, decide if it matters, move on.

That's 95% of what a CI tool actually needs to do for a small team. KompWatch does exactly that. Free tier covers 2 competitors.

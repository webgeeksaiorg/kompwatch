The ritual that broke me:

Every Monday morning. Six browser tabs. Competitor 1 pricing page. Competitor 2 features page. Competitor 3 blog. Open each one. Scan for changes. Write down anything that looked different. Close tabs. Forget about it until next Monday.

Two years of this.

Then I missed a pricing change on a Wednesday. Found out Thursday when a prospect mentioned it. Lost the deal.

That was the last Monday ritual.

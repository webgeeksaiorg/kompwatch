Gartner evaluated 12 CI vendors for the MQ. The two Leaders cost $16K–$30K/year minimum.

That's not a gap in the market — that's the market working as designed. MQ reports exist to help enterprise procurement justify spend. They're not built for sub-100-person companies.

Worth remembering when you're evaluating tools based on analyst coverage.

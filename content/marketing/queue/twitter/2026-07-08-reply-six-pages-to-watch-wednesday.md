Six pages cover 80% of what matters:

/pricing — obvious
/features — catches repositioning before the press release
/ (homepage) — if the hero copy changes, they found a new ICP
/changelog — what they're actually shipping vs what they're saying
/blog (post titles) — shows you what they're optimizing for
/careers — hiring pattern = product bet

That's it. You don't need to watch the whole site.

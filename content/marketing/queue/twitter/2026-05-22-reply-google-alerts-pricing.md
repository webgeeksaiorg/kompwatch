Google Alerts is great for news mentions and press releases. It's useless for the stuff that actually matters — pricing page changes, feature updates, terms of service tweaks.

Those don't generate articles. They just quietly change.

The competitor who raised prices 20% last Tuesday? Google Alerts has no idea.

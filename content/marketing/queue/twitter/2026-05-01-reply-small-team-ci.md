What worked for us: split the problem in two.

Automate the surveillance — price changes, feature adds, homepage copy. Set it and forget it, get a digest.

Do the synthesis manually, quarterly — read their changelog, scan G2 complaints, look at job postings. Two hours, not two weeks.

Trying to make automated tools do the strategy work is where the shelfware problem starts.

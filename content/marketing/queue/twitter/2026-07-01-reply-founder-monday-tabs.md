The honest founder workflow I used before building KompWatch:

Monday morning: open 6 tabs. Stare at competitor pricing pages. Try to remember what they looked like last week. Close tabs feeling vaguely informed.

Caught maybe 30% of actual changes. The other 70% came up in customer conversations and I had to nod like I already knew.

Klue's per-seat pricing does a weird thing: it punishes you for giving more people access to competitive intel.

Your sales team wants battlecards. Your PMM needs them. Your CS team could use them. But each seat costs more.

So you license fewer seats than you need and the tool reaches fewer people than it should.

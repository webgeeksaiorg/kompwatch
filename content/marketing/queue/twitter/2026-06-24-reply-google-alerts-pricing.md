Google Alerts is fine for brand mentions. Completely useless for pricing pages.

It monitors the web index, not the page itself. If a competitor quietly updates their pricing page without publishing a press release, Google Alerts never sees it.

That's almost always how competitor pricing changes happen. Quietly.

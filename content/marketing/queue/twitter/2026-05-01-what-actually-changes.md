6 months of monitoring data across ~40 competitors. What actually changed most:

Pricing pages: ~3x per quarter
Feature/product pages: roughly monthly
Job listings: every week, sometimes more
Blog/changelog: whenever (too noisy to track)

The real surprise: job listings are the best leading indicator. Three ML engineer openings before any product announcement. New AE hires in a vertical you're not in.

Pricing tells you what happened. Jobs tell you what's coming.

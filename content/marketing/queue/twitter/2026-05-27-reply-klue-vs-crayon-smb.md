Both are fine tools for enterprise PMM teams with dedicated CI headcount and $25K+ budgets.

Neither is built for a 10-person SaaS company where "CI" means the PM opens 6 browser tabs every Monday morning.

Klue vs Crayon is a real question. It's just not a question most people asking it actually need to answer right now. Probably start with: "do we have a person whose job this is?"

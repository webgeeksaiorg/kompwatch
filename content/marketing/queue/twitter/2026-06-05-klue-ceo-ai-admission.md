Klue's CEO said the quiet part loud: AI is replacing their core value prop.

Not a competitor attacking them. Their own leadership, explaining the layoffs.

That's a weird position to be in — your $30K/year product's main job was "read competitor content and summarize it." ChatGPT does that free.

The tools that survive this aren't the summarizers. They're the ones that catch what AI can't read: live pricing pages, JavaScript-rendered content, nav changes that signal strategy shifts.

Watching websites is still a human problem. Summarizing them isn't anymore.

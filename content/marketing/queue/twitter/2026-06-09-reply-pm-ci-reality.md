honest answer: most PMs track competitors by watching their own Twitter/LinkedIn, googling their competitor's name occasionally, and setting Google Alerts that fire once a month with something completely irrelevant.

very few have actual automated monitoring in place.

the ones who do are mostly running a cron job someone on the eng team set up years ago that nobody fully understands anymore.

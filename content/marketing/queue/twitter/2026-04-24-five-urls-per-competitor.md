Practical rule that works: 5–8 URLs per competitor, not the whole site.

Pricing page. Features page. Homepage. Jobs page. Maybe a blog.

More than that and you're drowning in footer link changes and cookie banner updates. The signal is in a handful of high-value pages. The noise is everywhere else.

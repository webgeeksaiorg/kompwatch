Depends on what you mean by "track."

If you want to know when copy changes: CSS selector on the specific element, not a full-page diff.
Pricing changes: monitor the pricing page + the checkout flow — they often change prices in the JS before updating the visible page.
Feature updates: their changelog, their job listings, their API docs.

Job listings are underrated. Three ML engineer roles posted last week = something's shipping in 90 days.

What's the specific change type you're trying to catch?

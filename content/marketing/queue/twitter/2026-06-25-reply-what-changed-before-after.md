The minimum bar for a useful competitor alert:

Before: [what the section said]
After: [what it says now]
Why it might matter: [one sentence]

If an alert doesn't include all three, it's creating work, not saving it.

"Competitor updated page" is surveillance theatre. Before/after with context is actual intelligence.

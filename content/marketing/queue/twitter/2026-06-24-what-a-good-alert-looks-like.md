A useful competitor alert tells you:
- What changed (specific section, not "page updated")
- What it said before vs now
- Why it might matter

Most alerts just say "competitor.com detected changes."

Cool. Where? What changed? Is it pricing? A feature? A job listing?

You end up ignoring them. Then a customer asks why you didn't know their main competitor dropped prices last week.

Review sites are a real blind spot. Most monitoring tools watch competitors' owned pages but not what customers are saying on G2, Capterra, or Reddit.

A competitor getting hammered on "bad customer support" in their G2 reviews is useful sales intel. Knowing they're getting five-star reviews after a product launch is also useful — different response, same information need.

We're building toward this. Not live yet but it's on the roadmap.

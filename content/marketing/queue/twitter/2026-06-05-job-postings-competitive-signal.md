Job postings are the most underused competitive signal.

Your competitor just posted 4 ML engineer roles. That's not a "we're hiring" announcement. That's a product roadmap leak.

"2 backend engineers, infrastructure focus" = scaling pain.
"1 enterprise AE + 2 SDRs" = going upmarket.
"Head of Partnerships" = they're building an ecosystem play.

Most CI tools won't catch this because job listings are JavaScript-rendered. Your Google Alert definitely won't.

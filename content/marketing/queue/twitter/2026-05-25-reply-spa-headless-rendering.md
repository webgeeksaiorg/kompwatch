Static HTML diffing breaks the moment your competitor's site loads content via JavaScript. Which is most SaaS sites built in the last 5 years.

The tool that texts "pricing page changed!" after crawling an empty div is worse than no tool. You're falsely confident.

Playwright headless rendering is the fix — actually waits for the content to load before taking the snapshot. Adds 3-4 seconds per page. Worth every millisecond.

The Crayon adoption problem isn't the software. It's the model.

Any tool that requires daily manual input from 5 different teams is going to fail in a 10-person company. Not because the team is lazy — because nobody has 2 hours a week to curate an intelligence feed on top of their actual job.

Automation-first is the only model that survives in a lean org.

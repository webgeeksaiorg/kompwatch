The win/loss data point most teams miss: when a competitor repositioned downmarket before you noticed.

They were enterprise. You weren't worried. Then your mid-market pipeline starts getting hit.

In retrospect, the signals were there: new pricing tier at half the old entry point, "self-serve" added to the homepage, "setup in minutes" copy appearing. All 3-4 months before deals started going wrong.

Not saying you'd win every deal with earlier warning. But you'd adjust your talk track. Stop being caught flat-footed.

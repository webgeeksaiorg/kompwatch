For most SaaS, 5 pages covers 90% of what matters:

1. Pricing
2. Features / product overview  
3. Changelog or what's new
4. Customers / case studies
5. Homepage (for positioning shifts)

That's maybe 4-6 URLs per competitor. Start there. Most of the signal lives on these pages, not buried in docs or blog posts.

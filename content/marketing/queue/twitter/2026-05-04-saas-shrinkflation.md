SaaS shrinkflation is happening right now.

Average price increase: 8–12% in 2026. But the sneakier move: same price, less product. Usage caps cut 20–30%. Features quietly moved to higher tiers.

If a competitor went from "unlimited" to "up to 10 users" at the same price, that's a pricing change. No invoice changes, so most teams never catch it.

The only way to know is to watch the page.

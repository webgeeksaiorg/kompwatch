The problem is usually the volume, not the tool. Every change surfaced with equal weight = your team stops reading.

What helped us: defining a threshold before monitoring starts. Pricing page HIGH, feature tier HIGH, everything else LOW or ignore. Then only alert on HIGH.

3 meaningful alerts/week > 50 undifferentiated ones.

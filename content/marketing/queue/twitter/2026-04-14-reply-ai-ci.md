The AI part isn't magic. It's just: diff the HTML, pass the diff to Claude, ask "what actually changed for a user, in plain English."

The result is "they removed the free tier and added a 14-day trial to their Pro plan" instead of 3,000 lines of HTML diff.

That's it. But that translation from noise to signal is the whole product.

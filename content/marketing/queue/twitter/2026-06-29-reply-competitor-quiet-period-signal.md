Interesting pattern I've noticed: competitors go weirdly quiet about 4-6 weeks before a major launch.

Less blog content. Fewer job listings. The changelog stops updating. Then one day everything drops at once.

The absence of changes is itself a signal. "Nothing changed for 5 weeks on their product page" right before a conference is not neutral news.

Trying to quantify this better. Still anecdotal but the pattern's real.

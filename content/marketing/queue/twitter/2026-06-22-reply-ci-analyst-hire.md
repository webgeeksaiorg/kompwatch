Worth asking before posting the JD: what will they actually spend their time on?

CI collection and processing is largely automatable. The synthesis — "here's what this means for our Q3 positioning" — is the genuinely valuable human part.

A lot of teams end up hiring analysts to do what software should be doing, then wondering why they're not getting strategic output.

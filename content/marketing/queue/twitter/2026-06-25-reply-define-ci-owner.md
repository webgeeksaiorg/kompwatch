Before buying any CI tool, answer four questions:

1. Who gets the alert?
2. Who decides if it's actionable?
3. Who updates the battle card?
4. How does the update reach reps?

If you can't answer those before the tool is live, the alerts will fire into a void. The tool becomes shelfware in 60 days regardless of how good it is.

Define the process first. Then automate it.

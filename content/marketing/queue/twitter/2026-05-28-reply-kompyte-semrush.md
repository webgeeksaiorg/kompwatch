Kompyte getting absorbed into Semrush was always going to go one direction: more features, higher price, bundled into a larger platform you have to buy.

If you were using Kompyte for competitor website monitoring specifically, you're probably now paying for SEO tools you don't need.

That's how these rollups work. The thing you liked becomes a feature inside something bigger, and you're paying for the something bigger.

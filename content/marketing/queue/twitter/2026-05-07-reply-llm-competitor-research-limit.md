Good for: historical context, synthesizing what you already know, drafting a comparison framework.

Bad for: monitoring. ChatGPT has a training cutoff. If your competitor changed their pricing last month, it doesn't know. If they removed the free tier two weeks ago, it doesn't know. If their job listings suddenly show 8 AI engineer openings this week, it definitely doesn't know.

LLMs are great at summarizing changes. They can't detect them.

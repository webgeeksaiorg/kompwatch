The 26% adoption stat is brutal. Sales spends 2% of a call on competitive positioning, product marketing spends 40% of their time on competitive content.

The mismatch is a distribution problem. Reps don't open a wiki mid-call. They need the right info in the right format at the right moment.

That's what makes real-time and exportable matter more than "comprehensive."

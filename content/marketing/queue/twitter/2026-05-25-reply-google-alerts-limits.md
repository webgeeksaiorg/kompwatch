Google Alerts catches indexed content — blog posts, press releases, news mentions.

It misses: pricing page changes, removed free tier, new feature added to /features, updated signup flow, job listings shifting from "growth" to "enterprise sales."

That's most of what actually matters for product decisions. The quiet stuff competitors change without announcing it.

Google Alerts misses nearly everything that matters for SaaS competitor monitoring because it doesn't render JavaScript. Every competitor pricing page in 2026 is a React app. curl the URL, you get a blank div. You need a headless browser to see what actually changed.

That's the fundamental problem — not a fixable edge case.

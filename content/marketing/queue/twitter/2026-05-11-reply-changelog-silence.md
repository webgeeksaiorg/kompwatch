A competitor going quiet on their changelog is actually a signal.

Sprint for 8 weeks, then nothing for 6 weeks? Either:
a) Rebuilding something big
b) Team turnover
c) Roadmap pivot

You can't know which without watching the pattern over time. That's what consistent monitoring gives you — context for the silence, not just the noise.

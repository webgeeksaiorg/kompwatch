Start with pricing. Then features. Then changelog if they have one.

Skip the homepage. It changes for design reasons, not strategy reasons.

The compare pages (/vs/competitor-name) are interesting if they have them — tells you who they feel threatened by.

Jobs page is a 3-6 month leading indicator. 4 ML engineers hired today = something shipping next quarter.

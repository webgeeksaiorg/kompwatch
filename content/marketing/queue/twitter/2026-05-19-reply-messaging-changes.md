Pricing changes get all the attention but messaging changes are more interesting.

When a competitor rewrites their hero headline from "the all-in-one CI platform" to "real-time battlecards for sales teams" — that's a positioning pivot. Something changed in their customer feedback. They learned something.

That signal usually shows up 6-12 months before a real threat becomes visible. Most teams aren't watching for it.

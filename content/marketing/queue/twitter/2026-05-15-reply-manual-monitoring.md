The Monday morning competitor check is a ritual in every PMM I've talked to.

6 tabs pinned. 45 minutes. Half the time nothing changed. The other half, something changed last Wednesday and you missed it by 5 days.

The check frequency problem is underrated. Competitors don't change on your schedule.

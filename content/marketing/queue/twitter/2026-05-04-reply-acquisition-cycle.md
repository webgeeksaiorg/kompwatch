The CI tool acquisition pattern:

Kompyte → acquired by Semrush (2022)
Semrush → acquired by Adobe (2025, $1.9B)
Klue → acquired Ignition (Sept 2025, entire team left to found DoubleO AI)
Crayon → doubled headcount for enterprise, moved away from SMB

Every consolidation creates displaced customers who wanted a simple tool and now own a feature inside an enterprise platform they don't fully use.

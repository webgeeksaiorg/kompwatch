For a 15-person team: Crayon is overkill and the price just went up. Klue requires a dedicated analyst to get value out of it.

I've been running KompWatch — it watches competitor websites for changes and sends a plain English email when something shifts. $49/mo, no analyst required, no 30-day onboarding.

If you want something simpler that just monitors URLs you care about, that's us.

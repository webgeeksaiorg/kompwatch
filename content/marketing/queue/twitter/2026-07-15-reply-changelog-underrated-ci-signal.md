Changelog pages are underrated for this.

Most competitors publish what they shipped. The info is public. But who's reading it?

I started monitoring competitor changelogs alongside their pricing and feature pages. Some of the most useful CI signals come from "minor updates" in a changelog that nobody treats as a CI input.

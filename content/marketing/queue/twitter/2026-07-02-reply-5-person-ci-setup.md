Here's the CI setup I'd give to a 5-person SaaS team with no budget:

1. Google Alerts for brand mentions (free, catches press coverage)
2. A real monitoring tool for the actual pages (Google Alerts doesn't watch website changes)
3. Careers page in the mix — you'll see hiring signals 2-3 months before any product announcement
4. Slack channel that summarizes changes weekly, not in real-time

The Slack summary is the key. Real-time alerts get muted. Weekly digests get read.

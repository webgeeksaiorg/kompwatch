You'll get a shell div and a JS bundle.

That's what Google Alerts sees. That's what most monitoring tools see. No JavaScript execution = no rendered content.

Open the same URL in Chrome and you see what actually changed.

"I have Google Alerts set up" ≠ "I'm monitoring my competitors."

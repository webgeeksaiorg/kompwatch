Depends on what "monitoring" means to you.

If you need battlecard management, sales enablement, win/loss analysis — Klue or Crayon. Budget $20-50K/year and hire someone to run it.

If you just need to know when your competitor changes their pricing page, homepage copy, or features list — you don't need that.

We built KompWatch for the second case. $49/mo, no analyst required. It won't replace Crayon. Shouldn't try to.

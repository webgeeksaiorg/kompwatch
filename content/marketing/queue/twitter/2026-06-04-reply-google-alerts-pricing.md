Google Alerts for competitor pricing changes is a common answer here.

Problem: Google doesn't crawl most SaaS pricing pages frequently enough to catch quiet edits. A feature limit cut in half, a plan tier quietly removed — none of that triggers a news alert.

You need something watching the actual HTML on a schedule.

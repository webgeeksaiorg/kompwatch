Visualping is fine up to 3 or 4 pages. At 5+ competitors (each with a pricing page, a features page, and a changelog), the free tier is gone and you're cobbling together manual checks for the rest.

The other issue: it diffs raw HTML. Modern SaaS pricing pages are mostly JavaScript. Visualping sees nothing when the content renders client-side.

Worked fine in 2018. Less fine now.

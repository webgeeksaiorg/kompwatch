Pricing experiments are the hardest to catch because they're intentionally hidden. Your competitor shows $79 to some users and $99 to others.

What you CAN catch: when they change the default, update the pricing page copy, add/remove a tier, or change annual vs monthly positioning.

That's usually the signal that the experiment ended and they made a decision.

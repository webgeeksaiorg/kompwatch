This is the #1 reason CI tools get abandoned. Not cost. Noise.

You set it up. First week: feels powerful. Week 3: 40 alerts/day, 2 actionable. You mute it. Forget it exists. Three months later you're doing manual Monday tab-checking again.

The fix isn't finer filters. It's AI summarization that tells you what *actually changed* vs background noise.

More often than you think. A year of monitoring 200+ SaaS pricing pages — about 40% changed at least once per quarter. That's not a fluke or a rebranding. That's ongoing pricing optimization.

The ones that changed the most: A/B testing CTA copy, adjusting trial lengths, moving features between tiers.

None of it shows up in press releases.

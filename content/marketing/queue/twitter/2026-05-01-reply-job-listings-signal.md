The most underrated competitor signal: job listings.

Engineering hires = product direction. Three ML engineer openings before any announcement = they're building something AI-related.

New AE hires in a vertical you're not selling into = expansion move. Watch the territories on those job posts.

Pricing pages change quarterly. Job listings change weekly and they're almost never spun as strategic messaging — it's just what's actually happening.

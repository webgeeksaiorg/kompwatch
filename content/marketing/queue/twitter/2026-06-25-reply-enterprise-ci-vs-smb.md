Crayon and Klue are built for enterprise CI teams with dedicated analysts, Salesforce workflows, quarterly review cadences.

If you have that: probably worth the $20-40K/yr.

Most companies don't have that. Most product teams are two PMs trying to keep track of three competitors between roadmap reviews.

The right tool for those teams isn't a scaled-down enterprise platform. It's something that does one thing well: tells you when stuff changes.

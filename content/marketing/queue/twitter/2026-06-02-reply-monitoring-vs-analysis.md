There's a distinction nobody talks about: monitoring (did this page change?) vs analysis (what does this change mean?).

Most $50/mo tools do monitoring. KompWatch does monitoring + AI summary. Seeto does analysis on demand but not monitoring.

They're solving different problems. Knowing which one you actually need is half the decision.

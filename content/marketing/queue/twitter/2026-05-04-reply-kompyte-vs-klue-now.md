The comparison that actually matters now:

Kompyte: Adobe portfolio, ~$20K avg ARR, roadmap integration pending, per-competitor upsell model.

Klue: Enterprise, $16–30K/yr, needs a dedicated CI analyst to get full value — they say so in their own case studies.

Both are built for companies with analysts. If your CI "team" is one PM who checks things between meetings, neither is really designed for you.

Different tool for a different problem.

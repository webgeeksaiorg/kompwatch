Google Alerts is great for news mentions. Completely useless for pricing page changes, feature updates, or anything that doesn't generate a news article.

A competitor can quietly drop their prices 30%, kill a feature tier, or add a new integration — none of that shows up in Alerts.

It's two different problems: tracking what people *say about* competitors vs. tracking what competitors actually *do* on their site.

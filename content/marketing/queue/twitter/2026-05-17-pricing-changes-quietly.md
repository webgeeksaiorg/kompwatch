Something I noticed after watching 50+ SaaS pricing pages for the past few months:

Most pricing changes happen quietly. No announcement. No press release. Just — the number changed.

67% of the changes I've caught have been pricing-related. Features second. Job listings third (those predict roadmap moves 6 weeks out).

The ones who check manually miss almost all of it.

142K tech jobs cut in 2026, ~50% cited as AI-driven. CI analyst is one of the first roles to go when companies automate.

The irony: most enterprise CI tools require an analyst to extract value from them. So you cut the analyst and the $30K/year tool becomes dead weight.

That's the trap.

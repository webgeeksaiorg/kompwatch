Google Alerts misses:
- Any JavaScript-rendered content
- Pricing page changes (rarely indexed)
- Navigation and UI changes
- Changes to existing text (not new pages)

It catches press releases and blog posts. That's it.

It's not a monitoring tool. It's a news alert.

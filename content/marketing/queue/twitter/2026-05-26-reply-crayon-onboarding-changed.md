The part that gets me is the onboarding. Crayon used to take "days to first value." SoftwareOne's enterprise motion turns that into weeks minimum.

Enterprise procurement requires security reviews, vendor assessments, legal sign-off. A 15-person startup doesn't have that capacity.

The acquisition didn't just change the price. It changed who can actually buy the product.

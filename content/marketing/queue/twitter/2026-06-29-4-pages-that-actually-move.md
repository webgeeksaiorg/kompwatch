The question I get most after people sign up: "Do I really have to add each competitor URL one by one?"

Yes, currently. We're adding CSV bulk import soon.

But also: take 10 minutes to really think about which pages matter. Competitors' homepage is rarely the signal. The pricing page, the changelog, the careers page, the feature comparison page — those four tell you more than the homepage ever will.

I've seen people add 30 URLs and then complain about noise. Start with the 4 pages per competitor that actually move.

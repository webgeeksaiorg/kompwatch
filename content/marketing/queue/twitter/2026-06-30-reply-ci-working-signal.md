One weird signal I use to check if CI is working for a team: do they bring competitor intel to the weekly standup unprompted?

If someone has to ask "did we check what competitors are doing?" every week, CI is failing. It's treated as a chore.

If the PM mentions it naturally — "oh by the way, CompetitorX moved to usage-based pricing this week, here's what that means for us" — it's working.

The tool is almost irrelevant at that point. Culture ate the process.

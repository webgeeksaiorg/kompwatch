Klue's Compete Agent is genuinely impressive tech. Real-time deal intel, AI-generated battlecards, Salesforce integration. The problem: mid-five to low-six figures/yr for teams under 20 reps. You're paying more for the AI agent than your competitive monitoring is worth.

What most teams actually need: "send me an email when my competitor changes their pricing page." That's it. $49/mo problem, not a $50K/yr one.

we figured out our aha moment: the first time a competitor change hits your inbox.

so we built a thing. free users who see their first detected change now get a nudge to upgrade. not a generic "upgrade to pro!" modal — a contextual prompt showing exactly what they'd be able to do with that change on Pro.

the hypothesis: if you've already seen the value, the upgrade ask lands differently.

shipping experiments on a 2-person budget is fun. we'll see what the data says.

One specific page worth monitoring that most people skip: the free trial / signup page.

When a competitor adds a credit card field to their free trial → they're testing conversion. When they remove it → they're testing PLG. When they change the trial length from 14 to 30 days → they heard "not enough time to get value."

Every change is a hypothesis they're running. You can learn a lot from watching.

Crayon and Klue are built for companies with a dedicated competitive intelligence function.

A full-time CI analyst. A program to run. Battlecards to maintain. Salesforce to sync.

Most product teams don't have that. They have a PM who checks competitor websites on Monday and a Notion page that's 6 months out of date.

KompWatch is built for the second group. You paste URLs. We tell you when something changes. No CI analyst required.

The quarterly review cadence made sense when competitive moves were slow. Pricing changed once a year, features shipped in annual releases.

Modern SaaS: competitor pricing pages change 39% of the time in a given month. By the time your Q3 review surfaces it, the market has already moved.

Weekly automated monitoring + quarterly strategy review is the combo that actually works.

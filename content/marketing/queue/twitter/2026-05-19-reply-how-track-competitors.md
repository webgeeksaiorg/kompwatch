Depends on how much signal you actually need.

If you want to know when a competitor changes pricing, updates their features page, or shifts their messaging — website monitoring is enough and costs almost nothing.

If you want intent data, social listening, win/loss analysis — that's a different budget and a different problem.

Most early-stage teams overestimate how much they need the second category.

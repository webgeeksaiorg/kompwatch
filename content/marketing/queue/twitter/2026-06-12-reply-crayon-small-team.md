For a 10-person team, Crayon is overkill twice over — first on price, then on complexity.

Worth asking: do you need battlecards pushed to Salesforce, or just know when a competitor's pricing or feature page changes?

Those are different product categories. If it's the latter, Visualping does the detection but gives you raw HTML diffs. KompWatch does the same with an AI summary of what actually changed. $49/mo.

If you genuinely need battlecard tooling, Crayon makes more sense. But most small teams don't.

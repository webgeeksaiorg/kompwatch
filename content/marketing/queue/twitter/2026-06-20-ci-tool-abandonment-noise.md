Most CI tools get abandoned because of the alerts. Not the cost.

You get 50 changes a week. A pricing tweak is in there somewhere. So is a footer typo fix. The tool treats them the same.

Your team learns to ignore the digest. By month 8 nobody's opening it.

The problem is unweighted monitoring. Not the subscription price.

GummySearch shut down in December 2025 after Reddit killed their API access.

If you used it for tracking competitor mentions and community signals — that workflow is just... gone.

What people are duct-taping together since: Google Alerts (misses most of it), manual Reddit search (not scalable), and IFTTT RSS hacks (noisy).

We don't do Reddit scraping (that door's closed for everyone now), but we do track competitor website changes — pricing pages, feature pages, job listings. Different surface, same underlying problem: you need to know when things change.

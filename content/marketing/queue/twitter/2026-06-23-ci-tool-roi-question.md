The ROI question for competitor monitoring is backwards.

Teams ask: "Is this tool worth $X/mo?"

Better question: "Did we catch the pricing change before the sales call? Or did the prospect tell us?"

If it's usually the prospect, the tool isn't working — or nobody's reading it.

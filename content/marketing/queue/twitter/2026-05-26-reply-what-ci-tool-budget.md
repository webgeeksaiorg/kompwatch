Honest answer to this depends on what you actually need.

"Competitive intelligence" is a huge umbrella. If you want news mentions, Talkwalker or Brand24 ($99-250/mo). Job listings? LinkedIn alerts. Pricing/feature page changes? That's where it gets weird — most tools either cost $25K+/yr or give you diffs of empty divs.

We built KompWatch for that last case specifically. Not a comprehensive platform. Watches pages, tells you what changed in plain English. $49/mo.

What's the specific thing you're trying to track?

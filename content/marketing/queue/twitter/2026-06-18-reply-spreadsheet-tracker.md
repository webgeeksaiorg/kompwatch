Spreadsheets are fine for the strategic layer — positioning maps, battlecard intel, win/loss patterns.

Where they break: they only update when someone remembers to check. And the stuff that most needs to be caught (pricing changes, feature adds) happens on a Tuesday at 2 AM, not during your monthly review session.

Automate the data collection. Keep the human for the analysis.

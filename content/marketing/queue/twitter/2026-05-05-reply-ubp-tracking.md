The UBP shift is particularly rough for competitive intel. Seat-based pricing was stable — "Pro plan is $X/seat, 3 seat minimum." Easy to track.

Usage-based pricing pages get restructured quarterly. Tiers renamed, minimums moved, add-ons repackaged. Watching the page change is table stakes now. Understanding what the restructure actually means is the harder problem.

Pricing pages are actually the hardest to track with Google Alerts because the change doesn't generate new indexed content — they just quietly update the number.

The only way to catch it is screenshotting + diffing on a schedule. Which is exactly what I built KompWatch to do, after missing a competitor's price drop for 3 weeks.

The problem with "I check once a week" is that competitors don't change on your schedule.

Pricing change drops Thursday. You check Monday. Deal is Tuesday.

You checked. You just checked too late.

Daily automated monitoring beats weekly manual — not because you're more disciplined, but because the timing actually aligns.

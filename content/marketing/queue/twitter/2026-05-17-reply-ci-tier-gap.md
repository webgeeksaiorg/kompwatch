There's a tier gap nobody talks about.

Crayon and Klue are built for companies with a dedicated CI analyst. Full workflow, battlecards, CRM integration, enablement layer. Right tool — wrong buyer if you're a 15-person team.

The $49/mo tools do one thing: tell you when something changed. That's 90% of what most teams need.

The mistake is comparing them like they're the same category.

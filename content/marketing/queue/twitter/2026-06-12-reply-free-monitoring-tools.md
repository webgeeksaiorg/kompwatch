Free tools that actually work: Google Alerts (good for press mentions, useless for website changes), Visualping free tier (50 checks/mo, gets you started, runs out fast), Wappalyzer (tech stack changes only).

The gap is website change detection with AI-readable summaries. Free tier tools give you raw diffs or nothing.

$49/mo is worth it once you have more than 2 competitors and you're checking manually even once a week.

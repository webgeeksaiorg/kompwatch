For a small team, honestly depends on what you're trying to do:

- Just want news/brand mentions → Google Alerts (free)
- 1-5 static pages → Visualping (free, breaks on JS sites)
- Technical team, okay with maintenance → DIY Playwright script
- Want it to just work, 10+ competitors → something like KompWatch ($49/mo)

Crayon/Klue are $25-40K/yr and do much more — CRM integration, battlecards, social listening. If you need all that, fine. If you just need website change alerts, that's massive overkill.

What specifically are you trying to track?

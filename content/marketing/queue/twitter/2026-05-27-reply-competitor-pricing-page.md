Pricing pages are deceptively hard to monitor manually.

Most companies change them without announcement. No changelog, no blog post, no press release. They just update the page on a Tuesday.

The worst case: you find out because a prospect says "their price went down, can you match it?" Three months after it happened.

Set up a monitor on the exact pricing page URL, not just the domain. Competitor sites redirect pricing URLs all the time.

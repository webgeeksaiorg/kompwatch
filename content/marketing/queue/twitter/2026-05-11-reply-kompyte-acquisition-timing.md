Worth flagging: Adobe closed the Semrush acquisition in April. Kompyte is a Semrush product, so it's now inside Adobe.

Same period: Crayon is under SoftwareOne (IT asset management). Klue cut 40% of staff and rebuilt around Microsoft stack.

Three of the four major CI tools absorbed into larger companies in about 12 months. Mid-acquisition is historically the right window to evaluate alternatives, before roadmaps get formally realigned.

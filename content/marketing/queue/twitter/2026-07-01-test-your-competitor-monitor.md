Quick test for your competitor monitoring tool:

Find a competitor with a React or Next.js pricing page. Run your monitor on it. 

If the tool is HTTP-only (curl-level), it's loading an empty div and reporting "no changes" constantly. 

~40% of SaaS pricing pages won't render without JavaScript execution.

Real-time alerts for competitor changes sounds appealing. In practice: it's 11pm, you're in bed, your phone buzzes — competitor updated their footer copyright year.

We ship real-time only for high-severity changes (pricing moves, new feature announcements, plan restructuring). Everything else batches into a weekly digest.

Sleep matters. So does actually reading the alerts.

I hear this constantly. Crayon's minimum viable contract is now $12K-15K/year. Klue won't tell you the price until you're on a call, which tells you the price.

If you're a 5-person startup, that's a full junior hire. It's an insane trade-off for "I want to know when my competitor changes their pricing page."

Kompyte is cheaper ($99/month). SEMrush has CI features ($140/month). KompWatch is $49/month and does exactly one thing — website change monitoring with AI summaries.

None of these replace Crayon for enterprise sales workflows. All of them are reasonable if you just want website change alerts.

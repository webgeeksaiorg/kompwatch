The #1 reason CI tools fail inside companies isn't the price.

It's that nobody reads the alerts after week two.

Crayon's own G2 reviews say it: "too much noise, not enough signal." That's not a product bug. It's what happens when you monitor everything and let humans decide what matters.

You need fewer alerts. Not more coverage. Not a better dashboard. Fewer alerts that are actually right.

That's the whole product bet with KompWatch — AI filters before you see it, not after.

The process for a 5-person team should be:

1. Pick the 3-5 competitors that actually matter right now
2. Watch their pricing page, features page, homepage
3. Get notified when something changes
4. Spend 10 minutes deciding if it matters

That's it. No analyst. No battlecard template. No quarterly review deck.

The enterprise CI process was designed for enterprise CI teams. You're not that.

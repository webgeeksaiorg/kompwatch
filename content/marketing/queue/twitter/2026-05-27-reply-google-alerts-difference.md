Google Alerts fires when someone publishes *about* your competitor. A blog post. A press release. A news mention.

It does not fire when your competitor changes their own website.

Silent pricing page rewrite on a Tuesday afternoon? No alert. Feature quietly removed? No alert. Free tier killed? No alert.

Two completely different signals. Neither is wrong. But if you want to know what changed on competitor.com — Alerts won't tell you.

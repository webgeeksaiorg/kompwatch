"Competitive intelligence" and "competitor monitoring" are not the same thing.

Monitoring: knowing what changed on your competitor's pricing page last Tuesday.

Intelligence: understanding what that change means for your positioning, your pipeline, your next quarter.

Most teams buy intelligence tools when they need monitoring first. You can't analyze what you haven't captured.

Monitoring is the foundation. Nobody sells it that way because it sounds boring. But boring is where you start.

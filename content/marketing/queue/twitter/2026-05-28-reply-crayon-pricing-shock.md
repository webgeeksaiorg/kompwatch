The number that gets people is not the license. It's the license + the person you need to actually run it.

Crayon at $30K/year + one analyst at $80K/year = $110K to know when your competitors change their pricing page. That math breaks at most company sizes.

Not saying don't buy it. If you have a 50-person sales team that needs battlecards weekly, it might pencil out. But for a 10-person SaaS? You're paying for a jet when you need a bicycle.

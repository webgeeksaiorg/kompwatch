We default to every 6 hours on Pro, hourly on Team. Free is daily.

Hourly feels like overkill until the one time your competitor runs a flash sale and your sales team doesn't find out until the next morning. Then it doesn't feel like overkill anymore.

The frequency question is really "how much does a 24-hour delay cost you?" For most teams, the answer changes after one bad experience.

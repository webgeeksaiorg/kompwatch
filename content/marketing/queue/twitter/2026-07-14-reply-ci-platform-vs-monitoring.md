Crayon and Klue are full CI platforms — win/loss analysis, market maps, sales content libraries, analyst workflows. Great if you have a CI team and the budget to support it.

Most early-stage SaaS teams need something much simpler: tell me when my competitor's website changes and explain what changed.

That's a $49/mo problem, not a $25K/yr problem.

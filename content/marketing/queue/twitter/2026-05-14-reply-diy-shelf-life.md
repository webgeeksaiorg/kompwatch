I ran a homemade cron job + email alert setup for about 14 months before I just built something real.

Worked fine until it didn't. SPAs broke it. Rate limiting broke it. The "summary" was raw HTML diff that nobody wanted to read.

DIY gets you 60% of the way. The last 40% is where you either give up or build a product.

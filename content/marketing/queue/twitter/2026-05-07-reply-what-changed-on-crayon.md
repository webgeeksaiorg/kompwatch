Crayon rebranded as SoftwareOne in January 2026. Same product, new parent, unclear roadmap for teams not in the Fortune 500 range.

The thing worth watching: SoftwareOne's core business is software procurement and licensing management for enterprise. Crayon/competitive intel is a feature in that portfolio now, not a standalone product they're actively building.

Whether that matters depends on how deep your team's usage goes and whether you're on a renewal cycle soon.

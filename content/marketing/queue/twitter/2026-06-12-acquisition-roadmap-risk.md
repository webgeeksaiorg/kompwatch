Adobe bought Semrush. Semrush owns Kompyte.

That's three layers of corporate ownership for a CI tool a lot of small teams were using.

Acquisitions don't kill products on day one. They kill roadmaps. The feature you needed 18 months from now quietly disappears from the roadmap, and you only figure that out in 18 months.

This is why I prefer smaller, focused tools. Less pitch deck energy. More predictable.

The most interesting pricing page changes aren't the big ones.

New plan announced → you'll hear about it. Competitor tweets it, ProductHunt post, tech press picks it up.

The quiet changes are what monitoring catches: free tier seat limit silently dropped from 5 to 3. "Contact sales" on the enterprise tier replaced with an actual price. Starter plan annual discount changed from 20% to 16%.

Those don't get announced. Your sales team won't know unless someone is watching.

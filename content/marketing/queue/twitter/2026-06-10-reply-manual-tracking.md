The spreadsheet phase lasts until the first time you miss something important. Then you automate.

I went through this exact cycle. Spreadsheet → RSS feeds → set-up-and-forget Slack integration → eventually built something that actually sends me useful alerts.

The pain of manual isn't daily, it's the one Wednesday you didn't check and your competitor launched a new tier.

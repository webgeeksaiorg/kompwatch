Pricing pages are the most actively maintained page on a SaaS site. More updates than blog, docs, or feature pages combined.

Because pricing experiments are cheap. Change a number, measure conversion, roll it back or keep it. Entire A/B cycle in a week.

The irony: the page most critical to your competitive positioning is the one your competitor is changing the most aggressively and least publicly.

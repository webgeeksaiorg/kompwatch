If you're under 30 people, Klue will probably cost more than your entire software stack. The product is built around a dedicated PMM who maintains battlecards full-time — that's not a criticism, just what makes it work at scale.

Without that person, you get 90 days of value and then a $20K/yr calendar reminder nobody opens.

The question isn't "is Klue good." It is good. The question is whether your team size matches the tool's requirements. Most early-stage teams don't.

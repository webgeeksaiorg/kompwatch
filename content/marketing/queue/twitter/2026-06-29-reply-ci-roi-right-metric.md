The ROI question for CI tools is almost always framed wrong.

It's not "what did we learn?" It's "how fast did we know, and did the right people act on it?"

Knew a competitor dropped prices two weeks after the fact? Zero ROI, even if the tool flagged it. Knew within 4 hours, briefed the sales team same day, won the next three competitive deals? Different story.

Speed of signal → speed of action. That's the actual metric.

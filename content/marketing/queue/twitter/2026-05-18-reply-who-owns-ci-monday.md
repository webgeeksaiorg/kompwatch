At most companies under 100 people, competitive intelligence is owned by:

- The PM (when they have time)
- The PMM (when there is one)
- Whoever lost the last deal to a competitor
- Nobody (most of the time)

This isn't a people problem. It's a tooling problem. The tools that exist require dedicated headcount to run. So if you don't have headcount, you don't use the tool.

KompWatch is built for the "nobody" category. Automated, not assisted.

Crayon's pricing is tied to how many competitors you track.

You start with five. Add three more during the year. Then five more when your market gets competitive. Each one bumps your contract up.

That's not a pricing model. That's a mechanism that guarantees costs grow exactly when your competitive pressure grows.

KompWatch Pro: $49/mo, 10 competitors, flat. Team: $149/mo, 50 competitors, flat. The number in your market doesn't change our price.

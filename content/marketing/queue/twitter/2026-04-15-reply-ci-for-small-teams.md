For a 5-person team: spreadsheet + Google Alerts + one person who manually checks every Monday is probably fine. It's not automated but it's honest about what it is.

The break point is when that person's Monday tab routine hits 12+ competitors, or when you lose a deal because you didn't know about a pricing change until the customer mentioned it in the call.

That's when the ROI math on $49/month becomes really easy.

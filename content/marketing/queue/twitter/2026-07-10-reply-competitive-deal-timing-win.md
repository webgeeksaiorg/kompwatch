This is the whole game. The teams that win on competitive deals aren't smarter — they just know faster.

The window between "competitor made a change" and "prospect brings it up in a call" is usually a few weeks. That's your prep time.

Most teams find out on the call. A few find out the day it changes. That gap is a real advantage.

changedetection.io is genuinely great for raw technical use. No judgment using it.

The one thing it doesn't do: tell you what a change *means*. You get "these 47 characters changed in this CSS block" and you're left interpreting.

For engineering teams that can parse HTML diffs and filter noise manually — works fine. For a PM who just wants to know "did my competitor change their pricing?" — the interpretation layer matters a lot.

Klue is a great product if you have someone whose job it is to run it.

Battlecard templates, win/loss tracking, competitive playbooks — all valuable. All require active management to stay current.

If your competitive intel workflow is "PM checks manually when they have 20 minutes," that's a different product category. Not worse, just different.

The Gartner badge is real. Their feature set is real. What it doesn't tell you: Klue assumes you have someone whose job is competitive intelligence. Full time or close to it.

Small teams don't have that. And the tool actively gets worse the less you use it — alerts pile up, nobody reads them, and eventually someone cancels it because it "doesn't work."

It works. You just needed a CI analyst to come with it.

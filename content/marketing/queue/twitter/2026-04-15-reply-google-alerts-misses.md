Google Alerts catches mentions. It doesn't catch page edits.

If your competitor quietly removes their free tier — no press release, no blog post, just a pricing page update — Google Alerts is silent. There's nothing to index.

That's the 40-60% of meaningful competitor changes that happen with zero announcement. Price restructures, feature page rewrites, nav changes that signal positioning shifts.

Website monitoring fills that gap. Alerts when the page itself changes, not just when someone writes about it.

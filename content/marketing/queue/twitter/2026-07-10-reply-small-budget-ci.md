Depends on what you actually need.

If it's "tell me when something on their website changes" — you don't need Crayon or Klue. Those are $15K-$30K/yr tools with sales calls and onboarding and a Slack integration you'll mute in a week.

You need something that watches pages and tells you when stuff changes. That's a $49/mo problem, not a $500/mo problem.

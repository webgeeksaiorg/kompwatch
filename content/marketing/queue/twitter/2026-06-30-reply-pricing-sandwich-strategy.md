Pricing sandwich is the most common competitor play I see in the data.

Step 1: Introduce a new tier below your current entry point. (Looks affordable, creates a new comparison anchor.)
Step 2: Raise the middle tier 10-15%.
Step 3: Keep the top tier visible but push it to "Contact us."

Net result: they raised prices on existing customers, look accessible to new ones, and removed price visibility from the top. All without any PR.

You'd only catch this if you were watching the pricing page continuously. It doesn't show up in press releases.

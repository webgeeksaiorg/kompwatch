40% of B2B SaaS companies with $10M+ ARR have removed public pricing from their websites.

Not because they're embarrassed. Because obscurity is a negotiation tactic.

When pricing isn't public, you anchor at whatever number their sales rep opens with. You can't run a comparison. You don't know if you're getting a fair deal.

This is also why Google Alerts is useless for tracking competitor pricing. If they don't publish pricing, there's nothing to alert on. You have to watch the page itself.

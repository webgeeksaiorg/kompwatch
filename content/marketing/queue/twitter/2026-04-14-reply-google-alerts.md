Google Alerts works great until you need to know what actually changed on their website — not just that they were mentioned somewhere.

It'll tell you Acme Corp posted a blog. It won't tell you Acme Corp quietly dropped their starter tier price by $20 and added a seat limit.

Those are different kinds of alerts.

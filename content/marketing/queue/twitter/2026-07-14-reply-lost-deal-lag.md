Nine times out of ten when I dig into a lost deal like this, there's a competitor change that happened 4-8 weeks before the call that nobody caught. Pricing, a feature update, a new case study. Something shifted and the sales team was still arguing against the old version.

The gap isn't the loss. The gap is the 6-week lag.

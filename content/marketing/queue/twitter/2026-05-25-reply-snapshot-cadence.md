Daily snapshots sound like overkill until the one week your competitor runs a pricing A/B test that lasts exactly 4 days.

We do daily by default, weekly digest. So you're not drowning in noise, but the raw data is there if something weird happened mid-week.

The cadence question is really a signal-to-noise tradeoff. More frequent = more signal AND more noise. We lean toward: capture daily, surface weekly unless it's a pricing/feature change (those get flagged immediately).

Google Alerts is great for news/PR mentions. Terrible for website changes.

It doesn't catch: pricing updates, feature page edits, new plan tiers, removed guarantees, changed CTAs.

Those are the things that actually affect your positioning. Alerts won't see them because they're not indexed as "news."

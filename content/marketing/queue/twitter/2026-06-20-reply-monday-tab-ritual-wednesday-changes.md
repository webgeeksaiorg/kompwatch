The Monday tab ritual is everywhere. Most PMs I've talked to have 4-8 competitor pages pinned.

The problem isn't the checking. It's that competitors change things on Wednesday at 4pm. By Monday you're a week behind on something that happened 5 days ago.

Monitoring needs to run continuously, not weekly.

Visualping works great until your competitor's site runs on React.

Then you're snapshotting the shell HTML before the framework renders. Empty div. No change detected.

It's not Visualping's fault exactly — it's a hard problem. But "works until it doesn't" is a real limitation if you're monitoring modern SaaS sites.

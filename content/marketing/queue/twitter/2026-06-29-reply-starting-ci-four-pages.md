If you're starting from scratch, I'd ignore 90% of what's written about "building a CI program."

Start with four pages per competitor. Just four:
1. Pricing page
2. Changelog or release notes
3. Careers page
4. Homepage (for positioning shifts, not news)

Check once a day. Take notes when something changes. After 30 days you'll know which signals actually matter for your market.

Then automate that. Not before.

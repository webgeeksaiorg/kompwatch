The alert is easy. The problem is you get 40 alerts a week and 37 are noise. "Competitor changed footer copyright year." Cool.

The thing worth building is the AI layer that reads the diffs and tells you: "Their pricing page added an annual discount. Their features page added 'API access.' Their jobs board just listed 3 ML engineers." That's the digest worth reading.

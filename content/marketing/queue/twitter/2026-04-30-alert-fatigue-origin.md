Built my own competitor monitor in 2023. Playwright + cron + email.

Worked great for 11 days.

Then: 40 alerts a day. Rotating hero images. A/B test variants. Cookie banners. I couldn't tell signal from noise so I turned it off and went back to manual tab-checking.

The noise problem is why I built the AI digest layer before anything else.

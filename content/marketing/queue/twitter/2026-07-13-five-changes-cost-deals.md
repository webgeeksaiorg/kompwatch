5 competitor changes that actually cost deals (not all of them are pricing):

1. Pricing page restructure — new tier appears, looks cheaper
2. Feature added to free plan — erodes your differentiation
3. "Now available" on a feature you said they didn't have
4. Case study from a customer in your exact vertical
5. Removed a plan that your prospect used to compare against you

Only #1 shows up in Google Alerts. The other four you find out from a lost deal.

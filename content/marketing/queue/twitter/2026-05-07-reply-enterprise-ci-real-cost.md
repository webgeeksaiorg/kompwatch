The software cost is the smaller number.

$25K/year for Klue. Call it $2K/month. Your CI analyst to run it: $80-100K/year fully loaded. The platform only works if someone owns it full-time.

Most teams discover this around month four when the battlecards haven't been updated in six weeks and the platform is technically active but practically ignored.

It's not a bad platform. It's a system that requires a function to operate. Budget for the function first.

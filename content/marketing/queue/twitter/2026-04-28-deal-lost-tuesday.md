The most avoidable competitive loss:

You're in a deal. Prospect says "your competitor just dropped to $X — can you match?"

You had no idea. They changed their pricing page last Tuesday. You found out on a Thursday sales call.

That's not a pricing problem. That's a monitoring problem.

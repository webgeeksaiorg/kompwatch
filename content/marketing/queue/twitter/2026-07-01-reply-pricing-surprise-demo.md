Sales reps finding out about a competitor's price change during a live demo is genuinely one of the worst things that can happen.

You're surprised. They know you're surprised. You either bluff or stumble through explaining why yours is still better at the now-higher relative price.

Pricing alerts that fire same-day exist specifically to prevent this.

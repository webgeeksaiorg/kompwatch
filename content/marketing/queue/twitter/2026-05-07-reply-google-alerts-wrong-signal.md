Google Alerts fires on news coverage of your competitor. Funding round. Acquisition announcement. Conference mention. Press release they wrote themselves.

It misses: when their pricing page changes. When they quietly remove the free tier. When the feature comparison table they've been using against you gets rewritten.

Different signal entirely. Not a limitation to work around — wrong tool for the job.

100%. Pricing pages change more often than you'd think, and almost never get announced.

I've seen: stealth price increases ($99 → $129 with no email), feature gates moved (thing that was free now paywalled), plan renaming to obscure the real cost.

All of it matters for positioning. None of it shows up in their changelog.

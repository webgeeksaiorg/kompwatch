I did this exact spreadsheet approach for about 8 months.

The moment it breaks down: when your team is 4 people and you're the only one who remembers to update it. Then you're away for a week. The spreadsheet becomes archaeology, not intelligence.

Automation doesn't just save time. It removes the single point of failure (you).

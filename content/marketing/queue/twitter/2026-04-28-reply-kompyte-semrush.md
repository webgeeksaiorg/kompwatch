Kompyte is fine if you're already inside the Semrush ecosystem and $300/mo feels reasonable. If you're a small team and you're not — you're paying for a lot of surface area you don't need. Most of what teams actually use it for is watching a handful of competitor pages for changes. That's a $49/mo problem, not a $300/mo one.

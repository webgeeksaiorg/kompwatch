The acquisition playbook for a tool like Kompyte:

Year 1 — "the roadmap is intact, we're investing"
Year 2 — pricing "adjusts upward" as it gets bundled
Year 3 — standalone product either sunsets or becomes the enterprise-only SKU at 3x the price

The tell: how fast does the self-serve tier disappear from the pricing page?

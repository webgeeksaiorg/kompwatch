Crayon's pitch: "never miss a competitor move."

Their problem: you get 300 "moves" a week. Pricing tweak, footer change, blog post, press release, job listing in a city you don't care about.

Nobody reads all 300. So you read zero.

"Never miss" only works if the tool knows which moves matter.

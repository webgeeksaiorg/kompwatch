Free options that actually work:

- Visualping free tier: 5 pages, 24hr check frequency, screenshot diffs
- ChangeDetection.io: open source, self-host if you're comfortable with that
- Manual + Notion: URL list + weekly 30min review. More work, full control.

The honest answer: free works until you're tracking >5 competitors or need the changes summarized for a team.

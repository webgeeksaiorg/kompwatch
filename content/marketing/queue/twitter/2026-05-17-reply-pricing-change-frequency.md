Counterintuitive thing about watching competitor pricing pages:

The *frequency* of changes tells you more than the changes themselves. A competitor who hasn't touched their pricing page in 14 months is either confident or frozen. One who's changed it 4 times this quarter is actively experimenting.

Pattern recognition over time. A single snapshot means nothing.

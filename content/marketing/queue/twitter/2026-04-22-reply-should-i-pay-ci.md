Depends what question you're trying to answer.

"What changed on competitor websites / pricing pages?" — you don't need $28K/year for that.

"Why are we losing deals? What do reps say in sales calls?" — that's a different product category. Crayon and Klue are genuinely built for it.

Know which problem you have before you buy.

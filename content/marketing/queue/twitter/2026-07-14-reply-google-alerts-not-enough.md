Google Alerts tracks mentions of a brand name across the web. News articles, review sites, blog posts. Useful for PR stuff.

It doesn't watch websites. If your competitor changes their pricing page, their features list, their case studies — you won't hear a word from Google Alerts.

Those are the changes that actually affect deals.

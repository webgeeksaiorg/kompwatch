How far in advance did you know the competitor had moved pricing?

I keep hearing this pattern: sales team finds out about competitor price drops during the deal, not before it. By then there's nothing to do — you're defending instead of positioning.

The signal is available. Competitor pricing pages are public. The gap is just that nobody's watching them consistently.

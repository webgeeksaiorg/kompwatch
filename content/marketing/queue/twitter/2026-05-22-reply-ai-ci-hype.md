The AI layer is useful. The problem is most CI tools use it to generate MORE content — longer summaries, more analysis, more battlecard suggestions.

What actually helps: using AI to filter down to less. "What genuinely changed that a PM would care about?" is a much harder prompt than "summarize these 40 website diffs."

Signal reduction > signal amplification.

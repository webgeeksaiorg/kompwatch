Same problem I had. Here's what actually works without a dedicated CI person:

1. Pick 3-5 competitors max. Not 15.
2. Watch 2-3 pages each: pricing, features, homepage CTA.
3. Set up automated monitoring so you get alerts, not a Monday task.
4. Review the digest once a week, not daily.

The goal is to stop it from being a job. Make it a weekly read.

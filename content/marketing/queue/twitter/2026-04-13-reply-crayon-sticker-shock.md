Yeah the first time you see a Crayon quote it's a bit of a moment. $15-30K/yr minimum, annual contract, and then they ask how many seats you need.

Worth asking: do you actually need *intelligence* (battlecards, Salesforce sync, analyst workflows) or do you just need *monitoring* (when did something change and what was it)?

Those are two different products at two very different price points.

The "$300/mo starting" thing is worth doing the math on.

When Semrush acquired Kompyte in 2022, the filing disclosed avg ARR per customer of ~$20K. That's $1,667/mo.

So "$300/mo starting" means starting. Before you hit competitor limits, user seat limits, or need any integration.

Not saying the tool doesn't work. Just — the starting price is the foot in the door.

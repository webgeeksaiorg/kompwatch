The pattern I keep hearing: company buys Crayon or Klue, uses it intensely for 60 days, then it slowly becomes an RSS feed that nobody checks.

Usually because the analyst who championed it left, or the alerts got noisy, or the battlecards went stale and nobody updated them.

The tool survives renewal because nobody wants to admit sunk cost. This is the most common CI story.

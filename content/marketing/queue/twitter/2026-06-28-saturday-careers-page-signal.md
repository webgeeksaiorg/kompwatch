Something I check on competitors once a week that most people don't: the careers page.

Not because I care about their hiring. Because job listings are the only place companies accidentally reveal their roadmap in public.

"Enterprise Security Engineer" — compliance push incoming.
"Head of Channel Partnerships" — going indirect.
"ML Platform Engineer x3" — they're building something.

Free signal. Nobody's watching.

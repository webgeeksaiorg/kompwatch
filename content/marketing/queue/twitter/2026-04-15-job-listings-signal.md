The underrated competitive signal: job listings.

Your competitor posts 4 ML engineering roles in January. By Q3 they ship an AI feature. The hiring was the tell.

Most teams find out about the feature at launch. Some teams found out 6 months earlier.

Job listing monitoring is in the CompeteWatch backlog. But you don't need a tool — just check their jobs page every 2 weeks and log the roles. Takes 10 minutes. Most teams don't bother.

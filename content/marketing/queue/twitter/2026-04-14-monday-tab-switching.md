Monday mornings used to mean 45 minutes of tab-switching.

12 competitor websites. Ctrl+F for "pricing." Sometimes "pricing" wasn't even on the page anymore — they'd changed the nav.

Half the time nothing changed. Half the time something changed on Wednesday and I caught it the following Monday.

I wrote a cron job to do it instead. Then added AI to summarize what actually changed. Then a friend said "I'd pay for that."

That's the whole origin story.

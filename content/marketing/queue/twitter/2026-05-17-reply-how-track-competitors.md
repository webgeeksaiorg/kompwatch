Depends on budget and what "tracking" means to you.

If you care about website/pricing/feature changes: set up a monitor on their key pages. Beats checking manually on Mondays.

If you want sales battlecards and CI for a whole org: Klue/Crayon territory, but $16K+/yr minimum.

The gap between "free but manual" and "enterprise" is real. That's the whole reason I built KompWatch.

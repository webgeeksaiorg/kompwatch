Pricing pages are the most valuable thing to watch. They change quietly — no blog post, no announcement, just a different number on a Tuesday afternoon.

42% of SaaS companies adjusted pricing in H1 2024. Almost none announced it.

The catch: most monitoring tools can't read them if they're React-rendered. You need headless browser execution, not raw HTTP.

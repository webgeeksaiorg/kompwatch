Most CI tools implicitly assume you have a dedicated analyst. The templates, the playbooks, the win/loss tagging — all of that requires someone whose job it is.

Under 50 people, that role usually doesn't exist. The PM owns it alongside everything else.

Which means the tool needs to do more of the work, not less. "Here's what changed this week, here's what it means" — not "here's a platform for your analyst to build battlecards in."

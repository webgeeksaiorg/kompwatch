The most underread competitor page: the changelog.

Everyone monitors the pricing page. Half check the homepage. Almost nobody reads the changelog.

That's where the actual roadmap leaks. What they shipped. What they deprecated. What they called "beta" six months ago that's now "GA."

If a competitor shipped 12 changelog entries in a month, they're in full execution mode. Three entries? Either maintaining or building something they're not talking about yet.

The changelog is where strategy hides in plain sight.

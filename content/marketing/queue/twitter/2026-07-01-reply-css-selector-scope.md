The move that cuts 80% of competitor monitoring noise:

CSS selector scoping. Instead of watching the entire pricing page, watch only the pricing table div.

`#pricing-table` catches tier/price changes.
`.hero-headline` catches positioning changes.

Ignores the cookie banner, nav updates, rotating testimonials, and every footer link change.

The moment a competitor changes something relevant, three people need to know:

1. The AE with an open deal in that vertical
2. The PM building the roadmap
3. The person maintaining the battlecard

That's the whole workflow. Not a quarterly strategy review. Just: change detected → right person notified immediately.

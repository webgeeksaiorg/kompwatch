The 90-day pattern is real. You set up the CI tool, alerts come in, week 3 you mute most of them, by month 6 it's a line item nobody questions because cancelling feels like admitting you didn't need it.

Enterprise CI is designed for a full-time analyst. That's not a product failure — it's what the product is for.

Without someone whose job is to curate the feed, you need a tool that does the filtering before it hits your inbox. Different product.

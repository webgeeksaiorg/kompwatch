At most small SaaS companies CI looks like: one person's Monday morning ritual, Google Alerts, and "did anyone see that competitor launched X?"

The enterprise tools (Crayon, Klue) assume a dedicated analyst and $20K budget. Most teams are somewhere below that.

That's the space we're building into with kompwatch.com — track the pages that matter, get a summary when they change.

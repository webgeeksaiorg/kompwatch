Google Alerts fires when someone writes *about* your competitor. That's reputation monitoring.

What you actually want: alerts when your competitor changes their own website. Pricing page, features page, homepage copy. The stuff that tells you what they're actually doing.

Different problem, different tool. We built KompWatch for the second thing.

"CI program" implies a full department. Most startups just need to know when their top 5 competitors change something important.

That's not a program. That's a Tuesday morning email.

The tooling should match the problem. Crayon is for teams that have a dedicated CI function. If you don't, you're paying for infrastructure you'll never use.

"Who owns competitive intelligence at your company?" is always a funny question.

At Series A: whoever cares most. Usually one PM who set up a Notion page.

At Series B: technically product, but sales thinks they own it, marketing is also doing something.

At Series C: dedicated PMM role, $500/mo Crayon contract, 15 people with access, 3 active users.

The tool problem comes after the process problem.

If you're just starting out tracking competitors, 5 pages will tell you 80% of what you need:

1. Pricing page
2. Features/product page  
3. Careers page (what they're hiring = what they're building)
4. Changelog / release notes
5. Homepage headline

The blog and press releases are mostly noise.

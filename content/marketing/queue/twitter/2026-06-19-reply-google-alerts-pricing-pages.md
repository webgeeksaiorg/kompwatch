Google Alerts for competitor pricing pages specifically: doesn't work.

Most modern SaaS pricing is a React component. Google crawls the static HTML, which is basically empty. The actual prices are injected client-side.

So you get alerts about competitor blog posts and press releases. Never about the pricing change that matters.

The set-and-forget CI stack usually isn't.

Someone has to triage alerts, review noise, update selectors when a competitor redesigns. Week 1: exciting. Week 8: calendar event nobody shows up to.

We send an email when something changes. One notification, one changed thing. No maintenance mode.

The spreadsheet approach works until it doesn't.

It breaks the moment someone leaves the company and the institutional knowledge goes with them. Or a competitor ships something major on a Thursday and nobody checks until Monday.

The thing that actually works: one source of truth that emails you when something changes, with enough context that you don't need to click through to understand it. That's it. Not a dashboard. Not a feed. An email you actually read.

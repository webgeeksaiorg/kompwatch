I did exactly this for two years. Six browser tabs pinned, checked every Monday, missed a pricing change every few weeks.

The thing that finally fixed it: automated snapshots + AI diff summary. You get an email that says "Competitor X changed their pricing page — here's what moved." Takes 30 seconds to read instead of 30 minutes to check.

Job listings are one signal. But their *website changes* tell you what already shipped, not just what they're planning.

New feature callout on the homepage: shipped. New pricing tier: live. New "Enterprise" page: moving upmarket.

We monitor the pages directly. Job boards tell you what's coming in 6 months. Website changes tell you what happened last Tuesday.

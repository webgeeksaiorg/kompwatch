I did this for 18 months. 6 browser tabs pinned, every Monday morning, before standup.

Half the time I'd miss a change that happened Wednesday. The other half I'd spend 45 minutes reading competitor blogs that hadn't changed anything meaningful.

The real cost wasn't the time. It was the false sense of coverage. You check 6 URLs and feel like you're on top of it. You're not.

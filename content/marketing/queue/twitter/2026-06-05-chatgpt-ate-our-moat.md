Klue's CEO said customers are asking why they should pay for CI software when they can just ask ChatGPT.

That's not a marketing problem. That's a product problem.

If your core value is "we read competitor content and summarize it," you're done. That's $0/month now.

What's NOT done: knowing when a competitor's pricing page changes at 2am. Catching the JavaScript-rendered section that GPT can't see. Getting alerted when a competitor quietly removes a feature from their pricing tier.

Reading is free. Watching is not.

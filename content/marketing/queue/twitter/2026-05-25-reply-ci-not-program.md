You don't need a competitive intelligence "program." You need to know when something important changes.

Those are very different things. The first costs $20K/year and a PMM hire. The second is a list of URLs and an automated weekly email.

At 10 employees, you don't have the budget or headcount for the first. But you absolutely cannot afford to miss the second.

Alert fatigue from competitor monitoring has one fix: monitor fewer things.

Not: better filtering. Not: smarter routing. Fewer pages.

Pricing page. Features page. Job listings. Footer (new products show up there first).

That's it. Everything else is noise pretending to be signal.

If your CI tool is surfacing 20 alerts/day across 15 competitors: it's not monitoring too little, it's monitoring too much.

Counterintuitive take: track fewer competitors, but track them deeper.

I've seen teams monitoring 25 competitors with daily snapshots and getting nothing useful out of it. They're getting alerts constantly and ignoring them all.

Five competitors. Four pages each. Daily snapshot. AI summary in plain English. That's actually actionable.

The goal isn't comprehensive competitive awareness. It's: when something actually matters, you knew within 24 hours. Depth beats breadth here.

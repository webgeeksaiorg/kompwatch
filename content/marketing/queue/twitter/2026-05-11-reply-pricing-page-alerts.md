Most "competitor monitoring" tools track news and SEO metrics. They miss the actual pricing page.

What you want: something targeting the HTML on /pricing specifically, diffing it against last week, surfacing only the pricing table — not the entire page. CSS selector targeting is the difference between "a change happened somewhere on this 4,000-word page" and "the Pro tier went from $99 to $129."

The specificity is what makes alerts actionable instead of noise.

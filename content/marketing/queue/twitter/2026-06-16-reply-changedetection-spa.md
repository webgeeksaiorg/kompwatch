changedetection.io works fine for static sites. Breaks on most modern SaaS sites because it doesn't execute JavaScript.

Playwright (headless Chromium) is the only reliable way to capture rendered HTML from React/Next.js/Vue apps. Pain to self-host, but actually works. The other option is you keep missing changes on pages that matter.

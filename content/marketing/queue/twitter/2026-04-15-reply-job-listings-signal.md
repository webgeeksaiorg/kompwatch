Job listings are the most underrated CI signal.

Your competitor's pricing page tells you what they're charging now. Their job listings tell you where they're going.

Four ML engineer postings = AI roadmap pivot, 6-9 months out. One "Head of Enterprise Sales" listing = moving upmarket. "Senior Security Engineer" = SOC2 push incoming.

These signals show up weeks or months before any product announcement. And they're completely public.

Worth tracking the jobs page alongside the pricing page. Different signal, but often higher value.

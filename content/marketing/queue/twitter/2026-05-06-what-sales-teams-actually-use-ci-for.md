Most sales teams use their "competitive intelligence platform" for two things:

1. Looking up a competitor before a call
2. Sending battlecards they update twice a year

Everything else — alerts, integrations, AI summaries — gets set up once and ignored.

The tools are priced for the full stack. The usage is almost always just #1 and #2.

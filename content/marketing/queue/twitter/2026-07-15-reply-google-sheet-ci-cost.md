We used a shared Google Sheet for two years. One column per competitor, one row per week. Someone had to fill it out every Monday.

It worked until it didn't — which was the quarter we lost three deals to a competitor that had quietly changed their pricing model two months earlier.

Manual was fine. Until the cost showed up in the deal lost column.

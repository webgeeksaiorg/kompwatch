Ran the numbers on when competitor websites change most:

Tuesday–Thursday = 73% of all changes detected.

Friday: almost nothing.
Weekend: nearly zero.
Monday: normal.

Competitor product and pricing teams work like everyone else. Changes go through internal review Tuesday, get approved Wednesday, ship Wednesday afternoon or Thursday.

If you're doing a manual Monday morning check, you're seeing last week's state. The meaningful changes from the prior week happened after you closed your tabs.

When a CI tool gets acquired by a company 100x its size, two things happen to the SMB roadmap: it freezes during integration (12-24 months), or it pivots to serve the acquirer's existing enterprise customers.

Neither is good if you're not enterprise.

Worth checking your renewal date any time a vendor you depend on changes hands. The price rarely stays the same.

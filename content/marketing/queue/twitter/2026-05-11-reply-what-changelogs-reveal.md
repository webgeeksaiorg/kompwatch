Changelogs reveal three things about a competitor:

1. Shipping velocity — are they sprinting or quiet?
2. Strategic direction — what are they prioritizing?
3. Team health — consistent cadence vs. erratic bursts often maps to team stability

None of this shows up in their marketing page. It's all in the changelog.

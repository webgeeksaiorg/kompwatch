Google Alerts catches news *about* competitors — press releases, review articles, "Company X raises $Y" announcements.

It doesn't crawl their website. You'll miss actual page changes entirely.

I ran Alerts on my top 3 competitors for two years. Missed a competitor that quietly added a free tier. Found out from a lost deal, not the alert.

Use it for brand mentions. Not for website monitoring.

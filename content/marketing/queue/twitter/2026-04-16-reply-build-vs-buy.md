Build vs buy for this one is actually pretty interesting.

Build: 3 days minimum to get Playwright working on JS-heavy SPAs, another day for the diff logic, another for LLM summarization, then ongoing maintenance when sites change structure. Real cost is your time plus compute.

Buy: $49/mo, done in 10 minutes.

The math almost always says buy. But if you enjoy the build and learn something from it — totally valid reason to do it yourself. I did exactly that, then turned it into the product.

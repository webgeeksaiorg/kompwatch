Kompyte's ownership chain is now:
- 2022: Semrush acquires Kompyte
- Nov 2025: Adobe announces Semrush acquisition ($1.9B)
- Apr 2026: Deal closes

So your "affordable" mid-market CI tool is now three layers deep inside Adobe's enterprise stack.

The people building your product roadmap now report to someone who reports to someone at Adobe Digital Experience. Your feature request is not being prioritized.

If you're on a legacy Kompyte contract, asking your rep about migration costs before the forced transition is worth doing now.

Congrats to the HeadsUp team on the PH launch.

Worth clarifying for people comparing tools: HeadsUp is an AI agent that *researches and synthesizes* competitor info on demand. We're a monitoring tool that *watches specific pages and alerts you the moment something changes*.

If you need a quick competitor brief before a pitch — HeadsUp. If you need to know your competitor's pricing page changed at 2am on a Thursday — KompWatch.

Different jobs. Both useful.

The AI use case that actually works here isn't "generate a battlecard." It's: translate a noisy HTML diff into one sentence of plain English.

"Competitor changed their pricing page" tells you nothing. "Starter plan dropped from $29 to $19, annual discount added, free tier removed" is actionable.

The diff detection is the hard part. The LLM summary is almost embarrassingly straightforward once you have clean input.

Alert fatigue is a symptom, not the problem. The problem is unweighted volume.

When "competitor added a sentence to their about page" and "competitor just cut pricing 20%" land in the same inbox with the same formatting — you train yourself to ignore all of it.

Severity scoring. Not just filtering. Scoring. So you know in 2 seconds if this one matters.

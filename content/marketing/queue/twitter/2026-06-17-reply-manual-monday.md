I did the 6-tab Monday morning routine for 2 years. It's not the worst habit — at least you're doing it.

The problem is Wednesday. And Thursday. And the pricing change that happened at 4 AM Tuesday while you were sleeping.

Weekly manual checks mean you're consistently 5-7 days late on anything that happens mid-week.

The "weekly competitive digest" email is a trap.

It arrives Monday. Everyone's busy. They skim the subject line. Archive it. By Wednesday when they need the info, they've forgotten it existed.

Better: alerts that fire when something specific happens + a Slack message to the one person who needs to know.

Push, not pull.

Competitor posts 4 ML engineer roles in one month? Something is being built. They post a Head of Partnerships role the month before a big announcement? There's a deal coming.

We track job listing changes as part of competitor monitoring — it's in the same snapshot cadence as their pricing pages. One place, one digest.

Not perfect signal but it's usually weeks ahead of the press release.

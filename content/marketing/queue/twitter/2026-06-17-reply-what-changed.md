The raw HTML diff is unreadable. A pricing page has 4,000+ lines. A single word change produces 50 changed lines filled with ARIA attributes, class names, and data properties.

What you actually want: "competitor dropped Pro from $79 to $59 and removed the seat limit."

That translation step is what makes alerts actionable vs. noise.

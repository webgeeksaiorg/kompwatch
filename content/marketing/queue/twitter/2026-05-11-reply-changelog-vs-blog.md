Monitoring a competitor's blog misses most of what matters.

Blog: "We're excited to announce..." (marketing)
Changelog: "Removed free tier. Added 5-seat minimum. Changed overage pricing." (actual product decisions)

The changelog is honest in a way the blog never is. It's what they had to ship, not what they wanted to announce.

"Feature parity" is the worst frame for a competitive deal.

"We have everything they have plus X" means you're letting the competitor set the terms of the conversation. They defined what matters. You're just saying you have it too.

The teams that win competitive deals ask: what do we do that they can't do? What customer outcome is ours alone?

CI isn't just knowing what they added. It's knowing what they're still not building — and whether your roadmap owns that territory before they get there.

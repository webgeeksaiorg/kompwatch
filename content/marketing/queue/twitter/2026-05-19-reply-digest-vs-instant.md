Hot take: weekly digests are the wrong default for competitor monitoring.

Most changes don't need weekly. A blog post? Weekly is fine.

But a pricing page rewrite the night before a prospect renewal call? You don't want that in next Monday's email.

The right model is tiered cadence: PRICING → immediate. FEATURE → same day. BLOG/JOBS → weekly digest.

That's what we shipped this week. Digest is still there. But pricing now routes around it.

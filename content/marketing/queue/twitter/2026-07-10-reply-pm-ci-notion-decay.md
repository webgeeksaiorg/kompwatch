Most PM teams I talk to do CI in the worst possible way: one person owns a Notion doc, updates it when they remember, and it's 3 months stale when sales actually needs it.

The problem isn't that they don't care. It's that CI is manual and manual things decay.

Set up automated page monitoring for the 6 pages that actually change (pricing, features, home, changelog, careers, blog). Treat it like error alerting, not a research project.

Google Alerts is fine for brand monitoring. Terrible for competitor website changes.

It catches when they publish a blog post or get mentioned in press. Doesn't catch when they quietly update their pricing page on a Tuesday afternoon or change feature availability on the plan comparison table.

Those are the changes that actually affect your sales calls.

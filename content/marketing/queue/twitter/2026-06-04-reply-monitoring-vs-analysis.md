There's website monitoring (did this page change?) and competitive intelligence (what does this change mean for us?).

Klue and Crayon try to do the second. Most product teams I talk to just need the first — plus a plain-English summary so they don't have to read a diff.

Different products. One costs $40K/year. One costs $49/mo.

The variable most people don't ask about in CI tool evaluations: crawl frequency.

If the tool checks your competitors' pages every 7 days and you find out 4 days after a pricing change — you've lost a week of context for every sales conversation that week.

Frequency of monitoring is maybe the most underrated spec when comparing tools.

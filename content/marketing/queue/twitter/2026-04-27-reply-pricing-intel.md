Worth tracking: Vendr published data showing 72% of SaaS companies changed pricing in 2025.

That means 7 out of 10 of your competitors probably moved on price last year. If you found out by accident (a customer told you, you stumbled on it), you had no idea how long you'd been selling on stale information.

Pricing intel isn't a luxury for companies with CI teams. It's just... knowing the market.

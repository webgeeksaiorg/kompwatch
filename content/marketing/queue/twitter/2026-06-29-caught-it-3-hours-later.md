There's a world of difference between catching a competitor pricing change 3 hours after it goes live vs. 3 weeks later.

3 hours: you update your deck, brief your AE, nail the next call.

3 weeks: you find out because a prospect mentions it mid-demo. "Oh, their new price? Yeah we saw that." You didn't.

The monitoring part is easy. The frequency part is the actual product.

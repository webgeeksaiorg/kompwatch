Start with 5 URLs. Not 50.

Competitor pricing page. Competitor features page. Competitor blog (for the weekly post dump that signals what they're about to pitch). Hiring page. Changelog if they have one.

Set up monitoring on those 5 first. Get comfortable with the cadence. Understand which changes actually matter for your deals.

Then expand. Most teams track too many things and action nothing because it's noise.

The timing problem is underrated. Battlecards are stale the day you publish them. By the time your enablement team updates the deck, the sales rep already lost the deal citing outdated positioning.

The only way to fix this is shortening the feedback loop between "competitor changed something" and "rep knows about it." Most CI tools don't actually solve that — they just make the stale intel look nicer.

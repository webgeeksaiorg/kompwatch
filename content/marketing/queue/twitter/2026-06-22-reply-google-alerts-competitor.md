Google Alerts works fine for press releases and brand mentions.

For pricing page changes? It'll never see them — modern SaaS pages are JavaScript-rendered SPAs. Alerts crawls the HTML shell, which is just `<div id="root"></div>` and a script tag. The actual content is invisible to it.

Different tools for different things. Alerts for news. Something with a real browser for website changes.

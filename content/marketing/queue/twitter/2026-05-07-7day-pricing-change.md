Median competitor pricing page changes every 7 days.

If you're doing a weekly manual check, you're already late half the time. If it's biweekly, you're catching maybe a quarter of changes near their actual date.

Nobody sets pricing strategy around "whenever Stive opens a browser tab on Monday." They set it around their fiscal quarter, their new feature launch, their competitor's move.

Monitoring on a schedule you control doesn't match the schedule they're changing on.

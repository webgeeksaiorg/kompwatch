If your CI tool requires a dedicated analyst to extract value from it — the real cost isn't the software license.

It's the license ($20K–$100K) plus a headcount ($80K–$120K) plus management overhead.

That math only works at a certain scale. Below that scale you're paying for a product that wasn't built for you.

Battlecard staleness is a solved problem if you treat it as a data problem instead of a content problem.

Your battlecard is only as good as your last competitor check. Most teams check manually. Monthly, maybe.

Competitors change pricing quarterly on average. Sometimes more.

The math doesn't work.

G2 review changes are some of the highest-signal intel you can get.

Your competitor quietly addresses a complaint → new reviews mention it → they're building against their biggest weakness. That's a roadmap signal.

They start getting slammed for pricing → watch for a new free tier or a pivot. Usually happens 60-90 days after the review pattern shows up.

Most teams don't track this because it's tedious to check manually. Automated now in KompWatch.

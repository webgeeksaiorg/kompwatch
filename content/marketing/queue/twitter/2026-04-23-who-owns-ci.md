Before buying any CI tool, ask: who owns this at your company?

If the answer is "we'd figure that out" — you won't get value from Crayon or Klue. Those tools need 8-15 hours/week of dedicated curation to generate ROI.

Not a criticism. An honest description of what the tool requires.

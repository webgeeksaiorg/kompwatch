At 5-10 people you don't need a competitive intelligence program. You need three things:

1. Know when your main competitors change pricing (automated)
2. Know when they ship something that overlaps your roadmap (changelog/blog RSS)
3. Know what their unhappy customers are complaining about (Reddit/G2 alerts)

That's genuinely enough to stay informed without a $30K platform and a dedicated analyst. The fancy stuff — battlecards, Salesforce sync, win/loss tracking — comes later, when you have a sales team who'll actually use it.

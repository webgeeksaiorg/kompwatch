Competitor job listings are the most underrated CI signal.

A pricing page tells you where they are. Job listings tell you where they're going.

Four ML engineer postings → something AI is shipping in 6-9 months. "Head of Enterprise Sales" → they're moving upmarket. "Senior Security Engineer" → they're going after compliance-heavy buyers.

Your competitor's LinkedIn is a roadmap preview. Most people aren't reading it.

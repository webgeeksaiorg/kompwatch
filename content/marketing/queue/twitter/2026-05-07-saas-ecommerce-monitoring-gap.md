Search "how to track competitor pricing."

First page: Wiser, Prisync, Competera. All built for ecommerce retailers tracking SKU prices on Amazon and Shopify.

The SaaS version of this problem is completely different. Your competitor's pricing page is a React app. It doesn't have a SKU. It changes when someone updates a Webflow block at 11pm on a Tuesday.

Ecommerce price trackers do not solve this. Almost nothing does it well yet.

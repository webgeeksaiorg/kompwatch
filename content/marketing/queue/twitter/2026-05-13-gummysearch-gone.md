GummySearch shut down in December.

If you used it to track competitor mentions on Reddit, you lost your best tool with two weeks notice.

Reddit API restrictions made the economics impossible. That's the honest answer.

I've been doing it the old way since: keyword alerts + manual searches on r/SaaS, r/startups, r/ProductManagement. Slower. But I've found more real signal in the actual comments than any aggregator showed me anyway.

Sometimes the tool disappearing is the reminder that you needed to be in the room yourself.

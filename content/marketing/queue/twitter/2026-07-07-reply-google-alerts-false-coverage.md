The CI workflow I see most often at small SaaS companies:

1. Someone set up Google Alerts 2 years ago
2. The alerts go to a Slack channel
3. The Slack channel has 12 people and zero engagement
4. Nobody knows if the alerts are still firing

That's not a workflow. That's a false sense of coverage.

Real CI: specific pages, triggered on actual change, goes to one person who owns it.

Job listings are wildly underused as competitive intel. Three ML engineer openings means a model feature is 6 months out. A "Director of Partnerships" posting means they're going channel. A cluster of support hires means scale issues.

The catch: you have to check consistently. Most teams mean to do this and don't.

Automating a watch on their careers page is probably the highest-ROI thing you can set up in 20 minutes.

The template isn't the problem. Every battlecard I've seen was fine when it was written.

The problem is the update trigger. When did your competitor change pricing? When did they ship that new feature you've been differentiating against?

Without automated detection, you're updating on vibes. Or after a sales rep loses a deal and mentions it in Slack.

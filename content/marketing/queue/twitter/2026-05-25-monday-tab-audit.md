Every Monday I used to open the same 6 tabs:

1. Competitor A pricing page
2. Competitor B features page
3. Competitor C blog
4. Competitor D changelog
5. Competitor E jobs page
6. LinkedIn to see if anyone moved

Then I'd scan all of them manually. Took 40 minutes. Most weeks nothing changed. Occasionally I'd miss something that happened on a Wednesday.

The problem isn't monitoring. It's that humans make bad cron jobs.

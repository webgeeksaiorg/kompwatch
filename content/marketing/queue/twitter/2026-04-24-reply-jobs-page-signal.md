Competitor jobs pages are underrated.

Six ML engineer postings in two weeks = they're building something. Suddenly stopped hiring for sales = something changed with their pipeline. Opened a London office = new market push.

Most people check pricing. Fewer check hiring. The hiring page sometimes tells you more.

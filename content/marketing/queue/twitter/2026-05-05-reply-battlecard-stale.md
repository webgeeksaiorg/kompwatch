The battlecard problem is almost always a data freshness problem, not a format problem.

Teams spend months perfecting the template and one afternoon a quarter actually updating it. Meanwhile the competitor changed pricing twice and shipped a feature that directly answers the top objection.

Automation doesn't write the battlecard. It just makes sure you know what changed before the customer tells you.

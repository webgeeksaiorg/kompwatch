If you're on Klue and wondering what those layoffs mean for your contract — probably nothing this quarter. Renewals don't change overnight.

But I'd start mapping what you'd do if the product pivoted hard or got folded into something else.

Most of what Klue does for smaller teams is: scrape competitor websites, summarize changes, deliver a digest. That layer got commoditized. $49/mo territory now.

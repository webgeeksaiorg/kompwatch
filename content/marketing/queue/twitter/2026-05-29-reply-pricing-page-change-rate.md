Something worth knowing: SaaS pricing pages change every 2-3 days on average.

Not major rewrites — pricing tier names, feature inclusions, call-to-action text, trial length. The stuff that actually affects your positioning conversations.

Most teams look at competitor pricing quarterly. That's ~60 changes you missed.

Just something to factor in when you're writing your competitive battlecards.

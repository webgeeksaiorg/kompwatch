Just shipped a founding customer lock: first 20 paying users get $29/mo Pro for life.

Normal reaction to that decision: "but you're leaving money on the table."

My actual math: if the first 20 people are still paying in year 3, I've won. Churn tells you everything about whether the product works. Retention is the proof, not the conversion.

$29/mo locked forever is cheap to buy that proof.

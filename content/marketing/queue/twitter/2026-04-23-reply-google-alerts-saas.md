Google Alerts is fine for brand mentions. For competitor website changes it has three problems:

1. 24+ hour delay
2. Misses Reddit, LinkedIn, pricing page updates
3. Returns news articles about your competitor, not changes to their actual product

Different tool for a different job. Not a knock on Alerts — it just wasn't built for this.

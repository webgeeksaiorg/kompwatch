Every Crayon and Klue user I've talked to has the same complaint: too many alerts.

Pricing changes, blog posts, LinkedIn updates, press releases, job listings, Twitter activity. All of it flows in, unranked, undifferentiated.

The "intelligence" part requires a human to read and filter everything.

That's not competitive intelligence. That's noise with a $15K/year subscription attached.

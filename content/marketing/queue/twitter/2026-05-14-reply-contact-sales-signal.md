"Contact sales" on a pricing page isn't a pricing model. It's a filter.

They're filtering out everyone who isn't ready to spend $20K+. Which is most teams.

The CI market has always had this. Crayon, Klue — both built for companies that can afford a dedicated analyst. The small team market was genuinely underserved until recently.

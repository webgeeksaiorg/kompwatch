We had 6 browser tabs pinned. Checked them every Monday. Missed the week our main competitor dropped their Pro plan price by $20 and started pulling our trial users.

Found out 3 weeks later when we noticed the conversion dip and dug in.

That's what actually built KompWatch. It literally just watches the URLs you care about and emails you when they change.

The noise problem is real. First-gen website monitoring tools flag everything — rotating testimonials, cookie banner tweaks, footer copyright year. You get 40 alerts a week and start ignoring all of them.

The useful signal is: pricing changed, new feature launched, a page section appeared or disappeared. AI filtering actually helps here. Not "AI" as a buzzword — it's just: can you tell me if this change is significant or not?

Most teams don't need more data. They need less noise.

Google Alerts catches when someone writes *about* your competitor. Press releases, blog posts, news mentions.

It does not catch when the competitor quietly rewrites their pricing page, drops a feature tier, or changes their headline copy.

Those are the changes that actually matter in a sales call. And they happen with zero announcement.

Two totally different signals. Worth having both.

The step people skip: establishing a baseline before you start.

When a change alert fires, you want to know "changed from what?" Most tools compare current vs. previous check — no historical context for what things looked like when you started.

Snapshot every competitor page intentionally on day one. Future alerts show change relative to that baseline, not just "it looks different from yesterday."

A competitor removing their free tier is a bigger signal than shipping a new feature.

It means they've decided free users don't convert to paid. Or they got pushed by investors to focus on enterprise. Or CAC on free was killing them.

Any of those things changes how you position against them. But you only know it if you noticed when the free tier disappeared.

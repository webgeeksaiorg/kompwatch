The #competitive-intel Slack channel graveyard is real. Every tool promises to "keep your team informed" and then your team mutes the channel after two weeks.

The fix isn't more filtering options. It's a different mental model: weekly digest > real-time firehose. One email on Monday > 30 Slack messages a day.

People actually read the digest.

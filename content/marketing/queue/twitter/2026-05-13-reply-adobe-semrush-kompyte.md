The acquisition chain is what I'd be watching.

Semrush bought Kompyte. Adobe bought Semrush in February. That's two layers of enterprise acquisition, and the roadmap will follow Adobe's priorities — not SMB competitive intelligence.

It's not that they'll kill it. It's that the next 18 months of product decisions will be made by people whose incentives are pointing somewhere else.

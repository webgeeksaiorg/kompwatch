The thing nobody tells you: competitor activity has rhythms.

Big product push in Q1 → quiet Q2 → pricing change in Q3 right before their renewal cycle. Once you can see 6 months of change history in a grid, it's obvious.

An email alert per change doesn't let you see the shape. You need the timeline.

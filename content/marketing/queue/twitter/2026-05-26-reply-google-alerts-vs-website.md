There's a predictable pattern:

Week 1: you set up Google Alerts for your 3 main competitors.
Week 3: alerts are noisy, you start ignoring them.
Month 2: a competitor drops their free tier. You find out on a prospect call.

The problem isn't that Google Alerts doesn't work. It's that it monitors the wrong thing. It catches content *about* your competitor. Not changes *to* their website.

Two different signals. Neither replaces the other.

Klue acquired Ignition in September 2025.

Two enterprise CI platforms merging means: integration work, product roadmap disruption, and usually a pricing reset once the dust settles.

If you're evaluating CI tools right now, factor in acquisition risk. Both Crayon (SoftwareOne, $1.4B) and Klue (Ignition acquisition) have significant ownership/structure changes in the last 12 months.

That's not necessarily bad. But it's a variable worth knowing about before you sign a multi-year contract.

I evaluated 4 competitive intelligence tools before building my own. Every single one:

- Required a demo call
- Wouldn't quote pricing until call 2
- Had a feature I needed locked behind "Enterprise"

There's a real gap in the market for tools that just let you sign up and start watching competitors. No deck. No 30-day "onboarding."

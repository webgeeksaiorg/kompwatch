Every "best competitor price tracking" roundup defaults to Prisync, Pricefy, PriceMole.

Those are e-commerce repricing tools. They track Amazon SKU prices.

If you're a SaaS company trying to know when a competitor changes their pricing page, that's a completely different problem — and almost no one is writing about the SaaS-specific version.

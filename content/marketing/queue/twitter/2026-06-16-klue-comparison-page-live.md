Just shipped a Klue vs KompWatch comparison page.

Not a puff piece. Actual differences:

Klue: battlecards, Salesforce integration, win/loss analysis, dedicated CI analyst assumed, $15-40K/yr
KompWatch: watches competitor websites, tells you what changed, $49/mo

Different products for different teams. If you need battlecards, we're not it. If you need "what did my competitor's pricing page change to this week" — that's us.

I built the first version of this myself — cron job that diffed HTML and emailed me the diff.

Took two weekends. Worked fine until my competitors started using React. Then it caught nothing because the DOM was rendering client-side.

SPA support, screenshot capture, AI summaries to filter noise, Slack delivery — that's 3 months of work if you're doing it properly.

Worth building if CI is your product. If it's your workflow, just buy something.

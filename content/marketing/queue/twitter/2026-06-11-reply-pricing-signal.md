Pricing page changes are the most underrated competitive signal.

Switching from per-seat to usage-based: strategic shift in who they're targeting.
Removing a tier: they're going upmarket or killing a low-margin product line.
Adding an annual discount: they're under churn pressure.

These changes are all publicly visible and almost nobody is systematically watching for them. Manual checks miss them. Google Alerts don't catch them. You only find out when a prospect mentions it on a call.

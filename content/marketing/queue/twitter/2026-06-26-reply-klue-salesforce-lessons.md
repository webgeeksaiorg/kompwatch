The Klue / Salesforce incident is a good reminder that "competitive intelligence platform" is a loaded phrase from a data security perspective.

You're storing: which competitors you're tracking, what you think about them, how you position against them, your win/loss analysis.

That's your strategy. In someone else's database. Worth knowing exactly what security posture you're relying on.

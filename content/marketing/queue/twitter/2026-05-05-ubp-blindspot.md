Usage-based pricing has made competitor monitoring harder.

Used to be: "Pro plan is $99/mo for 5 seats." Stable. Easy to track.

Now: hybrid models, consumption tiers, overage caps, graduated pricing, usage minimums. Pricing pages get restructured quarterly.

Basic screenshot diff tells you something changed. Doesn't tell you what. That's where the AI layer actually earns its place.

GummySearch shut down in December. Reddit killed their API access.

Thousands of indie hackers lost their main tool for monitoring what people said about their competitors on Reddit.

There's still no great replacement for the Reddit-monitoring piece. But for tracking *competitor websites* — pricing pages, feature pages, job listings — the stack that works: Playwright + CSS selectors + LLM summarization.

That's exactly what we built. Just noting it exists.

The worst part about competitor pricing changes: they don't send you an email.

No announcement. No press release. The page just looks different on a Tuesday.

Google Alerts won't catch it (wrong signal type — that's mentions, not page changes). Quarterly manual reviews miss it entirely.

Most teams find out on a sales call. At which point it's already cost them deals.

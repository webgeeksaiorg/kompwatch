Klue laid off 40% of staff in June 2025. Then acquired Ignition in September 2025.

That sequence tells you everything about where enterprise CI is heading: fewer humans, more AI agents doing the "reading competitor content" work.

The bet is: if you can't compete on content summarization anymore (because GPT-4 does it free), compete on agentic workflows and sales enablement.

Might work. Might not. But the old model — "pay us $30K/year to read things for you" — is clearly done.

Small teams were never the target market anyway. We are now.

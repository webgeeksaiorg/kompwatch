Google Alerts for pricing pages specifically: useless.

It only fires when Google re-indexes the page. Competitor can change their prices on Tuesday. You won't know until the next crawl — could be 2 weeks, could be 6.

For blog posts and press releases it's fine. For pricing, feature, and product pages: you need something that checks on a schedule you control.

"A CI platform without a dedicated owner becomes an expensive RSS feed within 90 days."

Found this in a vendor comparison blog today. It's the most honest thing I've read about enterprise CI tools.

Klue and Crayon are built on the assumption that you have a CI analyst. Someone whose whole job is curating intel and pushing battlecards to sales.

Most teams don't. So they pay $25K/yr, get overwhelmed, stop opening the emails, keep paying.

The gap isn't pricing. It's the assumption.

We've been tracking 2,000+ competitor pages since we launched.

Wednesday is when SaaS companies quietly change their pricing.

Not Monday — too visible. Not Friday — news dump day. Wednesday afternoon, 2-4pm ET, when their team's in a meeting and no one's watching the external comms.

Three changes we caught last Wednesday alone: a $20/mo plan quietly discontinued, a feature removed from the free tier, and a "contact us for enterprise" CTA replacing a public price.

None of it announced anywhere.

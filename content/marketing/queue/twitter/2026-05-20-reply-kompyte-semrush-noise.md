the Kompyte-via-Semrush combo has this issue where it pulls parent company data instead of the direct competitor's site. so you're tracking Salesforce when you actually wanted to track a specific HubSpot product page.

specificity matters a lot in CI. "track this exact URL with this CSS selector" > "track this company."

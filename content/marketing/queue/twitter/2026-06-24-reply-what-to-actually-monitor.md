Pages worth tracking:
- Pricing page (obviously)
- Features/product page (they quietly remove things)
- Job listings (hiring ML engineers = they're building something)
- Blog index (content strategy shifts signal strategic shifts)
- Footer/nav (new product lines appear here first)

The job listings one is underrated. Three ML job posts in 30 days tells you more than any analyst report.

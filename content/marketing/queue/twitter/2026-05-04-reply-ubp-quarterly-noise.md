Usage-based pricing pages are the hardest to monitor.

Structure changes quarterly — a new tier appears, credit packs get renamed, the minimum seat count bumps. Basic screenshot diff gives you a red box over 60% of the page.

AI digest is the only way to get "here's what actually changed" instead of "something changed."

depends what you actually need.

if it's tracking 5-10 URLs for pricing/content changes: don't buy enterprise. it's genuinely overkill and you'll use 10% of what you pay for.

if you need battlecard automation, CRM integrations, analyst workflows, win/loss analysis — those tools exist for a reason. just know you're buying a CI team, not a CI tool.

different products for different jobs.

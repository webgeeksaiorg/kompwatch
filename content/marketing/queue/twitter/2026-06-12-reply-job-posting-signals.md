Job listings are one of the most underrated CI signals.

3 ML engineer postings + 2 data platform roles at a competitor = someone building something new, probably 12-18 months out.

Problem: it changes fast and checking manually is a pain. Most careers pages are JS-rendered so basic scraping misses updates.

A few KompWatch users track their competitor's /careers page specifically for this. Works better than you'd expect.

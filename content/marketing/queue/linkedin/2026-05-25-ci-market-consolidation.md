The competitive intelligence market consolidated fast.

Kompyte → absorbed by Semrush → now inside Adobe's $1.9B acquisition. Crayon → acquired by SoftwareOne for $1.4B. Klue → $62M Series B, buying three companies in five months.

Every major CI tool is heading upstream. Enterprise deals. Enterprise pricing. Enterprise sales motion.

Nobody is focusing on the team of 15 people who just needs to know when a competitor changes their pricing page.

That's not a complaint — it's a product opportunity. We built KompWatch for exactly that team. Paste competitor URLs, get notified when pages change, AI explains what happened in plain English. $49/mo. No sales call. No 30-day onboarding.

The market kept pointing us at the same gap. We finally just built into it.

Still pre-launch, still figuring out distribution. But the direction is clear.

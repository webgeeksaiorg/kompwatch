Three things I noticed watching competitor pricing pages this week:

**1. Quiet discounting is back.**
Two competitors running "limited time" banners that have been up for 30+ days. Classic pressure tactic, but it tells you they're feeling churn.

**2. Feature pages are getting longer.**
Everyone's adding an AI section. Most of it is vague. When the copy says "AI-powered insights" with no specifics, that's a feature that doesn't work yet.

**3. Job postings are the leading indicator, not the lagging one.**
Before a pricing page changes, there's usually a sales hire. Before a feature ships, there's usually a specialized engineering role. Competitor careers pages are underrated.

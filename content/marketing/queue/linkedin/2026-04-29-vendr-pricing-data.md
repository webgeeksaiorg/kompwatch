Vendr published 2025 SaaS pricing data.

72% of SaaS companies changed their pricing last year.

Seven out of ten competitors moved on price. New tier. Annual discount removed. Seat limit changed. Onboarding fee quietly added.

Most product teams found out the usual ways: a customer mentioned it on a call. A sales rep lost a deal and finally checked. Someone noticed during quarterly review three weeks after the fact.

The lag between when a competitor changes their pricing page and when your team actually knows about it averages two to three weeks on manual monitoring. Longer if nobody formally owns competitive tracking.

Two-to-three week pricing intel lag is a solved problem. The automation exists — headless browser crawl, diff, AI summary, email alert. Takes about 10 minutes to set up for each competitor.

The question isn't whether to automate it. It's whether it's actually in your workflow yet.

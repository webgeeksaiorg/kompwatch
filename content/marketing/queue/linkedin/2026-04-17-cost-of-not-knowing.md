The competitor pricing page problem nobody talks about:

You find out about it 5 days late. On a sales call. From the prospect.

Not from an alert. Not from your team. From someone you're trying to close, who did a Google search an hour before the call and saw your rival now undercuts you by $20/mo.

That conversation never goes well.

I had this happen three times in one quarter before I did anything about it. Each time I thought "I should set up some kind of monitoring." Each time I didn't.

The fix is boring: automated checks, email when something meaningful changes, AI to tell you what it means in plain English instead of a raw HTML diff.

Took me about 3 weeks to build the first version. That version is now KompWatch. We watch competitor websites and tell you what changed — pricing, features, positioning copy, whatever you point us at.

$49/mo. Free tier if you're watching 2 or fewer competitors.

Not the most exciting product announcement. But if you've had that sales call, you know why it matters.

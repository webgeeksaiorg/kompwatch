I just shipped a comparison page for one of my direct competitors. KompWatch vs Headsup.bot. Live at kompwatch.com/vs/headsup.

Here's why:

Headsup.bot is a real product doing similar things. They have a free tier. They have AI summaries. They're targeting the same teams I'm targeting. Pretending they don't exist doesn't help anyone.

The comparison page is honest. Where they're better: they have a free tier we don't have yet. Where we're different: Playwright-based headless rendering handles React/SPA sites that their scraper misses. Their free tier caps at 5 competitors and 10 alerts/month — which sounds fine until you realize 5 competitors × 4 pages each = 20 pages, and the limit runs out on competitor 1.

Writing the page took 3 hours. It forced me to actually think clearly about positioning instead of vague claims like "we're better."

The rule I use for comparison pages: if I wouldn't say it out loud to a prospect, I don't put it on the page.

Still pre-launch. Still building in public. Just now with a cleaner answer to "how does this compare to Headsup.bot?"

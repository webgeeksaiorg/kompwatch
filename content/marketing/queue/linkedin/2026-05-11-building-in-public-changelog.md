I build a tool that monitors competitor websites and sends you an email when something changes.

Last week a potential customer asked how they'd know when KompWatch adds new features.

We had no public changelog.

Building a competitor monitoring tool. No changelog. The irony was not subtle.

Shipped it two days ago: kompwatch.com/changelog. Timeline-style, tags for each update type, everything we've shipped since launch.

"Building in public" isn't just tweeting metrics. Sometimes it's fixing the gap between what you're selling and how you're actually operating.

Caught by my own product category. Couldn't skip the fix.

Kompyte is now an Adobe product.

The timeline: Semrush acquired Kompyte in 2022. Adobe closed its $1.9B Semrush acquisition on April 28, 2026. So Kompyte — a competitive intelligence tool used by sales teams — now sits inside a company primarily focused on AI brand visibility and competing with Salesforce on enterprise CX.

For Kompyte users, here's what this actually means:

**Short term:** Nothing. The product works. Your contract is valid. The CSM is still there.

**Medium term:** Roadmap priority shifts. Acquisitions create internal resource competition. A $300/year Kompyte customer is not Adobe's focus customer.

**Long term:** Watch whether self-serve pricing tiers survive the next pricing refresh. That's where bundling usually starts.

---

I'm not predicting doom — Adobe might invest meaningfully in Kompyte. But if I were a Kompyte customer, I'd start evaluating alternatives now, before a renewal conversation happens on Adobe's terms.

The question worth asking: which piece of Kompyte are you actually using? Battlecard automation (sales-team specific, enterprise price) or website change monitoring (general, should be cheap)?

Those are different tools with very different replacement options.

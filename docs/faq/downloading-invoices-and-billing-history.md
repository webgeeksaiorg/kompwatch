# How do I download my invoices and view billing history?

All KompWatch invoices are available through the Stripe Customer Portal. You can download PDF invoices for any past charge.

## Steps

1. Log in to your KompWatch account.
2. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
3. Click **Manage Billing** (or **Customer Portal**).
4. In the portal, scroll to the **Invoice history** section.
5. Click **Download** (PDF icon) next to any invoice.

Invoices include:
- Invoice number and date
- Your billing address / company name
- Itemized charges (plan name, billing period)
- Tax breakdown (if applicable — see VAT/tax FAQ)
- Payment method used

## Updating your billing address or company name

Invoice details are pulled from your Stripe customer profile. To update them:

1. In the Customer Portal, click **Update information** under your name/address.
2. Edit your company name, billing address, or email.
3. Save — future invoices will reflect the updated details. Past invoices cannot be retroactively changed, but you can add a note for your finance team.

## Getting invoices emailed automatically

By default, Stripe emails a receipt to your account email address after each successful charge. If you're not receiving them:

- Check your spam folder for emails from `receipts@stripe.com`.
- In the Customer Portal, verify the billing email is correct.
- You can add a CC billing email under **Invoice settings → Extra invoice recipients**.

# Why Is There Already a Competitor on My Dashboard?

When you first sign in to KompWatch, your dashboard includes a **pre-seeded demo competitor — Acme Analytics** — with sample snapshots and detected changes. This is example data so you can explore the dashboard immediately, without waiting for your own real snapshots to run.

---

## What Is the Acme Analytics Demo Competitor?

Acme Analytics is a fictional company. It exists purely so you can see what a live dashboard looks like: change detection cards, severity badges, diff views, and the AI-generated "What this means for you" summaries.

It's sample data — no real monitoring is happening.

---

## Does It Count Against My Plan Limit?

No. The demo competitor does **not** count toward your plan's competitor limit (Free: 2, Pro: 10, Team: 50). You can add your real competitors without removing it first.

---

## How Do I Remove It?

Once you've had a look around:

1. Go to [kompwatch.com/competitors](https://kompwatch.com/competitors)
2. Find **Acme Analytics** in the list
3. Click **Remove** → confirm the deletion

---

## What If I've Already Deleted It and Want to See the Demo Again?

Visit [kompwatch.com/demo](https://kompwatch.com/demo) for a fully interactive demo — no sign-in required — that shows a sample dashboard with four competitors and realistic change data.

# How Do I Delete My Account?

You can delete your KompWatch account at any time. This article explains what account deletion means, what data is removed, and how to do it.

## What Happens When You Delete Your Account

Account deletion is **permanent and irreversible**. Specifically:

- Your user profile and login credentials are deleted
- All competitors you were monitoring are deleted
- All snapshots, change history, and digests are deleted
- Your Stripe subscription (if active) is cancelled automatically
- Your data is removed from our active database within 24 hours
- Backups are purged within 30 days in line with our [Privacy Policy](https://kompwatch.com/privacy)

**You will not be charged after deletion.** Any unused portion of a monthly billing period is not refunded (consistent with our [Terms of Service](https://kompwatch.com/terms)). If you're on an annual plan and have significant time remaining, contact us before deleting — we handle prorated refunds case by case.

## Before You Delete

- **Export your data** — Download your competitor list, change history, and digests before deleting. Go to **Dashboard → Export CSV** for a full account export. These files will not be recoverable after deletion.
- **Note your competitor URLs and CSS selectors** — if you plan to restart later, you'll need to re-add them. There's no import-from-export feature yet.
- **Cancel your subscription first** (optional but cleaner) — go to **Settings → Manage Subscription** and cancel in the Stripe Customer Portal. Your account will remain active until the billing period ends, then you can delete.

## How to Delete Your Account

1. Go to **Settings → Account**
2. Scroll to the bottom of the page
3. Click **Delete Account**
4. A confirmation dialog will appear — read it, then type `DELETE` to confirm
5. Click **Confirm deletion**

Your account is scheduled for deletion immediately. You'll receive a confirmation email at your registered address.

> If you can't find the Delete Account option, make sure you're logged in to the account you want to delete. If you're on a Team plan, only the **account owner** can delete the account — team members cannot delete the workspace.

## Can I Recover My Account After Deletion?

No. Once deletion is confirmed, the process cannot be reversed. Your data is not recoverable.

If you deleted your account by mistake and it's been less than 24 hours, contact [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) immediately — we may be able to halt the deletion process before it completes.

## GDPR / Data Deletion Requests

If you're requesting deletion of your personal data under GDPR (Right to Erasure), you can use the standard account deletion flow above, or email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) with the subject line **"GDPR Data Deletion Request"**. We'll confirm deletion of all personal data within 30 days as required by law.

See [GDPR and Data Deletion](./gdpr-data-deletion.md) for our full GDPR rights documentation.

## Pausing Instead of Deleting

If you're leaving because you're not getting value right now but want to return later, consider:
- **Downgrading to Free** — keep your account and up to 2 competitors indefinitely at no cost. [Downgrade here](./downgrading-your-plan.md).
- **Pausing your subscription** — if you're on a paid plan, see [Pausing Your Subscription](./pausing-your-subscription.md).

These options preserve your data and settings without a monthly charge.

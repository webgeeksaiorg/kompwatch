# Does the Migration Reimbursement Apply to Crayon or Other CI Tool Customers?

The current **$500 migration reimbursement offer is specific to Klue customers**. Here's why, and what your options are if you're switching from a different tool.

## Why Klue-Specific?

The reimbursement offer was created in response to Klue's two rounds of significant layoffs in 2025–2026 (approximately 85 employees in 2025, then ~100 more in May 2026), which left many Klue customers in the middle of annual contracts and evaluating alternatives during an unplanned window. The offer is designed to reduce the financial friction of leaving an annual contract early under those specific circumstances.

Klue's situation — two major headcount reductions in under 18 months, a public product pivot toward Microsoft/Teams, and AI-driven scope changes — is different from the normal competitive market. The reimbursement reflects that.

## Switching from Crayon

Crayon customers can switch to KompWatch without any barrier other than re-entering competitor URLs. KompWatch does not offer a formal reimbursement program for Crayon early-termination fees at this time.

If you're on an annual Crayon contract with an early-termination fee:

- **Free tier evaluation**: Start on KompWatch Free (2 competitors, no credit card) to evaluate monitoring quality before your renewal date — at no cost.
- **Time your switch**: The most cost-effective move is to start parallel evaluation now and switch at your natural renewal. See [When Should I Switch CI Tools Mid-Contract vs. Wait for Renewal?](./when-to-switch-ci-tools.md)
- **Contact us**: Email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) if your situation involves unusual circumstances (vendor going out of business, acquisition, service failure). We handle edge cases individually.

## Switching from Other Tools (Bombora, G2, Similarweb, etc.)

No formal reimbursement program exists for other CI or competitive intelligence tools. The same guidance applies: use the free tier to evaluate before your renewal, and switch at the natural end of your contract.

## Will the Reimbursement Offer Expand?

Not planned at this time. The Klue offer is explicitly tied to the post-layoff churn window and expires **Q3 2026 (September 30, 2026)**. Future offers, if any, would be announced on the [/migrate](https://kompwatch.com/migrate) page and in the product changelog.

## Klue Customers: How to Claim

See the full process in the [Klue Migration Reimbursement FAQ](./klue-migration-reimbursement.md). The short version: upgrade to Pro or Team, cancel Klue, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) with the cancellation confirmation showing the fee, and we refund up to $500 within 5 business days.

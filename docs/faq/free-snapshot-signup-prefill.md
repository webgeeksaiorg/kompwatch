# Free Snapshot — Why Is My Competitor URL Pre-Filled on the Signup Page?

## Why is the competitor URL already in the signup form?

If you just ran a free competitor snapshot at `/snapshot`, KompWatch carries your competitor's URL forward into the signup form. When you click "Start monitoring this competitor" or navigate to `/login`, the URL you analyzed is pre-populated so you don't have to type it again.

This is purely a convenience shortcut — it saves one copy-paste step.

## Does this mean you stored my URL?

The URL is passed as a URL parameter in the link from the snapshot results page. It is not stored separately from your snapshot session. If you open the signup page directly (e.g., by navigating to `kompwatch.com/login` without coming from a snapshot result), the field will be empty.

## I don't want the URL pre-filled — can I clear it?

Yes. The field is editable — click it and clear the value, or type a different URL. Nothing is locked in place.

## Does the pre-filled URL become my first monitored competitor automatically?

Not until you complete signup and explicitly add it. The pre-fill populates the "Add your first competitor" onboarding step to reduce friction, but you can change or remove it before saving.

## What if I want to monitor a different competitor after signing up?

You can add any competitor URL from the dashboard. The free plan supports up to 2 competitors — the pre-filled one counts toward that limit only after you save it.

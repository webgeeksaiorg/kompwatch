# Is There a Money-Back Guarantee?

Yes. KompWatch offers a **7-day refund policy** for all paid plan subscriptions. If you subscribe, use the product, and it's not right for your team, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) within 7 days of your first charge and we'll refund you in full — no questions asked.

---

## How the Trial + Refund Policy Work Together

KompWatch is designed so you never need the refund policy:

1. **7-day free trial, no credit card required.** Start a Pro or Team trial without entering any payment details. You get full access to every paid feature for 7 days. If you decide it's not for you, just don't add a card — your account reverts to the free plan automatically.

2. **If you did add a card and want a refund:** Email us within 7 days of your first charge. We'll issue a full refund to your original payment method, no back-and-forth required.

The trial exists so you don't have to rely on the refund policy. But if you do need it, it's there.

---

## When Does the Refund Policy Apply?

| Scenario | What Happens |
|---|---|
| Cancel during free trial (no card added) | No charge — nothing to refund |
| Cancel during free trial (card added) | Trial cancellation — no charge |
| Cancel within 7 days of first paid charge | Full refund, no questions asked |
| Cancel after 7 days of first paid charge | No refund — cancel to prevent future billing |
| Annual plan, cancel within 7 days | Full refund of annual charge |
| Annual plan, cancel after 7 days | No refund for remaining months |

---

## How to Request a Refund

1. Email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) with the subject line "Refund request"
2. Include the email address on your KompWatch account
3. We'll confirm and process within 24 hours — typically faster

Refunds appear on your statement within 5–10 business days depending on your bank.

---

## Can I Cancel Anytime?

Yes. Monthly subscribers can cancel anytime — you keep access until the end of the current billing period, then downgrade to free. Annual subscribers can cancel to prevent auto-renewal; the remaining months are not refunded after the 7-day window.

Cancel via **Settings → Manage Subscription** in the Stripe Customer Portal — no need to contact support.

---

## What About Founding Customer Pricing?

If you subscribed at the founding rate ($29/mo) and cancel, you lose the founding rate permanently. Spots are not re-issued. The 7-day refund applies normally, but if you request a refund and cancel, you cannot reclaim your founding rate later.

# Switching from Seeto to KompWatch

Seeto is a screenshot-focused website monitoring tool starting at $99/mo that captures visual diffs of competitor homepages. If you need broader coverage beyond homepage screenshots — pricing changes, blog posts, job listings, AI digests — this guide covers what KompWatch offers and how to get started.

## Why Teams Switch

Teams that move from Seeto to KompWatch typically cite:

- **Cost** — Seeto starts at $99/mo ($1,188/yr). KompWatch Pro is $49/mo ($588/yr) — half the price with significantly more coverage.
- **Screenshot-only coverage** — Seeto's core feature is visual screenshot diffs. It doesn't analyze what actually changed in the page content, parse pricing tables, or monitor blogs and job listings. KompWatch monitors content changes across all tracked pages, not just visual appearance.
- **No AI summaries** — Seeto shows you a visual before/after. KompWatch tells you what changed, why it likely matters, and how severe it is — in plain English, powered by Claude AI.
- **Homepage-only monitoring** — Seeto focuses on the homepage. KompWatch monitors multiple pages per competitor: pricing, features, blog, docs, jobs, press. See [Which Pages to Monitor](./which-pages-to-monitor-per-competitor.md).
- **No job listing tracking** — Competitor hiring patterns predict strategic direction months before public announcements. Seeto doesn't monitor job listings. KompWatch does, on all paid plans.
- **Free plan** — Seeto has no free tier. KompWatch has a free plan (2 competitors, no credit card required).

## How to Switch

You can't import monitors from Seeto, but setup takes under 10 minutes.

1. **Sign up at [kompwatch.com](https://kompwatch.com)** — free, no credit card required.
2. **Add your competitor URLs** — the same ones you were monitoring in Seeto. See [Adding a Competitor](./adding-competitors.md).
3. **Add more pages per competitor** — unlike Seeto's homepage focus, add your competitors' `/pricing`, `/features`, `/blog`, and job listing URLs as separate competitors. See [Which Pages to Monitor](./which-pages-to-monitor-per-competitor.md).
4. **Set CSS selectors** (optional but recommended) to isolate specific sections and reduce noise from nav/footer/banner changes. See [CSS Selector Targeting](./css-selectors.md).
5. **Connect Slack** in Settings → Integrations if you use a `#competitive-intel` channel.

Your first snapshot runs immediately. Change detection begins with the second snapshot — within 24 hours on Free, a few hours on Pro.

## Will I Lose My Seeto History?

KompWatch doesn't import historical data from Seeto. Your monitoring history starts fresh from when you add a competitor. If you have past Seeto screenshot history you want to retain, download or export it before cancelling.

## Running Both in Parallel

KompWatch's free tier (2 competitors, no credit card) lets you evaluate side-by-side before cancelling Seeto. Add your top 2 competitors and compare digest quality over 1–2 weeks.

## Who Is Enterprise For?

The Enterprise plan is designed for:

- **VP Strategy or CI program owners** who need unlimited competitor capacity and real-time alerts across the full competitive landscape
- **Organizations with IT security requirements** that mandate SSO (Okta, Google Workspace, Azure AD, SAML 2.0) and provisioning via SCIM
- **Teams that need contractual uptime guarantees** — Enterprise includes a formal SLA
- **Companies needing custom integrations** beyond the standard webhook/Slack/Zapier stack

If you're tracking under 50 competitors and don't need SSO or a dedicated account manager, the **Team plan at $149/mo** covers most needs.

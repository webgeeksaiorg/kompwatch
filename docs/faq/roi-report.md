# ROI Report & Stakeholder Sharing

The **Stakeholder ROI Report** is live at [kompwatch.com/reports/roi](https://kompwatch.com/reports/roi). It automatically calculates the competitive intelligence value KompWatch has delivered to your team for any period you choose, and lets you share or export it in one click.

## What Does the ROI Report Include?

- **Estimated value delivered** — dollar value and hours of manual monitoring replaced, displayed as the headline metric
- **Changes detected** — total count for the selected period
- **High-impact alerts** — count of HIGH and CRITICAL severity changes
- **Competitors tracked** — number of active monitors
- **Digests delivered** — how many AI digest emails were sent in the period
- **Changes by severity** — bar breakdown across LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH / CRITICAL
- **Changes by type** — bar breakdown across Pricing, Feature, Blog, Jobs, Tech, General
- **Top competitors by activity** — ranked list of the competitors that changed most

## How Is Estimated Value Calculated?

Each detected change is estimated to replace approximately **15 minutes of manual monitoring work** (finding it, reading it, writing up a summary). KompWatch uses a blended analyst rate of **$75/hour** as a baseline.

> Formula: `total changes × 15 min ÷ 60 × $75/hr`

For example, 40 detected changes = ~10 hours replaced = ~$750 in estimated analyst time saved.

The methodology note appears at the bottom of every report and exported PDF.

## Choosing a Time Period

Use the **period selector** (top-left of the report) to switch between:

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Last 7 days | Rolling 7-day window |
| Last 30 days | Rolling 30-day window (default) |
| Last 90 days | Rolling 90-day window |
| Last month | Full previous calendar month |
| This month | Current calendar month to date |

## Sharing the Report

Click **Share report** (top-right of the report) to generate a shareable link and copy it to your clipboard. Send it directly to a manager or stakeholder — they can click through and explore the report without needing a KompWatch account.

## Downloading as PDF

Click **Download PDF** to export a branded, print-ready PDF. The PDF includes all metrics, severity and type bar charts, top competitors by activity, and a methodology note. Useful for attaching to a budget presentation or board pack.

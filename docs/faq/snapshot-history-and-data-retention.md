# Snapshot History and Data Retention

KompWatch stores historical snapshots and change logs so you can look back at how competitors have evolved over time — not just what changed today.

## How Long Is Data Kept?

| Plan | Snapshot History | Change Log | Digests |
|------|-----------------|------------|---------|
| Free | 30 days | 90 days | Last 10 |
| Pro | 6 months | 1 year | Last 50 |
| Team | 2 years | 2 years | Unlimited |

- **Snapshot history** — the raw HTML and screenshot captured at each monitoring interval
- **Change log** — the AI-detected changes and summaries derived from snapshot comparisons
- **Digests** — your past digest emails, viewable in-app at [kompwatch.com/digests](https://kompwatch.com/digests)

## Browsing Historical Snapshots

To view a past snapshot for a competitor:

1. Go to [Competitors](https://kompwatch.com/competitors) and click a competitor
2. On the competitor detail page, click the **Snapshot History** tab
3. Select any snapshot from the timeline to view its captured HTML and screenshot side-by-side

You can also compare any two snapshots by selecting them both — a diff view highlights what changed between those two points in time.

## What Counts as "Snapshot Age"?

Age is calculated from the date the snapshot was captured, not from today's date. A snapshot taken 25 days ago on the Free plan has 5 days left before it ages out; the same snapshot on Pro persists for another 5+ months.

## What Happens When You Downgrade?

If you downgrade to a lower plan, **existing snapshots and changes are not immediately deleted**. You get a 30-day grace period to export your data before older records fall outside the new plan's retention window and are permanently removed.

You'll receive an email notification when a downgrade causes data to be scheduled for deletion.

## What Happens When You Delete a Competitor?

Deleting a competitor from your account **permanently deletes all its snapshots, changes, and digests** — there is no recovery. If you want to stop monitoring a competitor temporarily without losing history, **pause it instead**:

1. Go to [Competitors](https://kompwatch.com/competitors)
2. Click the three-dot menu next to the competitor
3. Select **Pause monitoring**

Paused competitors keep their full history intact. You can resume monitoring at any time.

## Exporting Before Data Ages Out

If you need to preserve data beyond your plan's retention window, export it first:

- **CSV or JSON export** from the dashboard or competitor detail page
- Exported records include the full change log — date, type, severity, and AI summary
- Raw HTML snapshots are not included in exports (screenshots are, as URLs)

For full export instructions, see [Exporting Your Change Data](./exporting-your-data.md).

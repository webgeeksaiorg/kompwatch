# How Do I Change My Email Address?

KompWatch uses **magic links** for authentication — your email address is both your login identity and where all alerts and digests are delivered. Here's how to update it.

## Changing Your Login Email

Currently, email address changes are handled through our support team rather than via a self-serve flow in the dashboard.

**To change your email:**

1. Email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) from your **current registered email address**
2. Include the new email address you want to use
3. We'll update your account and send a confirmation to both addresses

Changes are processed within **24 hours** during business days. You'll receive a confirmation at both your old and new email addresses once the change is complete.

> **Why isn't this self-serve?** Because email is your login method, changing it without verification steps creates a security risk — anyone who briefly accessed your inbox could lock you out by changing the address. Manual verification through the original address ensures the change is authorized.

## Changing Where Digests Are Delivered

If you want change alerts and digests to go to a **different email address than your login**, you can set a separate notification address:

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications**
2. Under **Digest delivery**, enter the address you want digests sent to
3. Save

This lets your login remain on one address (e.g. `you@company.com`) while sending competitive intelligence digests to a shared inbox (e.g. `competitive@company.com` or a Slack email-to-channel integration).

## Inviting Team Members to Their Own Login

If a team member needs their own dashboard access with their own email, add them as a team member instead of sharing a login:

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click **Invite member**
3. Enter their email — they'll receive a magic link to set up their own access

See [Inviting Team Members](./inviting-team-members.md) for details on roles and permissions.

## What If I Can't Access My Current Email?

If you've lost access to your registered email address and can't receive the login magic link:

1. Email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) from any address you control
2. Include: your name, the old email address, the new address, and any billing details (last 4 digits of card, subscription plan) to help us verify ownership
3. We'll verify your identity and update the address manually

Response time: within 24 business hours.

## What Changes When Your Email Changes

| What updates | What stays the same |
|---|---|
| Login email address | All your competitors and snapshots |
| Digest delivery address | Change history and digests |
| Stripe billing email (we update this too) | Subscription status |

We update your Stripe customer record at the same time so billing receipts go to your new address.

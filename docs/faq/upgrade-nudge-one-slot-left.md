# "One Slot Left" — Why Am I Seeing an Upgrade Prompt?

If you see a banner saying **"One slot left on Free"** (or a similar message) on the Add Competitor page, it means you've used all but one of the competitor slots on your current plan. You can still add that last competitor — the banner is just a heads-up, not a blocker.

---

## Why Does This Appear?

KompWatch shows a soft upgrade reminder when you're one slot away from your plan limit, so you have time to decide whether to upgrade *before* you hit the wall. It appears **above** the Add Competitor form and doesn't prevent you from adding your remaining slot.

This is separate from the **hard limit notice** you'll see once all slots are filled (at which point the add form is replaced entirely).

---

## Can I Just Ignore It?

Yes. Click anywhere outside the banner and use the form below it as normal. The nudge is informational — your last available slot is still open and ready to use.

---

## What Happens After I Use My Last Slot?

Once you've added your final competitor, the add form is replaced with an upgrade card. At that point you'll need to either:

- **Upgrade your plan** to get more slots, or
- **Pause or delete** an existing competitor to free up a slot

See [I Can't Add More Competitors — Plan Limit Reached](./plan-competitor-limit-reached.md) for full details.

---

## Plan Limits at a Glance

| Plan | Max Competitors |
|------|----------------|
| Free | 2 |
| Pro  | 10 |
| Team | 50 |

---

## How Do I Upgrade?

Click **"See Pro plan"** in the banner, or go to [kompwatch.com/pricing](https://kompwatch.com/pricing). You can also upgrade at any time from [kompwatch.com/settings](https://kompwatch.com/settings) → **Manage Subscription**.

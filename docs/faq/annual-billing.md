# Annual Billing

KompWatch offers annual billing at a 20% discount versus paying month-to-month. You can select annual or monthly pricing at any point before checkout.

## Pricing

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (billed yearly) | Savings |
|------|---------|------------------------|---------|
| Pro  | $49/mo  | $39/mo ($468/year)     | $120/year |
| Team | $149/mo | $119/mo ($1,428/year)  | $360/year |

## How to Switch to Annual Billing

**New subscriptions:** Toggle the billing period on the [pricing page](https://kompwatch.com/pricing) before clicking the upgrade button. The toggle defaults to Annual.

**Existing monthly subscribers:** Go to [Settings → Manage Subscription](https://kompwatch.com/settings) to open the Stripe Customer Portal. From there you can switch to annual billing — the change takes effect at your next renewal date and you'll be charged the annual rate upfront.

## When Am I Charged?

Annual plans are billed **upfront** — one payment covers the full year. You'll see a single charge for the full annual amount (e.g. $468 for Pro Annual) rather than twelve monthly charges.

## What Happens If I Cancel an Annual Plan?

Cancellations take effect at the **end of your annual period**. You keep full access for the remainder of the year you've paid for and will not be charged again.

We do not offer pro-rated refunds for unused months on annual plans. If you have an exceptional circumstance, [contact us](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) and a team member will review it.

## Can I Downgrade Mid-Year?

Downgrades from an annual plan take effect at the **end of your current billing period** (your annual renewal date). If you downgrade to a plan with a lower competitor limit, you'll be prompted to deactivate excess competitors before the change takes effect.

## Switching Back to Monthly

You can switch from annual to monthly at any time via the Customer Portal — the change takes effect at your next renewal date.

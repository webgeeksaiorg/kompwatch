# Founding Customer Program vs. Founding User Badge — What's the Difference?

KompWatch has two separate "founding" programs. They're independent — you can participate in one, both, or neither. Here's how they differ.

---

## Quick Comparison

| | Founding Customer Program | Founding User Badge |
|---|---|---|
| **What it is** | A locked-in low rate for early subscribers | A permanent profile badge for early G2 reviewers |
| **Who qualifies** | First 20 monthly Pro subscribers | First 100 KompWatch users who leave a G2 review |
| **What you get** | $29/mo Pro forever (vs. standard $49/mo) | Founding User badge on your account + shared digests |
| **How to claim** | Subscribe via the Founding Customer checkout flow | Leave a G2 review and email us the link |
| **Spots available** | 20 total | 100 total |
| **Cost** | $29/mo (billed monthly) | Free — no payment required |
| **Expires?** | Active as long as your Pro subscription is active | Permanent — never expires |

---

## Founding Customer Program — $29/mo Pro for Life

This is a **billing perk for early paying subscribers**. The first 20 people who subscribe to KompWatch Pro on a monthly plan lock in **$29/mo forever** — even as prices increase for future customers.

- Spots are claimed at checkout. The pricing page shows a live counter of remaining spots.
- When all 20 spots are claimed, the program closes permanently.
- If you cancel or downgrade to Free, the founding rate is permanently forfeited.

→ See full details: [Founding Customer Program →](./founding-pricing.md)

---

## Founding User Badge — G2 Review Recognition

This is a **recognition program for early users who share honest feedback** on G2. The first 100 KompWatch users who leave a G2 review and email us the link receive a permanent Founding User badge on their account.

- No payment or plan required — Free plan users are eligible.
- The badge is cosmetic: it appears on your account profile and in digest emails.
- Honest critical reviews are just as eligible as positive ones.

→ See full details: [Founding User Badge →](./founding-user-badge.md)

---

## Can I Get Both?

Yes. If you're a paying Pro subscriber who claimed a founding spot **and** left a G2 review, you qualify for both the $29/mo pricing lock and the Founding User badge. They're completely independent.

# What Is the Sample Digest Page?

[kompwatch.com/sample-digest](https://kompwatch.com/sample-digest) shows a realistic example of the weekly or daily email digest you'd receive after signing up. It's designed to help you evaluate KompWatch before committing.

## Is the Sample Data Real?

No — the sample uses **realistic but fictional data**. The competitor names, URLs, change summaries, and severity ratings are illustrative examples, not live scrapes of real companies.

The format, structure, and AI-generated summaries are accurate representations of what you'd receive for your actual competitors.

## Does the Real Digest Look Exactly Like This?

Mostly, yes. The real digest shares the same layout:

- Competitor name and URL
- Change type (Pricing, Feature, Blog, Job, Tech, or General)
- Severity badge (Low / Medium / High / Critical)
- AI-generated plain-English summary
- Timestamp of detection

The only differences are cosmetic — your digest will show your actual competitors and their real detected changes, and the subject line will reflect your monitoring period.

## How Often Will I Actually Receive Digests?

| Plan | Frequency |
|------|-----------|
| Free | Weekly (Mondays) |
| Pro ($49/mo) | Daily |
| Team ($149/mo) | Real-time (within ~1 hour of a change) |

No email is sent if no changes were detected in the period — you'll only hear from us when there's something worth reading.

# What Is the "Also Track YOUR Site" Prompt After a Free Snapshot?

After you run a free competitor snapshot, KompWatch shows an amber banner that reads:

> **Also track YOUR site vs. [competitor name]**
> See how your site stacks up — get alerts when they change pricing, ship features, or publish content that affects your positioning.

## What it does

Clicking "Start tracking both sites free →" takes you to the sign-up page with your competitor's URL pre-filled. Once you create a free account, KompWatch will monitor:

1. **The competitor you just snapshotted** — tracked automatically based on what you entered
2. **Your own site** — you can add it as a second tracked URL to see changes side by side in your digest

This gives you a direct comparison: when your competitor updates their pricing page, you see it alongside your own pricing page in the same weekly digest.

## Why track your own site?

- **Catch unintended changes** — CMS updates, agency edits, or CDN issues can silently overwrite your copy
- **Side-by-side digests** — see your site and a competitor in the same AI summary, so you can spot positioning gaps fast
- **Verify your own deploys** — confirm that a landing page update or pricing change went live as intended

## Does my own site count against my competitor limit?

Yes. On the Free plan (2 competitor slots), adding your own site uses one slot, leaving one for a competitor. If you want to track yourself plus multiple competitors, upgrade to Pro (10 slots) or Team (50 slots).

## Can I dismiss the banner?

The banner only appears on the free snapshot results page, not inside the dashboard. Once you sign up and log in, it won't appear again.

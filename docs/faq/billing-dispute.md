# Billing Questions & Disputes

We want every customer to feel good about what they pay for. If you have a billing concern, here's how we handle it.

## Unexpected Charge

If you see an unexpected charge from KompWatch:

1. Check that you didn't have a free trial that ended — you would have received an email reminder before the trial concluded.
2. Log into the [Customer Portal](https://kompwatch.com/settings) to view your invoice history.
3. If you still believe the charge is incorrect, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) with your account email and the charge amount.

## Refund Policy

- **Trial cancellations**: If you cancel during your free trial, you are not charged.
- **Monthly plans**: We offer refunds at our discretion for charges within the last 7 days if you haven't actively used the service.
- **Refund requests**: Email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com). We'll review and respond within 24 hours.

## Double Charges

Double charges are rare but can happen if a payment method update coincides with a billing cycle. Contact us and we'll resolve it immediately.

## Changing Payment Method

Go to [kompwatch.com/settings](https://kompwatch.com/settings) → **Manage Subscription** → **Update Payment Method** in the Stripe Customer Portal.

## Invoices & Receipts

All invoices are available in the [Customer Portal](https://kompwatch.com/settings). You can download PDF invoices for any payment.

---
*For any billing issue, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com). A team member will follow up within 24 hours.*

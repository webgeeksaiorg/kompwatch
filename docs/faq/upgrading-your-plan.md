# How to Upgrade Your Plan

Upgrading unlocks more competitors, faster snapshots, and more frequent digest emails. The process takes under 2 minutes.

---

## How to Upgrade

1. Go to [kompwatch.com/settings](https://kompwatch.com/settings).
2. Click **Upgrade Plan** (or **Manage Subscription** if you're already on a paid plan).
3. You'll be redirected to the Stripe checkout page.
4. Enter your payment details and confirm.

Your new plan is active immediately after payment.

---

## What Changes After Upgrading

| Feature | Free → Pro | Pro → Team |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| Competitor slots | 2 → 10 | 10 → 50 |
| Snapshot frequency | Daily → Every 6h | Every 6h → Hourly |
| Digest frequency | Weekly → Daily | Daily → Real-time |

The new snapshot and digest schedule begins on your **next scheduled run** — there is no manual step required.

---

## I Clicked "Upgrade" but Was Asked to Log In First

This is expected behavior. If you're not yet logged in, KompWatch will redirect you to the login page to verify your identity before processing payment.

**Your upgrade intent is preserved.** After you complete the magic-link login, you'll be automatically redirected back to the Stripe checkout — you won't need to click "Upgrade" again.

If for any reason you land on the dashboard instead of checkout after logging in, go to **Settings → Upgrade Plan** to resume.

---

## Billing and Proration

- You are billed immediately on upgrade.
- If you upgrade mid-cycle (e.g., from Free to Pro on the 15th of a monthly cycle), Stripe charges a prorated amount for the remainder of the month, then the full amount on your next billing date.
- Your invoice will reflect the exact amounts.

---

## Can I Upgrade for One Month and Downgrade Again?

Yes. Plans are month-to-month (or annual if you select that option). You can upgrade, use the higher-tier features, and downgrade at any time. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period. All your data — snapshots, change history, and competitor configurations — is preserved.

---

## Payment Issues at Checkout

If your payment fails at Stripe checkout:

- Double-check the card number, expiry, and CVC.
- Make sure the billing address matches your card's registered address.
- Try a different payment method (Stripe supports all major cards and some regional methods).
- If the issue persists, contact your bank — some banks flag new SaaS subscriptions for fraud review.

If you're still stuck, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) and we'll help manually.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does upgrading to Pro or Team give me a free trial?**
No — Pro and Team plans are billed immediately. There is no trial period for paid plans, but you can cancel anytime.

**Do I lose my existing competitor data when I upgrade?**
No. All your snapshots, detected changes, and digest history carry over. Upgrading only adds capacity and speed.

**Can I upgrade directly from Free to Team (skipping Pro)?**
Yes. You can select any plan tier from Settings → Upgrade Plan.

**Is there an annual billing discount?**
Yes — annual plans offer a discount versus monthly. See the [pricing page](https://kompwatch.com/pricing) for current rates, or check [Annual Billing](./annual-billing.md) for details.

# Founding Customer Program — $29/mo Pro for Life

KompWatch's Founding Customer Program gives the **first 20 paying customers** Pro at **$29/mo, forever** — even as the product grows and pricing increases for new customers.

## What Is the Founding Customer Program?

When you subscribe to Pro on a **monthly plan**, you may be eligible for the founding rate if spots remain:

- **Price:** $29/mo (instead of the standard $49/mo)
- **Savings:** 41% off, locked in for the lifetime of your subscription
- **Spots:** Only 20 total — first-come, first-served
- **Plan:** Pro only (Team plan is not included)
- **Billing:** Monthly — no annual commitment required

This is a one-time opportunity for early believers who want to lock in the best possible rate before the program closes.

## How Many Spots Are There?

**20 spots total.** The pricing page shows a live counter of how many remain. When all 20 are claimed, the program closes permanently and new subscribers pay the standard Pro rate of $49/mo.

Spots are claimed the moment checkout completes — selecting the plan or starting checkout does not hold a spot.

## How Do I Claim the Founding Rate?

1. Go to [kompwatch.com/pricing](https://kompwatch.com/pricing)
2. Look for the **Founding Customer** banner at the top of the page
3. If spots remain, click **Claim founding rate — $29/mo**
4. Complete Stripe checkout — your rate is locked when payment confirms

You'll see $29/mo reflected in the Stripe checkout before you confirm. That's your guarantee.

**Already a subscriber on monthly Pro?** Your rate cannot be retroactively switched to the founding price. Founding spots are only available via the special checkout flow.

## Is It Really Locked Forever?

Yes — **for as long as your Pro subscription stays active**. We will never raise your founding rate. Future price increases for new customers do not affect you.

If you cancel your subscription, the founding rate is not available again when you resubscribe.

## What Happens If I Pause or Cancel?

- **Pausing:** Not currently supported on monthly plans. If you'd like to pause, contact [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com).
- **Cancelling and resubscribing:** If you cancel and later resubscribe, founding pricing is gone. New subscriptions always pay the current standard rate.

## Can I Upgrade to Team at the Founding Rate?

No. The Founding Customer Program applies to **Pro monthly only**. If you upgrade to Team, your Team subscription is billed at the standard Team rate at the time of upgrade.

## The Counter Said Spots Were Available but I Was Charged $49/mo — What Happened?

If all 20 spots were claimed while you were in checkout, you would have been charged the standard $49/mo rate. The price shown at the Stripe **order confirmation** screen is what you pay — if it shows $29/mo, you're locked in.

If you were unexpectedly charged $49/mo and believe you completed checkout before spots ran out, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) with your account email and Stripe receipt. We'll review and correct any error.

## I See $9.99/mo on My Stripe Dashboard — Is That Correct?

No. The current founding rate is **$29/mo** and the standard rate is **$49/mo**. A $9.99 charge indicates a legacy beta price ID that was not intended for active use.

If your account shows $9.99/mo, please email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) — we'll review your account and clarify your billing status. See also: [My Subscription Price Doesn't Match the Pricing Page](./subscription-price-mismatch.md).

## Questions?

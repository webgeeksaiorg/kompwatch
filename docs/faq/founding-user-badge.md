# Founding User Badge — How to Claim It

KompWatch's Founding 100 program recognizes our first 100 G2 reviewers with a permanent **Founding User badge** on their account profile and shared digests.

## How to Claim

1. **Leave an honest review on G2** — visit [kompwatch.com/review](https://kompwatch.com/review) for step-by-step instructions and writing prompts. A G2 account (free, work email) is required.
2. **Wait for G2 to publish your review** — G2 reviews typically publish within 1–3 business days after submission.
3. **Email us your review link** — once published, send [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) a link to your review. Subject line: "Founding User badge — [your account email]".
4. **Badge applied within 24 hours** — we'll add the Founding User badge to your KompWatch account and reply to confirm.

## Common Questions

**Does the review have to be positive?**
No. We ask for honest feedback. Critical reviews are just as eligible for the badge and help us prioritize improvements.

**Does the badge expire?**
No. The Founding User badge is permanent regardless of your plan or subscription status.

**Is there a deadline?**
No fixed date, but the program closes once all 100 spots are claimed. Spots are awarded in the order reviews are confirmed, not submitted.

**I emailed but haven't heard back — what now?**
Check your spam folder for our reply. If nothing after 48 hours, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) again with "Founding User badge follow-up" in the subject line.

**I lost the link to my G2 review — can I still claim?**
Yes. Search for "KompWatch" on G2 while logged in to your G2 account to find your published review. Copy the URL and send it to us.

## For Support Agents

When a user emails to claim the badge:
1. Verify the linked G2 review is published and mentions KompWatch.
2. Confirm the sender's email matches a KompWatch account.
3. Add a `founding_user: true` flag to the user's account (or flag for manual DB update until the admin UI supports this).
4. Reply with the confirmation template and note the spot number if trackable.

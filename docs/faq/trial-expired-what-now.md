# My Trial Expired — What Happens Now?

Your trial ended and you didn't subscribe. Here's exactly what changed, what you still have, and what to do if you want to pick up where you left off.

---

## What Happened to My Account?

When a 7-day trial ends without a paid subscription, your account automatically **reverts to the Free plan**. Nothing is deleted.

| | During Trial (Pro) | After Trial (Free) |
|---|---|---|
| Competitors monitored | 10 | 2 |
| Snapshot frequency | Every 6 hours | Daily |
| Digest emails | Daily | Weekly |
| Change history | 90 days | 30 days |
| Your account | ✓ Active | ✓ Active |
| Competitors you added | ✓ Kept | ✓ Kept (first 2 active) |
| Changes detected | ✓ Kept | ✓ Kept (within 30-day window) |

Your competitors and history don't disappear — they're still there. If you had 10 competitors, only the first 2 are actively monitored on the Free plan; the rest are paused and resume immediately if you upgrade.

---

## Can I Restart My Trial?

Trials are one-time per account. If your trial has ended, you can't restart it for free. However:

- **Start a paid subscription and cancel within 7 days** for a full refund if it's not right. The refund window effectively gives you a low-risk paid trial period. See [Money-Back Guarantee →](./money-back-guarantee.md)
- **Ask for a trial extension** if you had a legitimate reason you couldn't evaluate the product during your 7 days (e.g., you were travelling, had a team emergency, or the product had an outage during your trial window). Email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) — we grant extensions in genuine cases. See [Trial Extension →](./trial-extension.md)

---

## What Did I Miss During My Trial?

If your trial ended and you didn't see any changes detected, that's the most common reason people don't convert — and it's often a setup issue, not a signal that the product doesn't work.

The most frequent causes:
- You added only 1–2 competitors (not enough surface area to catch changes in 7 days)
- You monitored the homepage instead of `/pricing` or `/features` pages where changes happen
- The first snapshot hadn't run yet when you checked

See [Why Haven't I Seen Any Changes Yet? →](./trial-no-changes-yet.md) for a full troubleshooting walkthrough.

---

## How Do I Upgrade from Free to Pro?

1. Log in to your KompWatch account
2. Go to **Settings → Plan**
3. Click **Upgrade to Pro**
4. Complete Stripe checkout — your competitors and history are immediately restored at Pro limits

If the **Founding Customer** banner is still visible on the pricing page, you can lock in $29/mo (instead of $49/mo) for as long as you stay subscribed. That offer closes permanently when the 20 spots fill. See [Founding Customer Program →](./founding-pricing.md)

---

## Will I Be Charged Automatically?

No. The Free plan never charges you. You won't be billed unless you actively click through to the Stripe checkout and confirm a subscription. There are no surprise charges from a lapsed trial.

---

## I Changed My Mind and Want to Subscribe Now

Go to [kompwatch.com/pricing](https://kompwatch.com/pricing) and start a Pro or Team subscription. Your trial history carries over — you pick up from where you left off.

If you have questions before subscribing, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) and a team member will respond within 24 hours.

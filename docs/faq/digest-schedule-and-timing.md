# Digest Schedule and Timing — When Do Emails Arrive, and Can I Change It?

Your digest delivery schedule depends on your plan. Here's what to expect and what you can customize.

---

## Default Schedule by Plan

| Plan | Default cadence | Default delivery time |
|------|-----------------|-----------------------|
| Free | Weekly (Mondays) | 9:00 AM UTC |
| Pro | Daily | 9:00 AM UTC |
| Team | Real-time (within 1h of a detected change) | Sent immediately |

Digests are only sent when new changes have been detected since the last one — if your competitors had a quiet week, no email goes out.

---

## Can I Change the Delivery Day?

**Free plan:** The weekly digest goes out on **Monday mornings** by default. If you'd prefer a different day — for example, Friday afternoon to prep for end-of-week review — you can change this in:

> **Settings → Digest Preferences → Weekly digest day**

Choose any day of the week from the dropdown. The first digest on the new schedule goes out on the next occurrence of that day.

**Pro plan:** Daily digests go out every morning by default. You can also switch to **Smart** mode (sends only when MEDIUM/HIGH changes occur) or **Weekly** mode in **Settings → Digest Preferences → Digest frequency**. See [Digest Frequency Control](./digest-frequency-control.md) for details.

---

## Can I Change the Delivery Time?

Yes, for Free and Pro plans. Go to **Settings → Digest Preferences → Delivery time** and choose your preferred hour (in your account timezone).

Common setups:
- **7 AM** — read with morning coffee before standups
- **5 PM** — end-of-day review before logging off
- **9 AM Friday** — tee up the weekly review meeting

Changes take effect on the next scheduled digest.

---

## Timezone

Digest delivery times use the timezone set in **Settings → Account → Timezone**. If no timezone is set, delivery defaults to UTC.

To check or update your timezone:
1. Go to [kompwatch.com/settings](https://kompwatch.com/settings)
2. Under **Account**, find the **Timezone** dropdown
3. Select your timezone and save

**Team plan:** Real-time digests fire as soon as a change is detected — timezone doesn't affect the trigger, only the timestamp displayed inside the email.

---

## Skipping a Digest (Without Pausing Monitoring)

If you're going on vacation or want to skip a digest cycle, you can:

1. **Disable email digests temporarily** — Go to **Settings → Digest Preferences** and toggle off **Email delivery**. Digests are still generated and saved to your [Digests page](https://kompwatch.com/digests); you just won't receive emails until you re-enable.

2. **Pause specific competitors** — Pausing stops snapshots from those competitors, so no new changes are generated and nothing appears in your next digest. See [Managing Your Competitors](./managing-competitors.md).

---

## Viewing Digests Without Waiting for Email

Your most recent digests are always available in-app at [kompwatch.com/digests](https://kompwatch.com/digests), regardless of email settings. If you want to check in before your next scheduled delivery, that's the fastest way.

# Managing Email Preferences and Onboarding Emails

KompWatch sends two categories of email: **product emails** (digests, change alerts, billing) and **onboarding emails** (tips, upgrade prompts, and feature announcements sent in the first 14 days after signup). Here's how to manage each.

---

## Why Am I Getting These Emails?

When you create a KompWatch account, you're enrolled in a short onboarding sequence (up to 5 emails over 14 days). These cover:

- How to add your first competitor and set a CSS selector
- How to read your digest and adjust severity thresholds
- What the Pro plan unlocks (and whether it's worth it for your use case)
- Tips from teams that have gotten the most value from monitoring

These are sent from `hello@kompwatch.com`. They are not cold emails — you signed up, so you're already in the system.

---

## How Do I Stop the Onboarding Emails?

Click the **Unsubscribe** link at the bottom of any onboarding email. This removes you from the sequence immediately — no confirmation required.

Alternatively:

1. Go to **Settings → Email Preferences** in your KompWatch dashboard
2. Toggle off **Onboarding and tips**
3. Save

This only affects marketing/onboarding emails. Digest emails and billing notifications are not affected.

---

## How Do I Manage Digest Emails?

Your digest email is the core product output — it's not a marketing email. You can:

- **Change digest frequency:** Settings → Notifications → Digest schedule (Free: weekly, Pro: daily, Team: real-time)
- **Pause digests temporarily:** Settings → Notifications → toggle **Pause digest emails**
- **Change the digest email address:** Settings → Account → update your email

If you want to stop receiving digests entirely but keep your account active, toggle **Pause digest emails** in Settings.

---

## How Do I Stop All Email from KompWatch?

If you want to stop all email (including digests and billing):

1. Go to **Settings → Email Preferences**
2. Toggle off all email categories
3. Save

Note: **Billing emails cannot be fully disabled** — receipts, payment failures, and subscription changes are required by Stripe and our terms of service. Everything else can be turned off.

---

## GDPR and CAN-SPAM

Every KompWatch email includes an unsubscribe link. We honor opt-outs within 10 business days (usually immediately). If you believe you received an email in error, contact [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com).

For data deletion requests under GDPR or CCPA, see [GDPR and Data Deletion](./gdpr-data-deletion.md).

# Why Did My Subscription Price Change?

If you received an email or invoice showing a different price than you expected, here's what may have happened and how to get clarity.

---

## Common Reasons for a Price Change

### 1. Billing Cycle Correction
In rare cases, early-access subscribers may have been enrolled on an incorrect Stripe price ID due to a configuration error during our beta period. If your subscription was affected, we will notify you directly via email before any corrected charge is applied.

**What to expect:**
- An email from KompWatch explaining the change and the corrected amount
- At least 7 days' notice before a new charge at the corrected price
- A prorated credit applied automatically if you were undercharged in a prior period

If you believe you were incorrectly charged, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) and we'll review your account immediately.

### 2. Trial Period Ended
Pro and Team plans include a **7-day free trial**. When the trial ends, your card is charged the full plan price. This is the most common reason for an unexpected first charge. See [Free Trial](./free-trial.md) for details.

### 3. Annual Renewal
If you're on an annual plan, your subscription renews once per year at the same locked-in rate. Check your Stripe receipt to confirm the renewal date and amount. You can manage your subscription in [Settings → Manage Subscription](https://kompwatch.com/settings).

### 4. You Upgraded or Changed Your Plan
Upgrades are billed immediately (prorated). If you recently changed plans, the different charge reflects the plan adjustment. See [Billing and Proration](./upgrading-your-plan.md#billing-and-proration).

### 5. Founding Pricing Lock vs. Standard Rate
If you subscribed before all founding slots were claimed, your rate is locked at the founding price — which may differ from the current pricing page. This is intentional and in your favour. See [Founding Member Pricing](./founding-pricing.md).

---

## How to Verify Your Current Price

1. Go to [kompwatch.com/settings](https://kompwatch.com/settings)
2. Click **Manage Subscription** — this opens the Stripe Customer Portal
3. Your current plan, price, and next billing date are shown under **Subscription Details**

Your Stripe receipts are also emailed to you after each charge and accessible in the Customer Portal under **Billing History**.

---

## I Was Charged an Amount I Don't Recognise

Email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) with:
- The charge amount and approximate date
- The last four digits of the card charged (from your bank statement)

We'll investigate within 24 hours. If the charge was in error, we'll issue a full refund.

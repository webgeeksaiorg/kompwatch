# What Is the Comparison Report Offered on Compare Pages?

On some KompWatch comparison pages (e.g. [vs Crayon](https://kompwatch.com/compare/kompwatch-vs-crayon), [vs Klue](https://kompwatch.com/compare/kompwatch-vs-klue)), you may see an offer to receive a **full comparison report** by email. This article explains what it is and what happens when you submit your email.

## What Is the Comparison Report?

The comparison report is a PDF-style summary of the head-to-head comparison between KompWatch and a specific competitor. It expands on what's already on the page and includes:

- **Pricing breakdown** — side-by-side plan tiers, limits, and contract structures
- **Feature matrix** — which capabilities each tool includes (monitoring depth, integrations, digest formats, etc.)
- **Who each tool is best for** — use-case fit by team size and role
- **Switching notes** — what changes if you migrate from the competitor to KompWatch

## Is It Free?

Yes. There is no cost to request the report.

## What Happens After I Enter My Email?

1. You receive the comparison report by email, typically within a few minutes.
2. We may send you occasional product updates about KompWatch. You can unsubscribe at any time from any email we send.

We do not sell or share your email with third parties.

## Why Do I Only See the Form on Some Visits?

KompWatch runs A/B tests on comparison pages to understand what's most helpful for visitors. The email capture form is currently being tested — some visitors see it, others see the standard page layout. This is normal.

## I Submitted My Email but Didn't Receive Anything

Check your spam folder for an email from `hello@kompwatch.com`. If it's not there after 10 minutes, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) and we'll send the report manually.

## Can I Get Reports for Multiple Competitors?

Yes — visit each compare page and submit the form. Each report is specific to that competitor.

# My Login Email Didn't Arrive

CompeteWatch uses **magic links** for login — no password required. You enter your email, we send a one-click login link, and you're in.

## If the email didn't arrive

**1. Check your spam or junk folder**
Magic link emails can occasionally be filtered. Search for "CompeteWatch" or emails from `noreply@kompwatch.com`.

**2. Wait up to 2 minutes**
Email delivery can be delayed. The link is valid for 15 minutes from the time it was sent.

**3. Request a new link**
Go back to the login page and enter your email again. Each new request invalidates previous links.

**4. Check you used the right email**
Make sure you're entering the same email you signed up with. If you're unsure, try any other emails you may have used.

**5. Allowlist our sending domain**
Ask your IT team or email provider to allowlist `kompwatch.com` to ensure future emails arrive reliably.

## The link expired

Magic links expire after **15 minutes** for security. Return to the login page and request a fresh link — it only takes a few seconds.

## I clicked the link but it says "invalid or expired"

Each magic link can only be used once. If you clicked it in a preview pane (some email clients pre-fetch links), it may have been consumed. Request a new link and click it directly in your browser.

## I never signed up but got a link

Someone may have entered your email by mistake. You can safely ignore the email — no account was created and the link will expire. If this happens repeatedly, contact us at support@kompwatch.com.

## Still stuck?

Reply to any CompeteWatch email or write to support@kompwatch.com and a team member will follow up within 24 hours.

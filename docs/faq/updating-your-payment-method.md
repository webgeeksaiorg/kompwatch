# How do I update my credit card or payment method?

You can update your payment method at any time through the Stripe Customer Portal — no need to contact support.

## Steps

1. Log in to your KompWatch account.
2. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
3. Click **Manage Billing** (or **Customer Portal**). This opens your Stripe billing portal in a new tab.
4. Under **Payment methods**, click **Add payment method** or select an existing card and click **Replace**.
5. Enter your new card details and click **Save**.

Your next invoice will automatically charge the new card.

## Notes

- **Multiple cards**: You can add multiple cards in the portal and designate one as the default.
- **PayPal / bank transfer**: Currently, KompWatch only accepts major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover). If you need an alternative payment method, [contact us](mailto:support@kompwatch.com).
- **Failed payment**: If a payment failed before you updated your card, Stripe will automatically retry within 24 hours. You can also trigger a manual retry from the portal under **Invoices**.
- **Security**: KompWatch never stores your card details. All payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1 certified).

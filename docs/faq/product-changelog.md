# Product Changelog & Release Notes

## Where Can I See What's New in KompWatch?

KompWatch maintains a public changelog at [kompwatch.com/changelog](https://kompwatch.com/changelog).

It shows a timeline of all shipped features, improvements, and fixes — most recent first — with plain-English descriptions of what changed and why it matters.

## What Does the Changelog Include?

Each entry includes:
- **Feature name** — what was shipped
- **Short description** — what it does and how to use it
- **Date** — when it was released

Entries are tagged by category (e.g. Dashboard, Integrations, Billing) so you can quickly scan for the areas you care about.

## How Often Is the Changelog Updated?

The changelog is updated whenever a meaningful feature or fix ships to production. We ship continuously, so updates appear frequently.

## Is There a Way to Get Notified of New Features?

Not via the changelog directly. For now, product updates are included in your digest emails. If you'd like a dedicated "what's new" email, reply to any digest and let us know — that's on our radar.

# Dashboard Setup Checklist

When you first sign in to KompWatch, you'll see a **"Get started with KompWatch"** panel at the top of your dashboard. This is the setup checklist — a quick guide to the five activation steps that unlock the most value from competitor monitoring.

---

## What Are the Steps?

The checklist walks you through:

| Step | What it unlocks |
|------|----------------|
| **Add your first competitor** | Starts your first snapshot immediately |
| **Add a second competitor** | Enables cross-competitor trend comparison |
| **Customize your alert threshold** | Filters noise — only get notified for changes that matter |
| **Receive your first digest** | Confirms end-to-end delivery is working |
| **Connect Slack or Teams** *(Pro+ only)* | Real-time alerts via webhook when changes happen |

The progress bar at the top tracks how many steps you've completed.

---

## Why Only 4 Steps for Free Users?

The **Connect Slack or Teams** step requires a webhook, which is a Pro and Team plan feature. If you're on the Free tier, you'll see 4 steps instead of 5. Upgrading to Pro unlocks the fifth step and real-time Slack/Teams alerts.

---

## How Do I Dismiss the Checklist?

Click the **×** button in the top-right corner of the checklist panel. It won't come back after you dismiss it.

If you complete all steps, the checklist automatically closes after a few seconds with a "Setup complete!" confirmation — no manual dismissal needed.

---

## I Dismissed It by Accident — Can I Bring It Back?

Dismiss state is stored in your browser's local storage (not your account). To restore the checklist:

1. Open your browser's developer tools (F12 or Cmd+Option+I)
2. Go to **Application → Local Storage → kompwatch.com**
3. Find the key `kw:onboarding-checklist-dismissed` and delete it
4. Refresh the page

The checklist will reappear if you haven't completed all steps.

> **Note:** If you clear browser data or switch devices, the checklist will reappear automatically until all steps are complete.

---

## The Checklist Disappeared — Did I Miss Something?

If the checklist vanishes without you dismissing it, it means all steps were completed and it auto-closed. You can verify on your dashboard:

- **Competitors added:** [/competitors](https://kompwatch.com/competitors)
- **Alert threshold set:** Settings → Notifications → Minimum severity
- **Digest received:** [/digests](https://kompwatch.com/digests)
- **Webhook configured:** Settings → Notifications → Webhook URL *(Pro+ only)*

---

## What If I Don't Want to Complete All the Steps?

You don't have to — dismiss the checklist at any time. KompWatch works fine with just one competitor and default settings. The checklist is a suggestion, not a requirement.

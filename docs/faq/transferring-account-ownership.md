# Transferring Account Ownership

Account ownership determines who controls the KompWatch subscription, billing, and team settings. If an employee leaves or your team structure changes, you can transfer ownership to another person.

## Who Can Transfer Ownership?

Only the current **account owner** (the person whose email is on the Stripe subscription) can initiate a transfer. If the original owner is no longer reachable, see [What if the Original Owner is Gone?](#what-if-the-original-owner-is-gone) below.

## How to Transfer Ownership

Account ownership transfer is a manual process (no self-serve option yet). Contact [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) with:

1. **Your current account email** (the owner's email)
2. **The new owner's email address**
3. **Confirmation** that you authorize the transfer (reply from the current owner's email address)

We'll update the account email and Stripe billing contact within 1 business day.

> If the account is on a Team plan, the new owner will automatically inherit admin access. All team members, competitors, and data stay intact.

## What Changes After Transfer

| Item | What Happens |
|------|-------------|
| Login email | Updated to new owner's email |
| Stripe billing email | Updated — invoices go to new owner |
| Competitors and data | Unchanged — all history is preserved |
| Team members | Unchanged — they retain their access |
| API keys / webhooks | Unchanged — no reconfiguration needed |

## What if the Original Owner is Gone?

If the original owner is no longer at your company and you can't access their email:

1. **Email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com)** from your company domain.
2. Provide: the account's company name, the original owner's email (if known), and proof of your authority (e.g., you're the billing contact, or you can forward the original Stripe receipt).
3. We'll verify ownership through alternative means and work with you to regain access.

This typically takes 1–2 business days.

## Can I Have Multiple Owners?

Not currently. There is one account owner, but the Team plan allows up to 10 members with equal access to all features (except billing settings, which are owner-only).

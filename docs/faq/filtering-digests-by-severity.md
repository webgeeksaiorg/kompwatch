# Filtering Your Digest History by Severity

The Digests page (`/digests`) has a built-in severity filter that lets you focus your digest history on the changes that matter most — without changing any notification settings.

---

## How to Use It

On the [Digests page](https://kompwatch.com/digests), look for the severity filter bar near the top of the list. Click any severity level to toggle it on:

- **Low** — minor cosmetic changes
- **Medium** — meaningful updates worth tracking
- **High** — significant strategic moves
- **Critical** — seismic events (acquisitions, major pivots, drastic price cuts)

You can select multiple levels at once (e.g. **High + Critical** only). Active filters are highlighted; click again to deselect.

When a filter is active:
- Only digests that contain at least one matching change are shown
- The change count on each digest card reflects the filtered set
- The preview snippet shows only matching changes

---

## Clearing the Filter

Click a highlighted severity level to deselect it, or reload the page without query parameters. The URL reflects your active filter (e.g. `?severity=HIGH&severity=CRITICAL`) — you can bookmark filtered views or share them with teammates.

---

## How Is This Different from the Notification Threshold in Settings?

| Setting | What it controls |
|---|---|
| **Settings → Notifications → Severity threshold** | Which changes are included in your *email digest* going forward (global, permanent) |
| **Digests page → Severity filter** | How your existing digest *history* is displayed in the app (per-session, no emails affected) |

The page-level filter is non-destructive: it only changes what you see on screen. Your email digests and stored change history are unaffected.

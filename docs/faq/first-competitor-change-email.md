# Why Did I Receive a "First Competitor Change Detected" Email?

When KompWatch detects the **first change** on any of your monitored competitors, we send a one-time activation email to let you know that monitoring is working end-to-end.

This email is intentionally separate from your regular digest — it's a confirmation that:

1. Your competitor has been successfully crawled at least twice
2. A difference was detected between snapshots
3. KompWatch's change detection and email delivery are both working for your account

---

## What Does the Email Contain?

The email includes:

- The competitor name and URL where the change was detected
- A brief AI-generated summary of what changed (e.g., pricing copy, feature list, navigation)
- The severity level: LOW, MEDIUM, or HIGH
- A link to view the full change diff in your dashboard at [/digests](https://kompwatch.com/digests)

---

## Will I Keep Getting These Emails?

No — this is a **one-time email per account**. It is sent the first time any change is detected, and is not repeated.

Ongoing change alerts are delivered via your regular digest schedule (weekly on Free, daily on Pro, real-time on Team). For real-time Slack or Teams notifications, see [Integrations and Notifications](./integrations-and-notifications.md).

---

## I Got the Email But Don't See Anything in My Dashboard

A few things to check:

1. **Dashboard refresh** — reload [/digests](https://kompwatch.com/digests) or [/competitors](https://kompwatch.com/competitors)
2. **Change may be LOW severity** — LOW severity changes appear in the dashboard but may be filtered in your notification settings. Go to **Settings → Notifications → Minimum severity** to adjust.
3. **Email delay vs. dashboard** — the email and dashboard entry are created at the same time; if there's a short delay, try refreshing in 1–2 minutes.

If the change still isn't visible after a few minutes, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com).

---

## I Don't Want These Notification Emails

To manage your notification preferences:

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications**
2. Adjust which severity levels trigger alerts
3. Or disable email digest delivery entirely if you prefer dashboard-only monitoring

Note: The first-change activation email cannot be individually disabled — it is sent once as an account health confirmation.

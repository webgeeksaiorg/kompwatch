# Annual Plan Billing and Savings

## Does KompWatch offer annual billing?

Yes. KompWatch offers annual billing on Pro and Team plans, giving you **20% off** compared to paying month-to-month.

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (per month) | Annual savings |
|------|---------|-------------------|----------------|
| Pro  | $49/mo  | $39.20/mo         | ~$118/year     |
| Team | $149/mo | $119.20/mo        | ~$358/year     |

Annual plans are billed as a single payment upfront.

---

## How do I switch from monthly to annual billing?

1. Go to **Settings → Billing** in your KompWatch dashboard.
2. Click **Manage Subscription** to open the Stripe Customer Portal.
3. Select your current plan and choose **Switch to Annual**.
4. Confirm the change — Stripe will prorate any unused time on your current monthly billing cycle and charge the annual amount minus that credit.

---

## Can I switch back from annual to monthly?

Yes, but the switch takes effect at the end of your current annual period — you won't receive a partial refund for unused months. You can toggle this at any time in the Stripe Customer Portal.

---

## What happens if I upgrade my plan mid-year?

If you're on an annual Pro plan and upgrade to Team, Stripe prorates the difference: you pay the remaining months at the Team rate minus what you've already paid for the Pro annual period. You won't be charged for a full new year.

---

## Is annual billing available on the Free tier?

No. The Free tier is always month-to-month (and costs $0), so annual billing doesn't apply.

---

## Will I be reminded before my annual plan renews?

Yes. Stripe sends a renewal reminder email **7 days before** your annual plan renews. You'll also see the renewal date in **Settings → Billing**.

---

## Can I get a refund if I cancel mid-year?

KompWatch does not offer prorated refunds for unused months on annual plans, except in the first 30 days. If you cancel within 30 days of starting an annual plan, contact support and we'll issue a full refund.

After 30 days, you retain access until the end of the annual period.

---

## Do annual plans include all the same features as monthly?

Yes. Annual billing is purely a payment frequency choice — it doesn't change which features, competitor slots, or snapshot frequency you have access to. You get the same Pro or Team feature set either way.

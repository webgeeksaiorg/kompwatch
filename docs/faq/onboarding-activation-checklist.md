# What Is the Onboarding Checklist on My Dashboard?

When you first sign in to KompWatch, a **Get started with KompWatch** checklist appears at the top of your dashboard. It walks you through three quick setup steps to make sure you're getting the most out of competitor monitoring.

---

## The Three Steps

### 1. Add your first competitor
Paste a URL — a competitor's pricing page, features page, or homepage — and KompWatch starts monitoring it immediately. Your first snapshot runs right away; change detection begins after the second snapshot completes (see [Why Don't I See Any Changes Yet?](why-no-changes-yet.md)).

→ Click **Add competitor** or go to [/competitors](https://kompwatch.com/competitors).

### 2. Set your alert threshold
Choose which severity levels appear in your digest — HIGH only, MEDIUM and above, or all changes including LOW. Setting this early keeps your inbox clean from day one.

→ Click **Configure alerts** or go to [/settings](https://kompwatch.com/settings). See [Managing Alert Fatigue](managing-alert-fatigue.md) for guidance on choosing the right threshold.

### 3. Connect Slack or Teams
Add a webhook URL to receive real-time alerts in your Slack channel or Microsoft Teams when a competitor changes. This step is optional but highly recommended — most teams get more value from real-time alerts than from email digests alone.

→ Click **Set up webhook** or go to [/settings](https://kompwatch.com/settings). See [Integrations and Notifications](integrations-and-notifications.md) for step-by-step instructions.

---

## Progress Bar

The checklist shows a progress bar and a `{completed}/{total}` counter. As you complete each step, the item is checked off and the bar advances.

When all three steps are done, **the checklist disappears automatically**.

---

## Can I Dismiss It?

Yes — click the **×** button in the top-right corner of the checklist to hide it at any time. You can still complete the steps by navigating directly to [/competitors](https://kompwatch.com/competitors) and [/settings](https://kompwatch.com/settings).

---

## Why Only Three Steps?

These are the three actions most strongly correlated with getting value from KompWatch in the first week:

1. **A competitor** gives the system something to monitor.
2. **An alert threshold** prevents digest noise that causes users to ignore alerts.
3. **A Slack/Teams webhook** ensures changes reach you where your team already works.

If you want to go deeper — CSS selectors, multiple pages per competitor, digest scheduling — see [Getting Started](getting-started.md) for the full guide.

# How to Cancel or Change Your Plan

You can manage your subscription entirely through the Stripe Customer Portal — no need to contact support.

## Access the Customer Portal

1. Go to [kompwatch.com/settings](https://kompwatch.com/settings).
2. Click **Manage Subscription**.
3. You'll be redirected to the Stripe Customer Portal.

## What You Can Do in the Portal

- **Upgrade** from Free → Pro → Team
- **Downgrade** your plan (takes effect at end of billing period)
- **Cancel** your subscription (access continues until period end)
- **Download invoices** and view billing history
- **Update payment method**

## Cancellation Details

- Cancellations take effect at the **end of your current billing period** — you keep access until then.
- You will **not** be charged again after cancellation.
- Your competitor data and snapshots are retained for 30 days after cancellation.
- We do not offer pro-rated refunds for partial months, but if you have a billing concern please [contact us](mailto:support@kompwatch.com).

## Downgrade Limits

If you downgrade and have more competitors than your new plan allows, you'll be prompted to deactivate some before the change takes effect.

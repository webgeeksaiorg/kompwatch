# Can I Pause My Subscription Without Canceling?

KompWatch does not currently offer a formal subscription pause feature through the Stripe portal. If you need to step away temporarily, here are your best options depending on the situation.

---

## Option 1: Downgrade to Free (Recommended)

If you want to maintain your account and resume tracking quickly later, **downgrade to the Free plan** instead of canceling. This keeps your account active at $0/month.

- Your competitor list is preserved (you keep the first 2 active; extras are paused automatically)
- Your snapshot history and change data are retained
- You can upgrade back to Pro or Team at any time and resume where you left off
- No re-signup or data loss

> ⚠️ **Founding Customer rate:** If you're on the $29/mo founding price, downgrading to Free ends your Pro subscription and **permanently forfeits the founding rate**. When you upgrade back to Pro, you'll pay the standard rate. See [Founding Customer Program](./founding-pricing.md) before downgrading.

To downgrade:
1. Go to [kompwatch.com/settings](https://kompwatch.com/settings)
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. Select **Free plan** in the Stripe Customer Portal

See [Cancel or Change Your Plan →](./cancel-or-change-plan.md)

---

## Option 2: Cancel and Resubscribe

If you'd prefer to stop billing entirely:

- Cancel via **Settings → Manage Subscription**
- Access continues through the end of your current billing period
- Your competitor data and snapshot history are **retained for 30 days** after your subscription ends
- When you're ready to return, sign up again at [kompwatch.com/pricing](https://kompwatch.com/pricing) — your email will be recognized and your account reactivated

> **Note:** If you resubscribe after 30 days, snapshot history from before your cancellation may no longer be available.

> ⚠️ **Founding Customer rate:** If your subscription is at the $29/mo founding price, **cancelling permanently forfeits that rate**. A new subscription will be charged at the standard rate at that time. See [Founding Customer Program — cancellation details](./founding-pricing.md) before proceeding.

---

## Option 3: Pause Individual Competitors

If you're going quiet on specific competitors (not your whole account), **pause them individually** rather than your plan:

1. Go to [kompwatch.com/competitors](https://kompwatch.com/competitors)
2. Click **Pause** on any competitor row
3. Snapshots stop immediately — no alerts, no digest entries, no data changes
4. Resume at any time with one click

This is useful during a competitor's known redesign, a quiet period, or when you're focused on just a subset of rivals. See [Per-Competitor Notification Settings →](./per-competitor-notification-settings.md)

---

## Can I Get a Credit for Unused Time?

We don't offer pro-rated credits for unused time within a billing period. If you have a specific billing concern, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) and we'll review on a case-by-case basis.

# I Can't Add More Competitors — Plan Limit Reached

If you try to add a new competitor and see a message like "You've reached your plan's competitor limit," you have three options: free a slot, upgrade, or pause existing competitors.

---

## What Are the Limits?

| Plan | Max Competitors |
|------|----------------|
| Free | 2 |
| Pro  | 10 |
| Team | 50 |

---

## Option 1: Pause a Competitor to Free a Slot

Pausing stops new snapshots without deleting any historical data — you keep all your change history.

1. Go to [kompwatch.com/competitors](https://kompwatch.com/competitors)
2. Click **Pause** on a competitor you don't need to monitor right now
3. Try adding the new competitor again

A paused competitor counts against your limit. Once paused, click **Resume** at any time to start monitoring it again.

---

## Option 2: Delete a Competitor You No Longer Need

> **This is permanent.** Deleting a competitor removes all its snapshots and change history with no recovery. Use Pause if you might want the history later.

1. Go to [kompwatch.com/competitors](https://kompwatch.com/competitors)
2. Click **Remove** on the competitor row
3. Confirm the deletion prompt

---

## Option 3: Upgrade Your Plan

If you regularly hit your limit, upgrading gives you more slots and faster snapshot frequency:

- **Pro ($49/mo)** — 10 competitors, snapshots every 6 hours
- **Team ($149/mo)** — 50 competitors, snapshots every hour

To upgrade: go to [kompwatch.com/settings](https://kompwatch.com/settings) → **Manage Subscription**.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does a paused competitor count toward my limit?**
Yes. Pausing stops monitoring but the competitor still occupies a slot. To fully free a slot, you need to delete the competitor.

**What if I'm on the Free plan and need 3 competitors?**
Upgrade to Pro. You can start a Pro trial directly from [kompwatch.com/pricing](https://kompwatch.com/pricing).

**Can I exceed the limit temporarily?**
No — the limit is enforced at the API level. You must free a slot or upgrade before adding a new competitor.

**I deleted a competitor by accident — can I recover the data?**
Deleted data cannot be recovered. Add the URL again to start monitoring fresh; KompWatch will begin a new snapshot history from that point.

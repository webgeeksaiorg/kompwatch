# HeadsUp vs KompWatch — Which Competitor Intelligence Tool Is Right for You?

**Last updated:** 2026-06-09

---

## Quick answer

HeadsUp and KompWatch solve different problems. HeadsUp focuses on product-led growth (PLG) signals inside your own product — surfacing which of your *existing customers* are at risk of churning or ready to expand. KompWatch monitors your *competitors' public presence* — detecting price changes, feature launches, messaging shifts, and web changes before they affect your pipeline.

If you're asking "which of my accounts needs attention today?", you want HeadsUp.  
If you're asking "what is Competitor X doing that I need to respond to?", you want KompWatch.

---

## What HeadsUp does

HeadsUp is a PLG CRM that ingests product usage data (Segment, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.) and surfaces expansion and churn signals to your sales and CS teams. It helps revenue teams act on behavioral data from *users who already have accounts*.

**HeadsUp is great for:**
- Identifying expansion-ready accounts based on usage patterns
- Flagging churn risk before renewal conversations
- Routing PQL (product-qualified lead) signals to sales
- Aligning CS and sales on customer health scores

**HeadsUp is not designed for:**
- Monitoring competitors' public websites
- Detecting when a rival changes their pricing page
- Alerting you to competitor blog posts, press releases, or feature announcements
- Competitive intelligence for sales battlecards or marketing positioning

---

## What KompWatch does

KompWatch is a competitive intelligence tool that tracks competitors' public footprints — websites, pricing pages, feature pages, blog posts — and delivers AI-generated digests of meaningful changes.

**KompWatch is great for:**
- Knowing when a competitor changes their pricing before your next sales call
- Detecting feature launches that could affect your roadmap conversations
- Tracking messaging and positioning shifts (e.g., "they just added 'AI-powered' to their hero")
- Automated weekly or daily digests so your team stays aligned without manual checking
- Lightweight setup: no SDK, no data pipeline, just a URL and a CSS selector

**KompWatch is not designed for:**
- Analyzing your own product usage data
- Surfacing customer health signals
- CRM or CS workflow automation

## Side-by-side comparison

| Dimension | HeadsUp | KompWatch |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary use case** | PLG signals / customer health | Competitor website monitoring |
| **Data source** | Your own product telemetry | Competitors' public web pages |
| **Main user** | Sales, CS, RevOps | Product, Marketing, Sales (CI) |
| **Setup complexity** | Requires data integration (Segment, etc.) | URL + CSS selector — 5 min setup |

| **AI output** | Customer health scores, PQL alerts | Change digests, severity flags |
| **Overlap** | None | None |

---

## Do I need both?

Yes, for most SaaS teams these tools are complementary, not competing:

- **HeadsUp** tells you which of your accounts to call this week.
- **KompWatch** tells you what your competitor just changed so you're prepared when you make that call.

A common workflow: KompWatch detects that a competitor reduced their price → your sales team is alerted via digest → HeadsUp shows you which accounts are up for renewal in the next 30 days → reps reach out proactively with updated competitive positioning.

---

## When to choose KompWatch over HeadsUp

- You need to monitor competitor pricing, features, or messaging
- Your team manually checks competitor websites today and wants to automate it
- You're a small team (1–10 people) who can't afford enterprise CI tools
- You want a quick setup with no data engineering requirements
- You're currently using Klue, Crayon, or Kompyte and find them too expensive

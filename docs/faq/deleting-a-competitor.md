# Deleting a Competitor

You can remove a competitor from your KompWatch account at any time. Deletion is permanent — it removes the competitor and all associated snapshot history and detected changes.

---

## How to Delete a Competitor

1. Go to [kompwatch.com/competitors](https://kompwatch.com/competitors)
2. Click the **⋯** menu on the competitor's row (or open the competitor detail page)
3. Select **Delete competitor**
4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog

Snapshots stop immediately and no further alerts will be sent for that competitor.

---

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete a competitor, the following are **permanently removed**:

| Data | Deleted? |
|------|----------|
| Competitor name and URL | Yes |
| All snapshots (HTML, screenshots) | Yes |
| All detected changes | Yes |
| References to this competitor in past digests | Digest emails already sent are unaffected — only the live feed is updated |

> **This action is irreversible.** There is no recycle bin or undo. If you want to stop monitoring temporarily while keeping your history, **pause the competitor instead** (see below).

---

## Pause vs. Delete — Which Should I Use?

| Situation | Use |
|-----------|-----|
| Taking a break from a competitor — going quiet for a sprint, quarter, or event | **Pause** |
| Competitor shut down, acquired, or no longer relevant | **Delete** |
| At your plan's competitor limit and need to make room | **Pause** (or delete if history isn't needed) |
| Competitor rebranded to a new URL | **Delete** the old entry, **add** the new URL |

**To pause instead of delete:**
1. Go to [kompwatch.com/competitors](https://kompwatch.com/competitors)
2. Click **Pause** on the competitor row
3. Monitoring stops; snapshot history is preserved
4. Resume any time with one click

See [Pausing Your Subscription and Individual Competitors →](./pausing-your-subscription.md)

---

## Does Deleting Free Up a Competitor Slot?

Yes. Once a competitor is deleted (or paused), that slot is immediately available for a new competitor within your plan's limit.

- **Free plan:** 2 competitor slots
- **Pro plan:** 10 competitor slots
- **Team plan:** 50 competitor slots

See [Plan Competitor Limit Reached →](./plan-competitor-limit-reached.md) if you've hit your limit.

---

## Can I Re-Add a Deleted Competitor?

Yes — you can add the same URL again at any time. KompWatch will treat it as a new competitor and begin fresh from the next snapshot cycle. Historical data from before the deletion will **not** be restored.

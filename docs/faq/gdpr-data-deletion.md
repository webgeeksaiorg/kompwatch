# GDPR, Privacy & Data Deletion

CompeteWatch takes data privacy seriously. This page explains your rights and how to exercise them.

## Your Data Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

As a CompeteWatch user, you have the right to:

- **Access** — Request a copy of all data we hold about you
- **Rectification** — Correct inaccurate personal data
- **Erasure** — Request deletion of your account and all associated data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Portability** — Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection** — Object to specific processing activities

## What Data We Store

- Account: email address, name, creation date
- Subscription: Stripe customer ID, plan, billing status
- Competitor monitoring: URLs you track, snapshots, detected changes
- Email: digest history sent to your address

We do **not** store payment card details — all payment data is handled by Stripe.

## How to Request Data Deletion

Email [privacy@kompwatch.com](mailto:privacy@kompwatch.com) with:
- Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
- Your account email address

We will:
1. Confirm your identity
2. Delete your account, competitors, snapshots, changes, and digests
3. Remove your email from all mailing lists
4. Confirm deletion within **30 days** (typically within 5 business days)

Note: Some data may be retained in backups for up to 30 days after deletion, then permanently purged.

## Data Portability

To request an export of your data, email [privacy@kompwatch.com](mailto:privacy@kompwatch.com) with subject "Data Export Request". We'll provide a JSON export within 30 days.

## Contact

For privacy inquiries: [privacy@kompwatch.com](mailto:privacy@kompwatch.com)

Our privacy policy is available at [kompwatch.com/privacy](https://kompwatch.com/privacy).

# Klue Migration Reimbursement — Up to $500

KompWatch will reimburse your Klue early-termination fee — up to $500 — when you switch to a paid plan. This offer runs through Q3 2026, targeted at teams evaluating alternatives during Klue's post-layoff churn window.

## Who Is Eligible?

You qualify if:

- You have an active Klue annual contract with an early-termination fee
- You sign up for KompWatch **Pro** ($49/mo) or **Team** ($149/mo) — the Free tier does not qualify
- You cancel Klue and provide proof of the cancellation fee
- You claim within the offer window: **through Q3 2026**
- **One reimbursement per company** — not per user or per seat

## How to Claim

1. **Sign up and upgrade.** Go to [kompwatch.com](https://kompwatch.com), create an account, and start a Pro or Team plan. We issue refunds through Stripe, so a paid plan is required before we can process the reimbursement.

2. **Cancel Klue and get written confirmation.** Request a cancellation confirmation from Klue that shows the early-termination fee amount. This is typically emailed from their billing or CS team.

3. **Email us proof.** Forward the Klue cancellation confirmation to [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com?subject=Klue%20reimbursement%20claim) with the subject line **"Klue reimbursement claim"**. Include:
   - The cancellation confirmation showing the fee
   - Your KompWatch account email

4. **Receive your refund.** Within 5 business days, we apply a Stripe refund to your KompWatch invoice for the cancellation amount — capped at $500.

## What If My Termination Fee Exceeds $500?

The reimbursement is capped at $500. If your early-termination fee is higher, we cover $500 and you cover the remainder. For fees well above $500, it may make sense to wait until your natural renewal date rather than incurring the full termination fee — use KompWatch's free tier (2 competitors) to evaluate in parallel first.

## What If Klue Doesn't Charge a Termination Fee?

Many Klue contracts do not have explicit early-termination fees — the obligation is simply to pay through the end of the contract term. In that case, there's no fee to reimburse. The reimbursement applies specifically to fees charged for ending the contract before its natural expiry.

## How Long Does the Refund Take?

Refunds are processed via Stripe within **5 business days** of receiving your cancellation proof. Stripe typically posts the refund to your card within 5–10 business days after we initiate it, depending on your bank.

## Can I Still Get the Free Plan First?

Yes — and that's the recommended approach. Start on the Free tier (2 competitors, no credit card) to evaluate KompWatch alongside Klue before committing. Upgrade to Pro when you're ready to claim the reimbursement. The reimbursement is processed after your first paid month.

The offer runs through **Q3 2026** (expires September 30, 2026). Check [/migrate/from-klue](https://kompwatch.com/migrate/from-klue) for the current status.

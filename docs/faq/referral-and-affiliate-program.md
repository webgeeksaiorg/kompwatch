# Does KompWatch Have a Referral or Affiliate Program?

Not yet — but it's on the roadmap. Here's where things stand and how you can help in the meantime.

## Current Status

KompWatch does not have a formal referral or affiliate program today. We're a small, focused team pre-launch, and a proper program requires payout infrastructure, fraud controls, and legal agreements we haven't shipped yet.

If you want to be notified the day a referral program launches, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) with the subject line "Referral program" — we'll add you to the early-access list.

## Can I Still Share KompWatch?

Yes — and we'd genuinely appreciate it. A few things that help:

- **Share a comparison page** — if a colleague is evaluating Klue, Crayon, or Kompyte, links like [/vs/klue](/vs/klue) or [/switching-from/kompyte](/switching-from/kompyte) show the tradeoff clearly.
- **Forward your digest** — if a change in your digest is relevant to a teammate, forward it. The digest is plain-text friendly and easy to read out of context.
- **Mention us in community discussions** — if someone in a Slack group or LinkedIn thread asks about competitor monitoring tools, a honest mention goes a long way.

There's no tracking or reward yet — just the good feeling of connecting someone with a tool that helps them.

## What Would a Referral Program Look Like?

When we do ship one, we're aiming for something simple:

- A unique referral link tied to your account
- Credit applied when a referred user subscribes (not just signs up)
- Transparent payouts — likely account credit or a small cash payout via Stripe

We'll announce it on the [product changelog](/changelog) and via email to existing users.

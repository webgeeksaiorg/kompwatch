# Can I Use KompWatch for Win/Loss Analysis?

Win/loss analysis helps you understand why deals close — or don't. KompWatch's competitor monitoring gives you the real-time context that turns raw win/loss data into actionable strategy.

## How KompWatch Supports Win/Loss Workflows

### 1. Know What Changed Before the Deal Closed

When a sales rep marks a deal as "Lost to Klue," the first question should be: *did anything change on Klue's side recently?*

KompWatch tracks competitor websites continuously, so you can look back at the change history for any competitor and see exactly what they updated — pricing, messaging, features, case studies — in the weeks before the deal closed.

Go to **Competitors → [Competitor Name] → Change History** to review recent changes with AI-generated summaries.

### 2. Correlate Pricing Changes With Loss Spikes

If you start losing more deals to a competitor, check whether they recently changed their pricing page. KompWatch flags pricing-zone changes with HIGH severity and sends an instant alert.

You can filter your change history by `changeType: PRICING` to see a timeline of every pricing-related update KompWatch has detected.

### 3. Feed Win/Loss Insights Back Into Battlecards

Use the **AI Digest** summaries in KompWatch to keep your battlecards current. When a competitor updates their positioning or adds a new feature, KompWatch surfaces it — and your battlecards should reflect it within days, not quarters.

See also: [Creating Sales Battlecards](/docs/faq/creating-sales-battlecards.md)

### 4. Share Context With Revenue Teams

KompWatch digests can be shared with your full go-to-market team (sales, marketing, CS) via the **Share Digest** feature on any digest card. This ensures win/loss debrief conversations start with current competitive context, not stale assumptions.

See also: [Sharing Digests With Your Team](/docs/faq/sharing-digests-with-your-team.md)

## What KompWatch Doesn't Do (Yet)

KompWatch monitors publicly visible competitor content. It does not:

- Record CRM data or deal outcomes
- Integrate directly with HubSpot, Salesforce, or Gong (this is on our roadmap — [upvote it here](https://kompwatch.com/feedback))
- Interview lost prospects or analyze call recordings

For full win/loss programs, teams often pair KompWatch with tools like Clozd, Wynter, or UserGems. KompWatch provides the "what changed on the competitor's side" layer that those tools often miss.

## Quick Recipe: Pre-Deal Win/Loss Check

1. Add your top 3–5 competitors to KompWatch
2. When a deal closes (win or loss), open the competitor's change history
3. Filter by the last 30–60 days
4. Look for HIGH-severity changes in pricing, features, or positioning
5. Add findings to your deal notes / CRM

Over time, you'll start to see patterns: which competitor moves correlate with deal losses, and which of your win stories map to competitor weaknesses you can exploit.

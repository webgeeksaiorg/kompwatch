## Day-by-Day Migration Checklist

### Days 1–2: Import your competitor list

1. Pull your competitor URLs from your old tool (Klue battlecards, Kompyte/Semrush dashboard, Crayon competitor profiles).
2. Add them to KompWatch: **Dashboard → Add Competitor**.
   - Add the **specific pages** that matter: `/pricing`, `/features`, `/changelog`, not just the homepage.
   - Set a CSS selector to narrow scope (e.g. `.pricing-table`, `#features-section`). See [Which Pages to Monitor Per Competitor](./which-pages-to-monitor-per-competitor.md).
3. KompWatch starts your first snapshot immediately. Changes are detected on the **second** snapshot.

> **Tip:** Use bulk-add if you have 5+ competitors. See [Bulk Importing Competitors](./bulk-importing-competitors.md).

### Days 3–5: Compare digest quality side-by-side

- Your first daily digest arrives after 24 hours (Day 2 at the latest).
- Run KompWatch and your old tool in **parallel** — don't cancel anything yet.
- Look for: Did KompWatch catch a pricing change or feature update your old tool missed? Is the AI summary more actionable than your current alerts?

### Days 6–7: Make your decision

- If KompWatch is giving you cleaner signals at a fraction of the cost → add a card in **Settings → Manage Subscription** to activate your paid plan seamlessly.
- If you need more time → contact [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com). We can extend your trial by a few days if you're mid-evaluation.
- If it's not a fit → do nothing. Your account reverts to the **Free plan** automatically (2 competitors, daily snapshots). No charge, no hassle.

## What Happens to Your Data After the Trial?

- **If you convert:** all competitors, snapshots, and 7 days of change history carry over automatically. Nothing is lost.
- **If you don't convert:** your competitors are preserved on the Free plan (up to your 2-competitor limit). Additional competitors are paused (not deleted) and reactivate the moment you upgrade.

## Do I Need to Cancel My Old Tool Before the Trial Ends?

No — and you shouldn't. Run both tools in parallel during your trial. Cancel your old tool **at its next renewal date**, not mid-contract. Most Klue, Kompyte, and Crayon contracts bill annually; check your renewal date and set a calendar reminder 30 days out.

## Will the Trial Interrupt If I Don't Add a Card?

No. The trial runs for 7 full days regardless. At the end:

- **Card on file** → subscription activates, billed at $49/mo (Pro) or your chosen plan rate.
- **No card** → account reverts to Free plan. No charge.

You can add or remove a card at any time during the trial in **Settings → Manage Subscription**.

## Common Questions from Migrators

**"I have 20 competitors in Klue — can I monitor all of them?"**
Pro supports 10 competitors; Team supports 50. If you're tracking a large portfolio, consider the [Team plan](./team-plan.md) or contact us — we can discuss a custom limit.

**"Kompyte was buried inside Semrush and I couldn't use it separately. Is KompWatch standalone?"**
Yes. KompWatch is a purpose-built competitor monitoring tool — not bundled into an SEO suite. You pay $49/mo for CI, nothing else. See [Why Kompyte Costs More Now](./switching-from-kompyte.md).

**"Will I lose my Crayon battlecard history if I switch?"**
KompWatch doesn't import Crayon battlecard data — that lives in Crayon's format. For change history going forward, KompWatch starts building from Day 1. Export your existing Crayon battlecards before your contract ends.

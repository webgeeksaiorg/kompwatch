# Which KompWatch Plan Is Right for Me?

The short answer: start free, upgrade when you hit a limit.

## Quick decision guide

| If you… | Recommended plan |
|---------|-----------------|
| Want to try KompWatch or track 1–2 competitors | **Free** |
| Track up to 10 competitors, need daily digests | **Pro ($49/mo)** |
| Track 11–50 competitors or need hourly snapshots | **Team ($149/mo)** |
| Need SSO, an SLA, a dedicated account manager, or 50+ competitors | **Enterprise** |

## Feature differences that matter most

### Free → Pro jump

The three reasons most teams upgrade from Free to Pro:

1. **More competitors** — Free allows 2; Pro allows 10
2. **Faster snapshots** — Free checks once per day; Pro checks every 6 hours
3. **Pro-only features** — Job listing tracking, tech stack detection, Slack/webhook alerts, battlecard export, and competitor activity scores are Pro and above

### Pro → Team jump

- **50 competitors** (vs 10 on Pro)
- **Hourly snapshots** — critical if you're in a market where pricing changes or feature launches happen fast
- **API access** — use KompWatch data in your own dashboards or data pipelines

### Team → Enterprise

Enterprise is for organizations that need:
- **Unlimited competitors**
- **Real-time monitoring and digest delivery**
- **SSO & SAML** (required by most IT/security teams)
- **SLA and uptime guarantee**
- **Dedicated account manager + custom onboarding**

[Contact us](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) for Enterprise pricing.

## Can I change plans later?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade any time from [Settings → Manage Subscription](https://kompwatch.com/settings). Changes take effect immediately; Stripe prorates billing automatically.

> **Downgrade note:** If you have more competitors than the new plan's limit, you'll need to remove some before the downgrade applies.

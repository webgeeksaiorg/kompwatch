# Can I Extend My Free Trial?

KompWatch offers a **7-day free trial** on Pro and Team plans. If you need more time to evaluate, here's what's possible.

## Can I Get a Trial Extension?

Yes — in most cases, we're happy to extend your trial. Email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) before your trial ends and include:

- Your account email
- Why you need more time (e.g. "I'm waiting for a budget approval cycle" or "I've been out sick and haven't had a chance to properly test it")

**We typically grant 3–7 additional days** for genuine evaluation requests. We don't grant extensions after the trial has expired — reach out before it ends.

## My Trial Ended Before I Could Evaluate Properly

If your trial has already ended and you've reverted to the Free plan, you can still upgrade to a paid plan and the first billing cycle will give you access to all features immediately.

However, if you genuinely didn't have time to evaluate, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) — we handle these case by case. In many situations, we can manually apply a trial extension even for recently expired trials (within ~48 hours of expiry).

## Do I Need a Credit Card for the Trial?

No. KompWatch's trial does not require a credit card. You get full access to the Pro or Team plan for 7 days with no payment details.

If you added a card and want to cancel before being charged, see [Cancel or Change Plan](./cancel-or-change-plan.md).

## What Happens at the End of the Trial?

| Scenario | What happens |
|---|---|
| No card added | Account reverts to Free plan (2 competitors, daily snapshots). No charge. |
| Card added, didn't cancel | Subscription activates at the plan rate. You'll receive a receipt. |
| Card added, cancelled during trial | Subscription does not activate. No charge. |

Your data (competitors, snapshots, change history) is preserved regardless of which path you take.

## I Signed Up With the Wrong Plan for My Trial

If you started a Pro trial but actually need Team features (or vice versa), email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com). We can switch your trial to the correct plan without resetting the clock.

## Can I Trial with My Full Team?

On a Team plan trial, you can invite up to the Team plan's seat limit during the trial period. All team members get full access. See [Inviting Team Members](./inviting-team-members.md).

If you need to evaluate with a larger group than the Team plan supports, contact [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) — we can discuss enterprise evaluation arrangements.

## What Can I Do During the Trial to Get the Most Out of It?

The best way to evaluate KompWatch is to run it against real competitors for at least 3–4 full snapshot cycles:

1. **Add 3–5 real competitors** (not test URLs) including their pricing and feature pages
2. **Wait 24–48 hours** — you need at least a few snapshot cycles to see what KompWatch catches
3. **Check your first digest** — the AI summary is the main product value; don't judge by the raw change feed alone
4. **Set up one integration** (Slack or webhook) to see the real-time alert flow on Team

See the [Onboarding Activation Checklist](./onboarding-activation-checklist.md) for a step-by-step guide.

# I'm on the Free Plan — When Should I Upgrade to Pro?

The Free plan is a real plan, not a trial countdown. You can stay on it indefinitely. But there are specific signals that tell you when you've outgrown it — and when Pro pays for itself almost immediately.

---

| Battlecard export | ✗ | ✓ |
| Activity scores | ✗ | ✓ |

---

## Upgrade When You Hit One of These Signals

### 1. You've hit your 2-competitor limit

The most common reason. If you have more than two competitors worth watching — and most teams do — the Free plan forces you to choose. You'll miss changes on the ones you didn't add.

Pro gives you 10 competitors, which covers most small and mid-size SaaS teams completely.

### 2. You find out about a competitor change too late

Free snapshots run once a day, and digests only go out weekly. If a competitor changes their pricing on Monday and your weekly digest is Saturday, you've been blind for six days.

On Pro, snapshots run every 6 hours and digests land daily — so you catch a pricing change within hours, not after the weekend.

### 3. You're losing deals where a competitor changed something you didn't know about

If a sales rep has been pushing back on pricing because a competitor quietly dropped their rates, and you only found out after losing three deals — that's a concrete cost. Pro's 6-hour snapshots and instant pricing alerts exist specifically to catch this.

The math: if Pro prevents even one deal loss per quarter for a product with $500+ ACV, it pays for itself for the full year.

### 4. You want Slack or webhook alerts

Free plan digests are email-only, weekly. Pro lets you connect Slack or any webhook, so the moment a change is detected, it hits your team's channel — not buried in a weekly email that's easy to miss.

This is especially useful for sales teams that need to know about a competitor pricing move *before* their next call, not after.

### 5. You need more than 7 days of change history

If you're running a quarterly competitive review, preparing for a board meeting, or trying to reconstruct a competitor's product roadmap over the past few months — the 7-day history window on Free won't get you there. Pro keeps 90 days of change history.

---

## Situations Where Free Is Enough

- You're genuinely tracking just 1–2 competitors and weekly digests meet your cadence
- You're in early-stage validation and not yet competing for deals
- You're evaluating before committing — Free is a real plan, not a time-limited demo

If any of the above applies, stay on Free. Upgrade when you need what Pro actually offers, not before.

## How to Upgrade

1. Go to [kompwatch.com/settings](https://kompwatch.com/settings)
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. Select the **Pro plan** in the Stripe Customer Portal
4. Your upgrade takes effect immediately — no wait, no support ticket needed

**First 20 subscribers:** If the [Founding Customer Program](./founding-pricing.md) is still open, you can lock in Pro at **$29/mo for life** instead of the standard $49/mo. Check the [pricing page](https://kompwatch.com/pricing) to see if spots remain.

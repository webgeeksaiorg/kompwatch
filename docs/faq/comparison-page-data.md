# How Current Is the Pricing Data on KompWatch Comparison Pages?

KompWatch publishes dedicated comparison pages (e.g. [vs Crayon](https://kompwatch.com/vs-crayon), [vs Klue](https://kompwatch.com/vs-klue)) that include competitor pricing figures. This article explains where those numbers come from and how often they're updated.

## Where the Pricing Data Comes From

Competitor pricing on comparison pages is sourced from:

- **Vendr / Buyer Benchmarks** — aggregated deal data from Vendr's annual SaaS pricing reports (the most recent is Vendr 2026)
- **Public pricing pages** — where competitors publish pricing openly
- **G2, Capterra, and community sources** — peer-reported ranges for tools that gate their pricing behind sales calls

This is research-backed data, not real-time scraping. It represents typical contract ranges, not guaranteed quotes.

## Is It Real-Time?

No. Competitor pricing on comparison pages is reviewed and updated manually — typically when a pricing change is publicly announced or when Vendr releases updated benchmark data.

This is different from what KompWatch *monitors* for your own tracked competitors: your monitored competitors are scraped on your plan's schedule (daily / every 6h / hourly) and changes are surfaced as alerts.

## How Accurate Is It?

Figures are as accurate as the sources allow:

- **Enterprise tools (Klue, Crayon, Kompyte)** often don't publish pricing publicly. Figures represent typical contract ranges from buyer benchmarks and are noted as approximate.
- **Self-serve tools (Caelian, Seeto)** publish pricing openly — those figures are more reliable.
- All prices are noted with the data vintage (e.g. "Vendr 2026 data") so you can assess freshness.

Comparison pages include a footnote: *"Competitor pricing is approximate and subject to change."*

## What If the Pricing Looks Wrong?

Pricing changes fast. If you spot a figure that looks out of date:

1. **Check the competitor's website directly** — their pricing page is the authoritative source.
2. **Email us** at [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) with the correct figure and a source link. We'll update the page promptly.

We appreciate corrections — keeping this data accurate helps everyone evaluate tools fairly.

## Does KompWatch Monitor Its Own Competitor Prices?

Yes — we monitor competitor pricing pages using KompWatch itself. When we detect a change on Crayon, Klue, or another competitor's pricing page, we review it and update comparison content accordingly. Think of it as eating our own dog food.

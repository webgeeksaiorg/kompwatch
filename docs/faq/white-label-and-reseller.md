# White-Labeling and Reselling KompWatch

Can you rebrand KompWatch for clients, embed it in your product, or resell it as part of an agency package? Here's what's currently available and what's on the roadmap.

## What "White-Label" Means in This Context

White-labeling would mean presenting KompWatch under your own brand — your logo, your domain, your email sender — so clients never see "KompWatch" directly. This is common for agencies that manage competitive intelligence for multiple clients.

## Current Status: White-Label Is Not Available

KompWatch does **not** offer a white-label or reseller tier at this time. All users interact with the KompWatch product directly, including branding in:
- The dashboard UI
- Digest email subjects and footers
- Notification sender addresses

## What Agencies Can Do Today

If you manage competitive monitoring for clients, here are practical workarounds:

**Option 1: Invite clients to their own accounts**
Each client signs up for their own KompWatch account (Free or Pro) and you help configure their competitor list and alert settings. You can be invited as a team member on their account (Team plan).

**Option 2: Team plan with sub-workspaces (coming soon)**
The Team plan supports multiple users. You can manage everything centrally and share digest access with your clients. Full client sub-workspace isolation is on the roadmap.

**Option 3: Export and repackage insights**
Use the [REST API](./rest-api-and-developer-access.md) or [Zapier/Make/n8n](./zapier-make-n8n-automation.md) to pull change data into your own reporting system (e.g., a Notion board, a Google Sheets dashboard, or a custom client portal).

## Reseller Program

There is no formal reseller or affiliate-revenue-share program at this time beyond the standard [referral program](./referral-and-affiliate-program.md), which pays a flat commission per converted referral.

If you're an agency managing 5+ client accounts, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) with the subject "Agency inquiry" — we can discuss volume pricing and early access to agency-tier features.

## Embedding KompWatch Data in Your Own Product

If you're a SaaS company wanting to embed competitor change data in your own UI, the [REST API](./rest-api-and-developer-access.md) and [MCP server](./mcp-server-and-ai-integrations.md) are the right paths. This is **not** the same as white-labeling — you're building on KompWatch's data infrastructure, but the UX is entirely yours.

Review the API docs for rate limits and acceptable use policies.

## Roadmap

White-label and reseller tiers are tracked as future features. If this matters to your use case, [email support](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) to be added to the waitlist — demand signals prioritize the roadmap.

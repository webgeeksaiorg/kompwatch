# Inviting Team Members

KompWatch supports multi-user access on the **Team plan** ($149/mo). Free and Pro plans are single-user accounts, though you can share digest emails with colleagues via a shared inbox alias.

## Who Can Invite Team Members?

Only the **account owner** (the person who signed up and holds the Stripe subscription) can invite additional members. Invitees get full access to all competitors, snapshots, and digests in the account — there is no read-only role yet.

## How Do I Invite Someone?

1. Go to **Settings → Team Members**
2. Click **Invite member**
3. Enter their work email address
4. Click **Send invite**

They'll receive a magic-link email from `hello@kompwatch.com`. The link is valid for 48 hours. Once they click it, they're added to your account and can log in via [kompwatch.com/login](https://kompwatch.com/login).

## How Many Members Can I Add?

The Team plan supports **up to 10 seats** at the $149/mo price. If you need more, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) — custom plans are available.

| Plan | Seats |
|------|-------|
| Free | 1 |
| Pro | 1 |
| Team | Up to 10 |

## Can I Remove a Team Member?

Yes — go to **Settings → Team Members**, click the **…** menu next to the member, and select **Remove**. Their access is revoked immediately. They will not receive a notification.

## I'm on a Free or Pro Plan — Can I Share Access?

Not directly. However, you can:
- **Share email digests** by signing up with a shared team alias (`competitive@yourcompany.com`)
- **Export or forward digests** manually from your inbox
- **Upgrade to Team** at [kompwatch.com/pricing](https://kompwatch.com/pricing) to unlock multi-seat access

## What If the Invite Email Didn't Arrive?

Ask your invitee to check their spam folder (look for `hello@kompwatch.com`). If it's not there after 5 minutes, go back to **Settings → Team Members**, find the pending invite, and click **Resend**.

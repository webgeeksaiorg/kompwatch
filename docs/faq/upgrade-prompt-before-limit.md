# Why Am I Seeing an Upgrade Prompt When I Still Have Free Slots?

If you're on the Free plan and you've added at least one competitor, you may notice a **"Upgrade to Pro"** card or banner in the dashboard — even though you haven't hit your two-competitor limit yet. This is intentional, not a bug.

---

## Why Does This Appear?

KompWatch shows a soft upgrade prompt once you've used your first free-tier competitor slot. The idea: if monitoring one competitor is already useful, monitoring 10 (with faster snapshots and daily digests) is likely worth it.

The nudge appears above the "Add Competitor" form on the [competitors page](https://kompwatch.com/competitors) when you have **1 of 2 slots used**.

---

## Is This a Bug?

No. You still have capacity to add a second competitor — the prompt is informational, not a gate. You can dismiss it or simply scroll past it to the Add form.

---

## How Do I Dismiss It?

Click the **×** or **"Not now"** link on the upgrade card. It won't reappear until your next session.

---

## What's the Difference Between This and the Hard Limit Message?

| State | What you see |
|---|---|
| 1 of 2 slots used | Soft upgrade prompt — you can still add a competitor |
| 2 of 2 slots used | Hard limit message — you must upgrade or remove a competitor first |

The hard limit message appears when you actually try to add a third competitor. See [I Can't Add More Competitors — Plan Limit Reached](./plan-competitor-limit-reached.md).

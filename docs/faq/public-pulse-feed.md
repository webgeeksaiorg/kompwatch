# Public Pulse Feed — SaaS Competitor Change Feed

## What Is the Pulse Page?

[KompWatch Pulse](/pulse) is a public, no-login-required feed of AI-detected changes across popular SaaS tools. It shows recent pricing updates, feature launches, blog posts, hiring signals, and tech changes — updated regularly.

It's a sample of what KompWatch tracks internally, surfaced publicly so you can see the product in action before signing up.

## Is It Real Data?

The Pulse page currently shows curated sample data representative of the kinds of changes KompWatch detects. It's designed to give you a realistic preview of the signal quality and change categories you'll see in your own account once you start monitoring your specific competitors.

## How Often Is It Updated?

The Pulse feed is updated regularly. For live, real-time detection against your own competitor list, sign up for a free account — KompWatch will monitor your chosen URLs and deliver AI-powered digests on your schedule.

## What Change Types Appear on Pulse?

| Tag | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **Pricing** | Price changes, tier restructuring, discount updates |
| **Feature** | New product capabilities, launches, updates |
| **Content** | New blog posts, case studies, comparison pages |
| **Hiring** | New job postings, team expansion signals |
| **Tech** | API changes, platform updates, infrastructure signals |

Each change is rated **Critical / High / Medium / Low** severity based on strategic impact.

# Understanding Your Competitor Digest

Your digest is a periodic email that summarizes every detected change across all your tracked competitors since the last digest was sent.

## What's in a Digest?

Each digest includes:

- **Competitor name and URL** — which competitor changed
- **Change type** — Content, Pricing, Feature, or Visual
- **Severity** — Low, Medium, or High (AI-assessed impact)
- **Summary** — a plain-English description of what changed, generated by AI
- **Detected at** — timestamp of when the change was captured

Changes are grouped by competitor and sorted with the most recent first.

## When Are Digests Sent?

Digests are sent based on your plan:

| Plan | Frequency | Condition |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Free | Weekly (every 7 days) | Only if new changes were detected |
| Pro | Daily (every 24 hours) | Only if new changes were detected |
| Team | Real-time (within 1 hour of a change) | Sent per-change as soon as detected |

> **Note:** If no changes were detected since your last digest, no email is sent. This is by design — we only email you when there's something worth reading.

## Why Didn't I Get a Digest This Week?

The most common reasons:

1. **No changes detected** — Your tracked competitors didn't change in the monitored period. Check your [dashboard](https://kompwatch.com/dashboard) under **Recent Changes** to confirm.
2. **Email landed in spam** — Search your spam folder for `digests@kompwatch.com` and mark it as "Not Spam."
3. **You're on the Free plan** — Free digests go out weekly (Mondays). If your last digest was less than 7 days ago, the next one is still pending.
4. **Wrong email on file** — Verify your address at [kompwatch.com/settings](https://kompwatch.com/settings).

For full troubleshooting steps, see [My Digest Email Didn't Arrive](./digest-not-arriving.md).

## Viewing Past Digests

Past digests are saved in your account. You can browse them in the [Digests section](https://kompwatch.com/digests) of your dashboard — no need to search your inbox.

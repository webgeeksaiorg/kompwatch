# I Signed Up via the Blog — What Happens Next?

KompWatch blog posts include an inline email form. Here's what signing up means and what to expect.

## What You Signed Up For

Submitting your email from a blog article adds you to the KompWatch early-access list. You'll receive:

1. **A sample digest** — a short email showing what a real KompWatch competitive intelligence digest looks like (pricing change detected, feature diff summary, AI-written summary). Arrives within a few minutes.
2. **Occasional product updates** — no more than 1–2 per month. These are things like major new features or important announcements.

You are **not** automatically enrolled in a paid plan. The blog signup is a free, no-commitment way to see the product before deciding whether to try it.

## Did It Work?

If the form showed a green confirmation message ("You're in — check your inbox for next steps"), your email was captured. Check your spam folder if the sample digest doesn't arrive within 10 minutes. Add `hello@kompwatch.com` to your contacts to ensure future emails land in your inbox.

## How to Unsubscribe

Every email we send includes an **Unsubscribe** link in the footer. Click it once and you're removed immediately — no confirmation step required.

Alternatively, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) with "unsubscribe" in the subject line and we'll remove you within 24 hours.

## Is My Email Shared?

No. Your email is stored in KompWatch's own database and used only for the emails described above. It is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties. See the [Privacy Policy](https://kompwatch.com/privacy) for full details.

## I Want to Try the Product, Not Just the Digest

The free plan includes 2 competitors, daily snapshots, and weekly digests — no credit card required. [Sign up here](https://kompwatch.com/login).

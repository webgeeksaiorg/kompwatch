# Can KompWatch Monitor Competitor Ads?

KompWatch monitors **competitor websites** — not ad platforms. But there's more overlap than you might think, and several high-value ad signals are within reach.

## What KompWatch Tracks vs. What It Doesn't

| Signal | KompWatch | Why |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| Competitor landing pages (ad destinations) | Yes | Any URL is fair game |
| Competitor homepage copy changes | Yes | Often reflects active campaign messaging |
| Competitor pricing page changes | Yes | Discount offers usually appear here first |
| Google Ads / Search campaigns | No | Requires access to Google's ad auction data |
| Facebook / LinkedIn / Twitter ad creatives | No | Behind platform authentication, not public web |
| Ad spend estimates | No | Third-party data, not on competitor websites |

## The Strongest Workaround: Monitor Ad Landing Pages

Most paid campaigns send traffic to a dedicated landing page — and those pages live on the competitor's own domain. KompWatch can monitor them directly.

**How to set it up:**

1. Find a competitor's active landing page. Look for:
   - Google Ads: search for your competitor's branded keywords and note the destination URL in the ad (hover over or click the ad on a test device/incognito)
   - Facebook Ads Library: `https://www.facebook.com/ads/library` (free, public) — shows active ads and their destination links
   - LinkedIn Ads: `linkedin.com/ad-library` (paid campaigns by company)
2. Copy the landing page URL (e.g. `https://competitor.com/lp/special-offer`)
3. Add it as a competitor in KompWatch — it counts as one URL against your competitor slot

Landing pages change more frequently than homepages because they're actively A/B tested. KompWatch will alert you every time headline copy, pricing, offer, or CTA changes.

## Free Ad Intelligence Tools (No KompWatch Required)

These public tools reveal what ads competitors are running right now:

| Tool | What it shows | Cost |
|------|--------------|------|
| [Facebook Ad Library](https://www.facebook.com/ads/library) | All active Facebook/Instagram ads — creatives, copy, targeting region | Free |
| [Google Ads Transparency Center](https://adstransparency.google.com) | Google Search and Display ads by advertiser | Free |
| [LinkedIn Ads Library](https://www.linkedin.com/ad-library/) | Active LinkedIn Sponsored Content ads | Free |
| SpyFu / SimilarWeb | Google keyword spend estimates, ad history | Paid |
| SEMrush Advertising Research | Ad copy history, estimated clicks/spend | Paid |

Pair these with KompWatch: use the ad library to find what campaigns competitors are running, then add those landing page URLs to KompWatch to get alerted when the offer or messaging changes.

## Detecting Ad-Driven Copy Changes

Even without direct ad monitoring, KompWatch gives you useful signals:

- **Homepage headline changes** often follow a new campaign launch — competitors update their homepage to match their current ad narrative
- **New promotional banners** (% off, free trial extensions, "Limited time") appear on pricing pages when a campaign goes live
- **New landing pages** sometimes show up in sitemaps — KompWatch picks up sitemap-linked pages on crawl
- **Blog content** aligned with a campaign topic (e.g. a surge in posts about "ROI" when a competitor is running ROI-themed ads)

# Onboarding Emails — What They Are and How to Manage Them

When you sign up for CompeteWatch, you'll receive a short sequence of emails over your first week. This article explains what each email is, why it's sent, and how to stop receiving them.

## What Emails Will I Receive?

| When | Subject | Purpose |
|------|---------|---------|
| Immediately on signup | "Welcome to CompeteWatch — let's set up your first monitor" | Welcome + quick-start guide |
| 2 days after signup | "3 ways CompeteWatch saves you hours every week" | Tips for getting the most out of monitoring |
| 5 days after signup (Free plan only) | "Your free plan is active — here's what Pro unlocks" | Overview of Pro and Team plan features |

**Note:** The day-5 email is only sent to users on the Free plan. If you've already upgraded to Pro or Team, you won't receive it.

## Why Am I Getting These Emails?

These are automated onboarding emails sent to all new CompeteWatch accounts. They're designed to help you set up monitoring quickly and understand what CompeteWatch can do.

They are not marketing newsletters — there's no ongoing subscription after the 5-day sequence ends.

## How Do I Stop Receiving Them?

If you'd prefer not to receive the remaining onboarding emails, email [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com) with your account email and we'll remove you from the sequence immediately.

> **Notification preferences** (including the ability to manage these emails in-app) are coming to the [Settings page](https://kompwatch.com/settings) soon.

## These Look Suspicious — Are They Legitimate?

Yes. CompeteWatch onboarding emails are sent from `hello@kompwatch.com`. If you're unsure, here's how to verify:

- All emails link only to `kompwatch.com` domains
- They include an "Manage preferences" link at the bottom pointing to [kompwatch.com/settings](https://kompwatch.com/settings)
- We never ask for passwords, payment details, or personal information over email

If you receive an email claiming to be from CompeteWatch that doesn't match the above, forward it to [support@kompwatch.com](mailto:support@kompwatch.com).

## Will I Get Digest Emails Too?

Yes, separately. Your [competitor change digest](./understanding-your-digest.md) is a different email sent on your plan's schedule (weekly for Free, daily for Pro). The onboarding sequence is a one-time thing — digests continue for as long as your account is active and monitoring competitors.

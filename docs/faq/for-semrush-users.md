# KompWatch for Semrush Users

If you use Semrush and have access to Kompyte through it, this page explains how KompWatch compares and whether switching makes sense for your team.

## I Already Have Kompyte Through Semrush — Why Would I Pay for KompWatch?

Kompyte is bundled into some Semrush plans, but that doesn't make it free. You're paying for Semrush, and Kompyte adds complexity to your workflow whether or not you actively use it. More importantly, Kompyte was acquired by Semrush in 2022 — and then Adobe acquired Semrush for $1.9B in February 2026.

That chain means your CI tool is now three acquisitions deep inside an enterprise creative-software company. Adobe's core business is not competitive intelligence. The realistic outcomes for Kompyte over the next two years: it gets bundled deeper into an enterprise Adobe suite (priced out of reach for most SaaS teams), quietly deprioritized, or deprecated when contracts come up for renewal.

KompWatch is a standalone, focused CI tool. It does one thing — monitors competitor websites and explains what changed — and is priced for SaaS teams, not enterprise suites.

## What Happens to My Kompyte Data?

KompWatch cannot import competitor lists or change history from Kompyte. Your monitoring history starts fresh when you add competitors in KompWatch.

Before switching, export any historical battlecard content or change summaries from Kompyte — especially if you've built internal reports from it.

## How Do I Switch?

1. **Sign up at [kompwatch.com](https://kompwatch.com)** — free, no credit card required on the Free plan.
2. **Add the same competitor URLs** you tracked in Kompyte. See [Adding a Competitor](./adding-competitors.md).
3. **Set CSS selectors** to target specific sections — pricing table, feature comparison, changelog. This step is optional but significantly improves signal quality. See [CSS Selector Targeting](./css-selectors.md).
4. **Connect Slack** in Settings → Integrations if you use a `#competitive` channel.

Your first snapshot runs immediately. Change detection starts after the second snapshot — typically within 24 hours on Free or a few hours on Pro.

## Can I Try KompWatch Without Canceling Semrush?

Yes. KompWatch's free tier (2 competitors) lets you run both tools in parallel for 30 days before committing. Add your two most important competitors to KompWatch and compare digest quality against what Kompyte surfaces. No credit card required for the Free plan.

## Do I Need to Cancel My Semrush Subscription?

No. Canceling Semrush is a separate decision from starting a KompWatch subscription. Most teams that switch use KompWatch for website change monitoring and keep Semrush for keyword research, backlink analysis, and SEO tooling — the tools don't overlap on core functionality.

## What If I'm Mid-Contract With Semrush?

You can start a KompWatch Free account and evaluate it immediately, even while under a Semrush contract. If you decide to upgrade, KompWatch is $49/mo (Pro) or $149/mo (Team) — month-to-month, cancel anytime.

If founding member pricing is still available, annual plans include a permanent rate lock. See [Founding Member Pricing](./founding-pricing.md).
